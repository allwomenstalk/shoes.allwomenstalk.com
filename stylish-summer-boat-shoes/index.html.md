# 7 Stylish Summer Boat Shoes ...

**Summer boat shoes are an** easy way to add a bit of preppy styling to your look. While boat shoes may have been seen as dowdy in the past, they’re having a bit of a revival at the moment. There’s quite a selection of stylish boat shoes to choose from. Your summer boat shoe can be a classic neutral number, or something more playful in pastel. Get styling with some of the following boat shoes for summer.

## 1. ASOS Loveboat Boat Shoes with Heart Detail

![ASOS Loveboat Boat Shoes with Heart Detail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/1_asos-loveboat-boat-shoes-with-heart-detail_500x209.jpg)

**Price:** $35.81 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/usd29X)
These brightly coloured **summer boat shoes are a** quirky take on the classic design. They have an easy slip on design with a heart shaped detailing at the front. They also have tassels at the front and laced sides. Wear these with a navy and white shift dress for a nautical inspired look.

## 2. Topshop Kapsize Moccasin Boat Shoes

![Topshop Kapsize Moccasin Boat Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/2_topshop-kapsize-moccasin-boat-shoes_500x241.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/3dTaoP)
These leather moccasin boat shoes have a classic lace up design. They come in a range of on trend seasonal colours from peach to pink, and powdery blue. For a dressed down weekend look, team a pair of these boat shoes with cropped jeans and a slouchy t-shirt.

## 3. ASOS Milo Boat Shoes with Laces

![ASOS Milo Boat Shoes with Laces](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/3_asos-milo-boat-shoes-with-laces_500x209.jpg)

**Price:** $35.81 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/r7Ws0A).
A cool green colour palette will be your go-to this summer. I love the combination of bright green and white on these shoes. These boat shoes have a twin eyelet lace-up closure, laced sides, and raised ‘pie crust’ seams. These **summer boat shoes would look great** worn with denim shorts and a loose white blouse.

## 4. Aldo Vidalez Flat Boat Shoes

![Aldo Vidalez Flat Boat Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/4_aldo-vidalez-flat-boat-shoes_500x226.jpg)

**Price:** $71.62 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/7sGGDB).
A pink shoe like this one is a great way for incorporating summer colour into your wardrobe. They have a classic boat shoe design with laced detailing and stitching on the vamp. For a really preppy look, team these shoes with a pair of tailored shorts and a button-up sleeveless blouse.

## 5. Topshop Trapeze Canvas Boat Shoes

![Topshop Trapeze Canvas Boat Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/5_topshop-trapeze-canvas-boat-shoes_500x294.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/qdf4mp)
Canvas is a popular choice of material when it comes to the summer boat shoe. These have a moccasin design, contrast soles, and laced fronts and sides. Boat shoes can have a tendency to be a bit chunky in their design, so balance it out by teaming these shoes with a floaty sundress.

## 6. Chloé Boat Shoe

_ **Price:** $595.00 at barneys.com_
If designer shoes are included in your budget, then these Chloé boat shoes are a classically neutral option. The all white main and sole are only accented by tan stitching. An all white shoe like this would be ideal for wearing with this season’s bold prints.

## 7. Sperry a/O 2-Eye Metallic Boat Shoes

![Sperry a/O 2-Eye Metallic Boat Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/7_sperry-a-o-2-eye-metallic-boat-shoes_500x196.jpg)

**Price:** $196.96 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/j0lgsy).
Metallics are still as popular as ever and even the humble **summer boat shoe has been vamped** up in this style. These leather boat shoes come in a bright metallic rose gold finish with gold-tone eyelets throughout. Wear them with black skinny jeans to really make them stand out.

Summer boat shoes are ideal for working a casual, dressed-down weekend look. Whether you incorporate them into a fully nautical inspired outfit or something more contemporary is completely up to you. What are your thoughts on the summer boat shoe?
