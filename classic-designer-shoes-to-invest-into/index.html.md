# 7 Classic Designer Shoes to Invest into ...

Designer Shoes can range from the classic to the crazy. While the price of the typical designer shoe makes them out of reach for many of us, there’s no denying the appeal of having a stylish designer number in our wardrobes, even if it does break the bank. If you are interested in buying a pair of designer shoes, look at classic styles that won’t date. A classic pair of designer shoes can take you through a range of occasions. Take a look at some classic designer shoes below.

## 1. Christian Louboutin Pigalle Shoes

![Christian Louboutin Pigalle Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ar/ly/sqctsvxm54c9373fe1c06763714201_264x268.jpg)

**Price:** $486.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A pair of black pumps is a must have in any capsule wardrobe. They’re a timeless style and will work with everything from office to evening wear. These patent-leather court shoes have a mid-height heel, pointed toe, and feature the signature Christian Louboutin red sole.

## 2. Yves Saint Laurent Tribute Leather Sandals

_ **Price:** $795.00 at barneys.com_
The YSL Tribute sandals are a contemporary classic. These designer shoes have enjoyed much popularity in recent years, being a firm favourite with celebrities the world over. These sandals have a platform heel, peep-toe, and t-strap design. The blush leather is ideal for making legs look longer, while the black patent is a classic choice.

## 3. Repetto Patent Leather Ballet Flats

_ **Price:** $255.00 at mytheresa.com_
Ballet flats are another must-have style of shoe. They’re perfect for wearing during the day and to give feet a break from high heeled shoes. These Repetto patent leather flats are bit dressier thank your average black ballet flats, but would still work well with a range of outfits.

## 4. Church’s Classic Burwood Leather Brogues

![Church’s Classic Burwood Leather Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v8/qz/tzfum3fg54c9374248c25292393870_300x148.jpg)

**Price:** $440.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wvotbo)
Brogue shoes have enjoyed renewed popularity lately, and these designer shoes are perfect for creating a masculine edge to an outfit. They are made from glossy black leather and feature a traditional punched wingtip design. Wear them with a full high-waisted skirt and blouse for a prep-inspired look.

## 5. Manolo Blahnik Frida Shoes

_ **Price:** $595.00 at barneys.com_
Another classic black shoe to have in your wardrobe, these Manolo Blahniks come in a stylish suede finish. These shoes have a tapered toe, 90mm heel, and a classic pump design. These shoes are perfect paired with a cigarette-style trouser and button-up blouse.

## 6. Christian Louboutin Lady Peep

_ **Price:** $945.00 at barneys.com_
Peep-toe shoes are another classic design to invest in, as are nude coloured shoes. Kill two birds with one stone when investing in a pair of these designer shoes. These Christian Louboutin shoes scream sex appeal and feature a platform pump ad high stiletto heel.

## 7. Jimmy Choo Youth Leather Biker Boots

![Jimmy Choo Youth Leather Biker Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vs/lh/k8wn3ra954c937449e73d246158402_300x278.jpg)

**Price:** $895.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Qx17t4)
These biker boots are a modern classic. The style is perfect for creating a cool, off-duty look. These biker boots are made from black leather and come in a flattering ankle-length height. They also have two buckle-fastening tabs and a designer-stamped plaque at the back. These boots look fantastic with anything from skinny jeans to floral dresses.

When looking to invest in classic designer shoes, go for a pair in a timeless design and one that you think will work with a majority of your wardrobe. Also, stick to neutral colours such as nudes and blacks as these are more wearable. What do you think of these classic designer shoes?
