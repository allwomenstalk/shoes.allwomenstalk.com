# 9 Stylish Fashion Slippers ...

Fashion slippers are having a huge trending moment right now. Don’t limit yourself to wearing them around the house as many of the designs available are just screaming to be shown off. From plush suede to death-defying spiked designs, there’s a fashion slipper for all tastes. Check out a few fashionable slippers, below.

## 1. Gold Rush Hour Flats

![Gold Rush Hour Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/1_gold-rush-hour-flats_320x152.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Get your dose of glittery goodness with these fashion slippers. They have a classic slipper silhouette and are clad in gold glitter. Let the shoes do all the talking by teaming them with a basic outfit of neutral shirt and denim jeans.

## 2. Vectra4 Canvas Studded Slippers

![Vectra4 Canvas Studded Slippers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/2_vectra4-canvas-studded-slippers_600x261.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ysfzbq)
These pretty pink slippers are given some edgy attitude with the addition of silver stud embellishments. The studs also counter the saccharine sweet pink colour of the slippers. Wear these with a white shirt dress for a cool daytime look.

## 3. Bronz Pony Skin Slipper

![Bronz Pony Skin Slipper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/3_bronz-pony-skin-slipper_600x270.jpg)

**Price:** $74.57 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/XDgbpG)
Whether you love it or loathe it, leopard print is a mainstay on the fashion scene. These fashion slippers have a pony skin, leather upper and low block heel. Wear these with a white silk blouse and cropped black trousers for a timeless and elegant outfit.

## 4. Moon Grey Laser-Cut Slippers

![Moon Grey Laser-Cut Slippers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/4_moon-grey-laser-cut-slippers_600x267.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/cmnv5j)
These light grey leather slippers have a stylishly minimal design. The stand out design feature has to be the all over circular laser-cut pattern. Create a casually polished outfit by teaming these slippers with a navy dress and satchel bag.

## 5. It Takes Two to Tangelo Flat

![It Takes Two to Tangelo Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/5_it-takes-two-to-tangelo-flat_322x196.jpg)

**Price:** $78.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Liven up your wardrobe with these bright orange fashion slippers. They have a plush suede finish and feature a large bow detail to the toe. This shade of orange would look great against crisp whites and indigo denims.

## 6. Valencia Black Mesh Slippers

![Valencia Black Mesh Slippers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/6_valencia-black-mesh-slippers_600x220.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/19g39M)
Shoes aren’t normally the place to find mesh fabrics, which makes these slippers quite unique. They have a black mesh body with woven upper pattern that creates a ‘squiggly’ effect. These are a daintier take on the fashion slipper and would look great worn with more feminine styles of clothing.

## 7. Alexander McQueen Skull Suede Slippers

![Alexander McQueen Skull Suede Slippers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/7_alexander-mcqueen-skull-suede-slippers_460x224.jpg)

**Price:** $645.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/FniK1g)
These high end designer slippers are quite luxurious. They’re made from black suede and feature sequin skull embellishments. Team them with skinny jeans and a blazer for an understated yet chic look.

## 8. Chain of Event Planning Flat

![Chain of Event Planning Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/8_chain-of-event-planning-flat_320x176.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These black velvety slippers have a traditional design. They also feature gold chain details which add a little bit of extra glamour. Team fashion slippers would look great teamed with fitted trousers and a button-down shirt.

## 9. Sam Edelman Stud Slippers

![Sam Edelman Stud Slippers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/9_sam-edelman-stud-slippers_600x239.jpg)

**Price:** $232.00 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/K6SuwQ)
Don’t let the spike detailing scare you off these slippers. The plain black slipper is given a deathly twist thanks to the addition of crystal stud embellishments. Wear these with a leather shift dress for a cool and contemporary look.

Fashion slippers look great worn with anything from cropped pants to full skirts. They’re also a fun way of working a menswear inspired look. Will you be donning the fashion slipper this season? Which of these styles of slipper do you like best?
