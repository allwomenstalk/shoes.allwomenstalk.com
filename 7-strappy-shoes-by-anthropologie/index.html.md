# 7 Strappy Shoes by Anthropologie ...

Strappy shoes are very on-trend right now, and it’s no wonder why they’re sexy, sweet, or sizzling, sometimes a combination of all three. If you’re on the hunt for a new pair of strappy shoes, keep reading, for I’ve compiled a list of some of the hottest from Anthropologie… and here they are!

## 1. Plaited Cayenne Sandals

![Plaited Cayenne Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/gn/s7zwrquw54c8f3504efd0110766199_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $188.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These are a strappy, modern, spicy take on the gladiator sandal, with skinny straps galore in vivid cayenne red suede with a back zip and slight wedge heel. Pair with cuffed linen shorts or skinny jeans and a tunic.

## 2. Via Cardorna Wedges

![Via Cardorna Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mm/o6/v6zlo86w54c8f35146575348046330_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $198.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Wedges in sweet soft sage, statuesque and feminine, with a back zip and nearly four-inch suede-covered heel. What more could a girl ask for? Perhaps a floral-print summer sundress to wear with them, and a cropped blazer?

## 3. Tancarville Heels

![Tancarville Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/si/uoy4r87n54c8f3523c03b715302714_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $298.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These strappy heels are the best of strappy sandals, sensible heels, and sassy ankle booties all in one, with an almond-shaped toe, back zip, wooden stacked heel, and sexy strappy upper. Pair with a long skirt or skinny jeans and a sequined tunic.

## 4. Bands & Buckles Flats

![Bands & Buckles Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jj/uw/bvqqyofs54c8f35319ffe442490147_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $128.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These adorable flats by Lucky Penny are just the thing to add sass to a weekday chinos ensemble or to wear with a spring-time sundress. They’re made of soft leather and feature three criss-crossing straps with adjustable buckles for a perfect fit.

## 5. Wet Cement Sling-backs

![Wet Cement Sling-backs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/io/wra9sdgu54c8f353e8483008836791_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $178.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
One look at these pretty sling-backs and you’ll see how they got their apt name, for they are indeed the soft color of fresh cement, a cool neutral grey. I love the swish and swoop of the straps, and the sensible low wood-look heel. These would look marvelous with high-waisted trousers and a lavender silk blouse.

## 6. If Looks Could Booties

![If Looks Could Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l1/ag/obl1m9q554c8f354d314f751515348_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $298.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Oh, there are so many things I love about these pebble texture kitten heels. The sassy snap closure at the ankle, the lace-up detail on the upper, the shiny gold grommets - all of these work together to make a pair of peep-toes I just can’t resist!

## 7. Strappy Stepper Shoes

![Strappy Stepper Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/ni/bpubovk154c8f355c125a783939197_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $138.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Gorgeous! This strappy little pair of sandals by Miss Albright feature a stacked heel, straps at the ankle that tie with a tasseled bow, and a brass rivet detail at the toe. Pair with a vintage floral-print shirt-dress or a pair of linen shorts with a cuff at the hem. What a way to welcome spring!

With so many strappy shoes, and so many ways to wear them, it’s no wonder they’re so popular and sought-after this season! I adore those black kitten-heels, and the flats with the criss-cross straps are gorgeous! Which of these strappy little shoes do you like best, and how would you wear them?
