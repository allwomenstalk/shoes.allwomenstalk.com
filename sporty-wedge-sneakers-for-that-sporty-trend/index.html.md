# 7 Sporty Wedge Sneakers for That Sporty Trend ...

Sporty wedge sneakers are all the rage right now. The Isabel Marant wedge sneaker quickly become a cult favourite and was seen on the feet of many a fashion tastemaker. The wedge sneaker is a look that may divide the masses, but it’s one that’s becoming increasingly on-trend. A range of high street and chain fashion brands now have their own take on the sporty wedge sneaker. Take a look at a few stylish sporty wedge sneakers, below, and let us know what you think of this trend.

## 1. Topshop ‘Acrobat’ Padded Velcro Trainers

![Topshop ‘Acrobat’ Padded Velcro Trainers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/1_topshop-acrobat-padded-velcro-trainers_600x589.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/iw1gcP)
The neutral colour combination of these trainers would make them perfect for teaming with light blue denim. They have a visible wedge heel, velcro straps, and contrasting suede-look panels. These would look great worn with denim shorts, loose white shirt, and bucket-style bag.

## 2. Topshop ‘Aerobic’ Sporty Wedge Sneakers

![Topshop ‘Aerobic’ Sporty Wedge Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/2_topshop-aerobic-sporty-wedge-sneakers_600x467.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ab3rMZ)
Many of the most popular style of wedge sneakers have concealed wedges, like this pair from Topshop. The concealed wedge heel gives them the effect of being a hi-top sneaker. They come in a cool cream colour and feature perforated detailing throughout.

## 3. Ash ‘Bowie’ Wedge Trainers

![Ash ‘Bowie’ Wedge Trainers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/3_ash-bowie-wedge-trainers_600x553.jpg)

These sporty wedge sneakers come in a lush navy colour and have a suede finish. They have a panelled design with a concealed wedge heel, padded ankle cuff, and lace-up closure. Team these sneakers with a sleeved black shift dress and large handbag for an effortless daytime look.

## 4. ASOS ‘Deny’ Sporty Wedge Sneakers

![ASOS ‘Deny’ Sporty Wedge Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/4_asos-deny-sporty-wedge-sneakers_600x480.jpg)

**Price:** $82.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/0CKElD)
These tan sneakers feature contrasting laces and soles, suede-look panels, and concealed wedge heels. They’re perfect for working a casually sporty look. These sneakers would look great teamed with a fitted mini dress and cropped jacket.

## 5. Topshop ‘Athlete’ Wedge Heel Trainers

![Topshop ‘Athlete’ Wedge Heel Trainers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/5_topshop-athlete-wedge-heel-trainers_600x488.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/bYyH5T)
These tan sneakers make quite the statement. They have a visible wedge heel, contrasting laces, velcro strap, and zip fastening. Work a contemporary off-duty look by teaming these sporty wedge sneakers with a basic t-shirt and a leather A-line mini skirt.

## 6. Ash Colourblock Strapped Wedge Trainers

![Ash Colourblock Strapped Wedge Trainers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/6_ash-colourblock-strapped-wedge-trainers_600x570.jpg)

A sweet pink sneaker is just one way to satisfy a feminine streak. These rose, white, and red sneakers have a stitched panel design and feature perforated detailing. Work a feminine angle by teaming these wedge trainers with a pleated white maxi skirt.

## 7. Modcloth Sporty Wedge Sneakers

![Modcloth Sporty Wedge Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/7_modcloth-sporty-wedge-sneakers_400x360.jpg)

**Price:** $87.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Sport-chic is a big trend right now, and the sporty wedge sneaker is certainly having a fashion moment. The wedge sneaker looks great styled with anything from skirts to jeans to leather pants for a cool off-duty look. What do you think of these sporty wedge trainers?
