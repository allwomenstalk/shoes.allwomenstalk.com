20 Funky Neon Shoes Youve Got to Have ...

By Sophia

Neon shoes are having a trending moment right now. Adding a splash of neon to your outfit is a fun way to update your look, and doing so with a neon shoe is a smart and stylish choice. From big name designers to chain store brands, everyone seems to be on the neon shoe bandwagon this summer. Why not take some outfit inspiration from the following stylish neon shoes?

1. McQ Alexander McQueen Neon Shoes

Price: $287.00 at net-a-porter.com
These designer shoes are definitely a funky option. They come in a neon orange colour and feature silver studs at the heel. Work a cool and casual daytime look by teaming these shoes with jeans, a basic tee, and a biker style jacket.

2. Hunt Cross-over Fluro Sandals

Price: $50.00 at us.topshop.com
These nude sandals feature a striking neon yellow strap at the toe. They have a crossover design with slim adjustable ankle straps. These basic sandals would look great worn with a range of summer outfits from denim shorts to printed dresses.

3. Giuseppe Zanotti Lace Neon Shoes

Price: $330.00 at net-a-porter.com
Coloured lace is everywhere this season, and it’s even managed to find itself onto these stunning neon shoes. The neon lace is toned down by the basic ballet flat design. Style these shoes with a navy shirt dress for a classy daytime look.

4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Mouse Neon Rubber Flats

Price: $135.00 at net-a-porter.com
The mouse design on these shoes definitely makes them quirkier than your average shoe. They’re made from neon pink rubber and would make a fun addition to an outfit. The rubber material makes them perfect for wearing during a summer sun shower!

5. Day and Nightlife Flat Neon Shoes

Price: $29.99 at modcloth.com
Really add some punchy colour to your look with these bright yellow neon shoes. They have a classic silhouette with slightly pointed toe. Since these shoes are so bright, keep the rest of your look simple with denim jeans and a plain blouse.

6. Saltwater Sandals Lime Sandal

Price: $39.99 at modcloth.com
These bright sandals from Saltwater Sandals come in their signature design. The lime green colour, however, is a fresh twist on the design. Team these sandals with a printed dress for a funky daytime look.

7. Jil Sander Leather Ballet Flats

Price: $212.50 at net-a-porter.com
Neon ballet flats are a stylish way to wear the neon shoe trend. The in-your-face neon colour is balanced out by the classic and timeless design. Work a trend-based look by teaming these particular shoes with printed pants and a basic singlet.

8. Christian Louboutin Patent Leather Brogues

Price: $695.00 at net-a-porter.com
These neon brogues will definitely get you noticed. They are made from a neon yellow patent leather and feature a wooden sole. Keep the rest of your outfit fairly minimal and try wearing these brogues with a white dress for a slightly more feminine look.

9. Cutout for Love Flat Neon Shoes

Price: $41.99 at modcloth.com
These funky neon pink shoes feature heart shaped cutouts. They also have a lace up design with a contrasting white sole. These shoes would look great worn with light denim clothes and basic accessories.

10. LD Tuttle Two Tone Leather Pumps

Price: $283.50 at net-a-porter.com
These fashion-forward shoes feature the slightest hint of neon in the form of patent pink neon ankle straps. Aside from that, they have a slight heel and pointed toe. Wear these with an LBD and embellished clutch for a cool night time look.

11. Stare2 Platform Neon Shoes

Price: $100.00 at us.topshop.com
Stand out (and above) from the crowd with these neon platform shoes. They have a luxe suede finish and come in a shade of highlighter yellow. Try styling these shoes with a black and white geometric print dress.

12. Belle by Sigerson Morrison Sandals

Price: $194.00 at matchesfashion.com
Let your feet breathe with these colourful neon sandals. They have a patent leather finish and have a T-strap design. Work a colour clashed look by teaming these sandals with colourful pants, maybe even in an interesting print.

13. Keds Sneakers

Price: $39.99 at modcloth.com
For a sportier, more casual take on the neon shoe trend you can’t go past these sneakers. They come in a bright orange colour and have a short lace up design. Pair them with some printed runner style shorts and a denim shirt for a fun weekend look.

14. Pink Knot Front Pumps

Price: $29.00 at us.dorothyperkins.com
These pink flat shoes would be an easy way to transition some neon into your wardrobe. They feature an interesting knotted detail at the front and have a funky printed lining. Show these shoes off in cropped pants.

15. Christian Louboutin Pigalle Pumps

Price: $625.00 at net-a-porter.com
Even iconic designer Christian Louboutin is getting in on the neon shoes trend. These patent leather pumps come in a fluorescent pink colour and would look great teamed with a full-skirted dress.

16. Walter Steiger Neon Sandal

Price: $479.00 at barneys.com
Thin strappy sandals are enjoying some popularity right now. Make a statement in this neon green pair. They’d be perfect for livening up a cocktail dress.

17. Neon Oxfords

These neon orange oxfords are a fun take on a classic footwear style. They have a slightly pointed toe and feature contrast trims and laces. Team these with an A-line denim skirt and basic t-shirt for a casual look.

18. Crossed Strap Sandals

Price: $16.80 at forever21.com
These strappy sandals come in a blindingly bright neon pink colour. They’d be perfect for teaming with white or denim clothing this summer. The best bit about these neon shoes? The price tag!

19. Glow Patent Bow Court Shoes

20. Whistle Espadrille Wedges

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes neon shoes so popular right now?
Neon shoes add a fun, bold pop of color that instantly elevates any outfit, making them a trendy choice for fashion-forward women.
Are neon shoes comfortable for everyday wear?
Many neon shoes are designed with comfort in mind, featuring cushioned soles and breathable materials suitable for daily use.
How can I style neon shoes without overdoing it?
Pair neon shoes with neutral or monochrome outfits to let the shoes stand out without overwhelming your look.
Do neon shoes work for both casual and dressy occasions?
Yes, you can find neon shoes in various styles from sneakers to heels that fit casual outings and more formal events.
Are neon-colored shoes easy to clean?
Most neon shoes are made with durable materials that are relatively easy to wipe clean with a damp cloth.
Can neon shoes match with patterned clothing?
Neon shoes often complement simple patterns well, but it's best to avoid clashing bold prints for a balanced look.
What types of neon shoes are trending now?
Chunky sneakers, strappy heels, and slip-on flats in neon hues are among the hottest styles this season.
Are neon shoes suitable for all seasons?
While they’re perfect for spring and summer, neon shoes can brighten up fall and winter outfits when paired thoughtfully.
Where can I find affordable neon shoes?
Many retailers offer stylish neon shoes at budget-friendly prices, especially during seasonal sales and online stores.
How do I choose the right neon shoe color for my skin tone?
Cool-toned neon shades like electric blue suit cooler skin tones, while warm neon colors like orange and yellow complement warmer tones.
