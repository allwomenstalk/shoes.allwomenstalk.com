# 8 Tips for Choosing  the Best Summer Sandals for You ...

**Summer sandals** are practically synonymous with warm weather, sandy beaches, and that gloriously freeing feeling when you finally ditch the socks. Isn't it just the best? As we dive into the sunny days of 2024, it's time to talk about an often overlooked yet essential part of your summer wardrobe: the perfect _summer footwear_.

Finding the best pair of **summer sandals** can sometimes feel like a _labyrinth_. Believe me, I’ve been there, done that, got the blister! It’s 2024, and the market for _footwear_ has evolved tremendously, with new materials, styles, and technologies to make our lives a tad bit more comfortable and fashionable. So whether you're planning to stroll along the beach, attend backyard barbecues, or just relax by the poolside, choosing the right sandals is crucial.

Personally, my go-to approach when hunting for that dream pair of sandals is to think of it as an investment. Much like picking out your favorite _pair of jeans_, the goal is to find something that balances both comfort and style. And why not? Summer's supposed to be easy, breezy, and blister-free {comfort matters}.

One fabulous way to ensure you nail this choice is comfort. In all honesty, those killer heels might look stunning in the shop display, but can they really keep up with your summer plans? After all, think about how many miles you'll be trekking on a weekend adventure or how long you’ll be standing around during a music festival {casual vibes}. Trust me; you'll thank yourself for prioritizing foot-friendly pairs.

Another important factor is style. We've all seen that unfortunate person hobbling around in overly stiff or dreadfully outdated _footwear_. Not gonna lie, it's painful just to watch. Trendy doesn’t have to be a trade-off with comfort. When it comes to _summer sandals_, some top brands have outdone themselves in combining style with ease. Contemporary designs and materials mean you don’t have to sacrifice looking fabulous to feel comfortable {style pointers}.

Now, let's not forget about the all-important factor — durability. This isn’t just about how a _pair of sandals_ look after the first beach party. This is about going the distance — no pun intended. A high-quality pair will withstand environmental elements like sand, water, and heat, ensuring you get the most out of them throughout the season. After all, who wants to spend precious summer hours shopping for replacements {durability counts}?

So, fellow sandal searchers, buckle up (pun totally intended) because this article will arm you with the ultimate guide to find that perfect pair. Ready to dive into the list of those prized tips? Let’s get started!

## 1. Pick Your Style

There are so many styles of sandals. You can go for gladiators, [thongs](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/reasons-to-wear-a-thong-everyday/), platforms, slides and wedges, to name a few. So what are you looking for? Thongs and slides are great for something casual and classic, but you might want a mule or platform if you are looking for something a little dressier.

## 2. What Are Your Comfort Needs?

Are these going out sandals or something you will wear on vacation seeing the sights? We know there is a breaking-in process with any new shoe, but some sandals are more comfortable than others. Take your needs into consideration when picking your perfect summer sandal. If you are doing a lot of walking, choose something for comfort over looks.

## 3. Can You Find a Sale?

Where do you like to buy sandals? Are they having a summer sale? Maybe you can find a good deal and get half off your second pair. What’s better than the perfect pair of sandals? Two pairs of perfect summer sandals.

## 4. Quality Materials

Make sure your new sandals are made of good quality material and with good workmanship. The worst thing is to be out and have the straps of your sandals fall apart. Knowing you are wearing a quality product will give you a little more peace of mind.

## 5. Good Fit

I always laugh when I see a girl trying to squeeze her feet into a super cute pair of sandals that just don’t fit. Don’t be afraid to size up if your heel is falling off the back of the sole. If your toes are crammed in the straps, look for a wider fit. Your sandal will do you better justice if it actually fits your foot correctly. If you aren’t sure, ask for assistance.

## 6. Prepare for Blisters

Looks like you have found your sole mate! Now you must prepare for the blisters. Wear your new sandals at home for a night with a thin pair of socks. It will give you an idea of where these new duds are going to rub your feet. Purchase silicone pads or moleskin to place on your sandal strap where it is likely to rub your skin. Your feet will thank you.

## 7. Color

I love a bright colored sandal. I see super cute coral and teal sandals at the store all the time. Unfortunately, these aren’t colors that look good on me, so I don’t have any outfits in these colors. Think about some of the key colors in your summer wardrobe. Choose a sandal color that will complement a couple of different outfits so you have something to wear with them.

## 8. Get a Pedicure

I mean it! Before you even start sandal shopping, get a good pedicure. Soften up your feet, paint them toes, and freshen up all over. Your feet will love you for it and you will feel better as you try on different sandals.

I hope these tips set you on the right track to finding your perfect summer sandal. Enjoy the warm sandal wearing weather while it lasts and show off those fresh pedicured toes. If this summer’s gonna hurt, then we might as well look cute!
