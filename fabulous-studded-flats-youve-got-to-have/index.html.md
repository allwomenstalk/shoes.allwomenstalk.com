# 7 Fabulous Studded Flats Youve Got to Have ...

**Studded flats shoes** are an easy way to add some edginess to an outfit. A pair of studded flat shoes is a stylish and on-trend choice right now. Studded detailing can be quite bold so if you’re after something more demure, stick to subtle stud details and simple flat styles. Take a look at the following examples of some stylish flat studded shoes.

## 1. ASOS Studded Ballet Shoes

![ASOS Studded Ballet Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/1_asos-studded-ballet-shoes_400x166.jpg)

**Price:** $44.76 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/r0Hl64)
These chic black ballet flats feature subtle pin stud detailing at the toes. These studded flats shoes have a microsuede finish and have a smart pointed toe. They would make a versatile everyday shoe.

## 2. Modcloth Studded Flats Shoes

![Modcloth Studded Flats Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/2_modcloth-studded-flats-shoes_336x210.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Brogue style shoes are perfect for adding a masculine twist to your outfits. These lace up shoes have a studded panel at the toe and a low wood-effect heel. Play these shoes up with a feminine and flirty dress or keep it androgynous in cigarette style pants and a button up shirt.

## 3. Topshop Fierce Stud Gladiator Sandals

![Topshop Fierce Stud Gladiator Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/3_topshop-fierce-stud-gladiator-sandals_400x246.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/FFN7wB)
Show off your new pedicure in these stylish strappy **studded flat shoes**. These sandals come in a gladiator style and feature stud detailing on the straps. The cream colour of these sandals is a great alternative to darker tan leathers. These would look great worn with a pleated maxi skirt.

## 4. Sam Edelman Studded Ballet Pumps

![Sam Edelman Studded Ballet Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/4_sam-edelman-studded-ballet-pumps_400x180.jpg)

**Price:** $152.19 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/7YUxoO)
From afar these two-tone ballet flats look like a feminine classic. The cream leather main and bow detail are very much in the style of Chanel. But on closer inspection you’ll be taken by the almost dangerous looking stud detailing at the toes. Wear these shoes with a silk blouse, full skirt, and framed handbag.

## 5. Giuseppe Zanotti Studded Flat Shoes

![Giuseppe Zanotti Studded Flat Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/5_giuseppe-zanotti-studded-flat-shoes_400x166.jpg)

**Price:** $650.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/MLma9G)
All over studding can still look super smart. While there’s the potential to go into grungy territory, these flats show off all-over studding at its best. Surprisingly, they’re made from denim. Slip these on with an outfit of tailored shorts and a tee for a relaxed weekend look.

## 6. DV by Dolce Vita Studded Slipper Shoes

![DV by Dolce Vita Studded Slipper Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/6_dv-by-dolce-vita-studded-slipper-shoes_400x167.jpg)

**Price:** $53.72 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/eW6OJi).
Slipper style shoes are still enjoying their time in the limelight. These suede shoes feature square stud detailing at the toe and ankle. The deep pink colour of these shoes is a nice change from more neutral styles. These would look great worn with dark denim jeans or black cropped trousers.

## 7. McQ Alexander McQueen Studded Neon Flats

![McQ Alexander McQueen Studded Neon Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/7_mcq-alexander-mcqueen-studded-neon-flats_400x190.jpg)

**Price:** $410.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Uy77bJ)
For something a bit more zany and out there, take a look at these studded flat shoes. They come in a cool neon orange colour and have a pointed toe and cutout back. The heels feature silver stud detailing. Team these with a fairly basic outfit as they really stand out.

A pair of **studded flats shoes can look quite** smart and chic. Demure ballet pumps with stud detailing can look quite feminine and timeless. On the other hand, studded shoes in bold colours and unique styles can be quite statement making. What do you think of these studded flat shoes?
