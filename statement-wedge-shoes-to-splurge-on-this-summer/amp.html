30 Statement Wedge Shoes to Splurge on This Summer ...

Miu Miu Wedge Sandals • Honey Wedge Shoes • CJG Slingback Mega Wedges • Statement of Purpose Wedge Shoe • Aldo Suede Wedge Shoes • More ...

By Sophia

Wedge shoes will be your best friend when it comes to summer dressing. Unlike stiletto heels, a wedge shoe is much more suited for outdoor events on grassy and uneven surfaces. Perfect for that garden party or picnic event! A few of these shoes might be a bit more than what you’re used to spending, but take inspiration from the following statement wedge shoes this summer.

1. Miu Miu Wedge Sandals

Price: $414.00 at net-a-porter.com
These shoes are definitely a splurge, but they’re bound to make a statement. They come in a summery coral colour and feature on-trend mirrored trims at the heels. These fashion forward wedges would look great balanced out by a neutral shift dress.

2. Honey Wedge Shoes

Price: $104.37 at asos.com
Florals are a popular choice when it comes to summer fashion, and these wedge shoes certainly make the most of the trend. They have a high platform wedge and feature wrap-around fabric ankle straps. Team these wedges with a crisp white dress for a fun garden-party outfit.

3. CJG Slingback Mega Wedges

Price: $260.00 at us.topshop.com
These wedges have a ridiculously high platform heel. If you can look past the height of the platform for a minute, you’ll see that they come in a chic mint colour and have a sleek slingback design. If you’re brave enough, these shoes would look stunning paired with colourful tailored shorts and a relaxed blouse.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are statement wedge shoes?
Statement wedge shoes are bold, stylish footwear with a raised heel that adds height and flair to any outfit.
Why are wedge shoes popular in summer?
Wedges offer comfort and stability while keeping your feet cool and stylish during warmer months.
How do I style statement wedge shoes for casual looks?
Pair them with sundresses, shorts, or cropped jeans for an effortless summer vibe.
Are wedge shoes comfortable to wear all day?
Yes, wedges typically provide better support than stilettos, making them great for extended wear.
Can I wear wedges for formal occasions?
Absolutely! Choose a sleek design or neutral color to complement dressier outfits.
What materials are common in summer wedge shoes?
Canvas, cork, espadrille, and leather are popular materials that keep your feet breathable and stylish.
How do I care for my wedge shoes?
Keep them clean by wiping down with a damp cloth and store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
Are wedge shoes suitable for wide feet?
Many brands offer wide-fit options, and wedges generally provide more room than pointy heels.
Can wedges help with balance compared to other heels?
Yes, the wider base of wedges offers more stability and balance while walking.
Where can I find trendy wedge shoes to buy this summer?
Check out fashion retailers online and in-store for the latest collections and seasonal sales.
