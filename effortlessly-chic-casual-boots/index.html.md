# 8 Effortlessly Chic Casual Boots ...

No time to scour the shelves for that perfect pair of sleek, non-clunky casual boots? Your toes are in luck, ‘cause I’ve done the legwork and found eight of the coziest casual boots on the market. With one of these pairs on your feet, you’ll scurry through the day in style and comfort. And since they’re super versatile, you can pair them with anything from skinny jeans to flared skirts and still look fabulous. Now that’s a win-win for every boot-loving gal out there!

## 1. DV by Dolce Vita Marko

![DV by Dolce Vita Marko](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/1_dv-by-dolce-vita-marko_192x312.jpg)

This prime pair of casual boots will keep your feet and legs warm. Plus, the ankle harnesses add an edgy touch to the otherwise simple style. Wear them to work on casual Friday with dark wash skinny jeans and your go-to faux leather blazer. Buy these now on Zappos for $69.99.

## 2. Tassel Zip Canvas Booties

![Tassel Zip Canvas Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/2_tassel-zip-canvas-booties_328x282.jpg)

While Forever21 shoes won’t last you for more than a season or two, these lace-up canvas booties are a great way to look trendy without depleting your bank account. They’d look ultra cool with a mini skirt, vintage tee, and your beau’s fuzzy wool cardigan. Or, pair them with skintight skinnies and a floppy hat for a city-slicker look your besties will fawn over. This pair is available now on Forever21 for $32.80.

## 3. New Look Brixton Studded Toe Cap Ankle Boots

![New Look Brixton Studded Toe Cap Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/3_new-look-brixton-studded-toe-cap-ankle-boots_248x188.jpg)

Simple black ankle booties score a badass upgrade, courtesy of silver-tone studs. Rock them during your next house party with opaque black tights, a sequined mini dress, and sultry red lips. Who said you had to wear painful heels to be the center of attention? Scoop these up now on Asos for $52.60.

## 4. Betsey Johnson Leigh

![Betsey Johnson Leigh](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/4_betsey-johnson-leigh_208x312.jpg)

I’ve fallen for Betsey Johnson’s girly-chic designs before, but these boots take the cake. Complete with ankle harnesses and star studs, they’re sure statement-makers! And the designs are down-to-earth enough that they won’t steal all of the attention. Snag them now on 6pm for $139.99.

## 5. Prairie Companion Boots

![Prairie Companion Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/5_prairie-companion-boots_600x766.jpg)

Indulge in the western trend with a pair of versatile cognac leatherette boots. The stacked heel will add inches to your height but is still comfy enough to wear for hours on end. They’d work especially well with a floral printed dress, knit tights, and a sweet floppy hat. Buy them now on ShopRuche for $74.99.

## 6. Sueded Slouchy Cowboy Boot

![Sueded Slouchy Cowboy Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/6_sueded-slouchy-cowboy-boot_393x561.jpg)

If you’re daring enough to incorporate colored boots into your winter wardrobe, check out these sultry red cowboy boots. The slouchy style makes them perfect to wear with skinny jeans or tights and a mini skirt. And the price is oh-so-right! Pick them up now on Charlotte Russe for just $35.50.

## 7. Dolce Vita Light Brown Suede 'Hal' Lace up Ankle Boots

![Dolce Vita Light Brown Suede 'Hal' Lace up Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/Dolce-Vita-Light-Brown-Suede-Hal-Lace-up-Ankle-Boots_480x360.jpg)

When you slip on these stunningly crafted lace-up ankle booties, you’ll feel like a style star. For a perfectly put-together look, pair them with a patterned dress, tights, and a pea coat. You can pick these up now on Bluefly for $130.50.

## 8. Marais USA Women’s Army Boot

![Marais USA Women’s Army Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/8_marais-usa-womens-army-boot_285x395.jpg)

Army boots don’t have to be heavy and unfeminine. Marais USA’s army style is simple, fitted, and sleek –and not at all clunky! I wear mine with wool tights and sweater dresses for a tough, edgy look. Buy them now on Amazon for $44.10.
