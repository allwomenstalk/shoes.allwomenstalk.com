# 9 Sleek and Stylish Silver Shoes ...

Put your best foot forward this season with some stylish silver shoes. Metallic accents are as fashionable as ever, and shoes are a fun way to get in on this trend. Whether it’s a pair of basic ballet flats or something more outrageous, anything goes when it comes to choosing a pair of silver shoes. Get some style inspiration from the following pairs of stylish silver shoes.

## 1. Silver Flats

![Silver Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/1_silver-flats_600x252.jpg)

A pair of flat stylish silver shoes will work well with most of your wardrobe. They’re great for wearing during the day and are perfect for transitioning into the night. These Mary Jane style shoes by Miu Miu are a splurge at around $600 but you can get a whole lot of similar styles in stores like Forever 21 and Topshop for under $40. Since they have a lower heel, you can easily get away with teaming your silver flats with short skirts and shorts.

## 2. Silver Points

![Silver Points](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/2_silver-points_240x247.jpg)

Silver pointed shoes have been on the radar for some time now. Take a cue from the recent runways of Louis Vuitton and add some edge to your look with a pair of silver points. These Manolo Blahnik points are a classic albeit expensive option. Don’t worry though because you can always look to stores like ASOS, Modcloth, and Nordstrom for a range of high and low heeled silver points.

## 3. Silver Heeled Sandals

![Silver Heeled Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/3_silver-heeled-sandals_600x496.jpg)

Silver heeled sandals are a great option for evening and formal outfits. They’re perfect for pairing with your favourite party dresses and cocktail gowns. Stick to shoes with slimmer straps for something more timeless. Designer brands like Jimmy Choo and Yves Saint Laurent do classic strappy heels while stores like ASOS and Forever 21 are always good for a bargain.

## 4. Silver Holograph Shoes

![Silver Holograph Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/4_silver-holograph-shoes_600x323.jpg)

Holographic finishes are a fun take on the silver footwear trend. They usually have a lighter silver finish and hints of colour, depending on the way the light hits. Designer holographic pumps by Stella McCartney will set you back a pretty penny but these flat holographic sandals from ASOS are a fun and affordable take on the trend.

## 5. Silver Boots

![Silver Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/5_silver-boots_264x216.jpg)

For a really edgy take on silver footwear, try a pair of boots. Stick to ankle length boots as anything too high might end up resembling moon boots. These Opening Ceremony boots are on the expensive side at a couple of hundred dollars but brands like BCBG Max Azria and Shakuhachi are much more reasonable.

## 6. Silver Sequin Shoes

![Silver Sequin Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/6_silver-sequin-shoes_600x502.jpg)

Glitter and sequin embellishments are still as popular as ever, especially where shoes are involved. Invest in the original trendsetting shoes by Miu Miu or hit up bargain buys at Topshop, Forever 21, and ASOS. They’re perfect for adding a bit of glitz and glamour to a plain outfit.

## 7. Silver Sneakers

![Silver Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/7_silver-sneakers_600x490.jpg)

Nothing screams sport luxe quite like a pair of metallic sneakers. Add some high definition shine to your outfit with a pair of sporty silver sneakers. Splurge on designer versions by Isabel Marant and Pierre Hardy or save with designs by Forever 21 and Miss KG. Team your sneakers with anything from skinny jeans to sporty-looking skirts and dresses.

## 8. Silver Loafers

![Silver Loafers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/8_silver-loafers_600x256.jpg)

Most loafers come in a soft suede or velvet finish. This season, why not mix things up with a pair of loafers in a glitzy metallic finish? Designers like Valentino (pictured here), Miu Miu, and Charlotte Olympia all do luxe version of the silver loafer. However, you can find plenty of budget buys by browsing stores like ASOS and Modcloth.

## 9. Silver Lace Ups

Silver lace-up shoes look smart and stylish. A pair of silver lace-ups by Bass or ASOS will set you back around the $80-$100 mark while luxury styles by Esquivel will cost you at least $600. They’d look great teamed with anything from cropped pants to midi skirts.

When it comes to silver shoes, there’s a range of different styles to choose from. You don’t have to break the budget on this luxe look either, with plenty of affordable options available at your usual chain store suspects. What do you think of these silver shoes?
