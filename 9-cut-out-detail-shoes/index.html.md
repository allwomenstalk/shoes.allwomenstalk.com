# 9 Cut\-out Detail Shoes ...

A trend that I’m enjoying right now is the cut-out shoe. The best part about this trend is that it can be applied to about any type of shoe. So it doesn’t matter if wear boots, sandals, or pumps, there’s bound to be a cut-out shoe to suit you. Below are just nine shining examples.

## 1. Belle by Sigerson Morrison Knotted Oxford

![Belle by Sigerson Morrison Knotted Oxford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/kq/p3d75x2854c8f71abf351009961657_300x86.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This knotted oxford shoe is an elegant take on the cut-out trend. It’s made from black cut-out leather entwined with black patent leather lacing. I think these shoes would look great with anything, from tailored shorts to full skirts. They’re the perfect shoe to have on high rotation.

## 2. Topshop Jenna Wine Suede Cut out Shoes

![Topshop Jenna Wine Suede Cut out Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iv/zr/f61fgaky54c8f71ba6b7d386844233_300x213.jpg)

**Price:** $125.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/2bvB2N)
The deep red wine colour is simply stunning. The suede material is an interesting and luxe feature. I love the demure of the round-toe paired with the sleek cut-out design. These shoes are well suited for both work and play.

## 3. Modcloth Going Places Heel

**Price:** $37.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I absolutely adore these heels. The bright red colour is bold and punchy. The scallop trim and the subtle cut-out details along the edges are really interesting. Wear these with a black and white outfit to really make them stand out.

## 4. Topshop Maisy off White Laser Cut Brogues

![Topshop Maisy off White Laser Cut Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2011/03/shoes2_350x233.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/EsP6Na)
These leather brogues will add a touch of androgynous elegance to your outfit. They feature both punch-hole and star cut outs. The muted off white shade makes them perfectly on-trend for the summer months. Pair them with pretty pastels for a daytime look, and dark bodycon dresses and leather jackets at night.

## 5. Modcloth Perfect Photo Op Flat

**Price:** $102.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love the quirkiness of these shoes! These brogue-style flats are the perfect combination of bright colours and classic styling. They’ve got a certain modern vintage feel about them. When styling these shoes, remember to just have fun with your outfit!

## 6. Forever 21 Cut out Ballet Flats

**Price:** $16.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These leatherette ballet style flats would suit most occasions. They are basic enough to be worn with ‘every day’ outfits, but the cut-out details also make them an interesting statement shoe. Pair these with jeans at a tee for a casual look, or printed tights and a mini dress for a night out.

## 7. Forever21 Suedette Teardrop Wedges

![Forever21 Suedette Teardrop Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/se/wt/gjqizqcm54c8f71f89b22704793987_300x276.jpg)

**Price:** $25.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Take the cut-out trend to the next level with these sky high wedges. They are made from suedette and feature a peep-toe heel and cut out details around the ankle cuff. The taupe colour is on-trend for spring. They’ll really help you stand out from the crowd!

## 8. Chie Mihara Wala Slot Cut-out Flat Sandal

**Price:** $251.02 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/0mkH51)
These flat leather sandals would make a great addition to any summer wardrobe. They feature a slot cut-out design and buckle ankle strap. Wear them with shorts or sun dresses. They’d be great for a casual day out, or even dinner and drinks at night.

## 9. Kerb off White Patent T-bar Shoes

![Kerb off White Patent T-bar Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vr/m8/i6sckzvd54c8f720e8a53568492261_300x169.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/fe8wzY)
These t-bar shoes would be perfect for completing the school-girl style dressing trend. I love the patent finish. They feature a wooden sole and delicate teardrop cut-out feature. They’d look great paired with stockings or knee high socks.

Do you like the cut-out look? Which of these would you like to wear?
