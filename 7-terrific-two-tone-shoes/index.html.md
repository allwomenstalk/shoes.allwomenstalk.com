# 7 Terrific Two\-Tone Shoes ...

Two-tone shoes are an excellent way of brightening up your feet. From neutral leathers to bold block colours, there are two-tone shoes to suit all tastes. You can choose classic designs such as oxfords and loafers, or something more modern like graphic suede sandals. Have a look at some terrific two-tone shoes below.

## 1. Topshop Mack Two Tone Lace up Brogues

![Topshop Mack Two Tone Lace up Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/cj/tneqzh3d54c8f9be8ecdf128366168_300x182.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/hmeVa1)
These terrific two-tone shoes come in a classic brogue design. The toe is slightly pointed and the overall shoe is quite slim. The chocolate and tan leathers complement each other quite well. Wear these with a full-skirted dress to add a masculine twist to an otherwise feminine outfit.

## 2. Shellys of London Contrasting Leather Boots

![Shellys of London Contrasting Leather Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/h7/weikpy1f54c8f9bf735b1818773332_300x304.jpg)

**Price:** $102.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Take a step back in time with these retro-inspired boots. They are reminiscent of the Mod style of dressing. These boots are made from light pink leather and feature a soft grey at the centre of the design. Recreate the retro look with a shift dress and some opaque tights. Go easy on the bee-hive hairdo, though!

## 3. Modcloth Jeffrey Campbell Wingtip Shoes

![Modcloth Jeffrey Campbell Wingtip Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kc/mc/ysdfj8c454c8f9c03849b530917305_300x150.jpg)

**Price:** $102.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These preppy looking shoes are a fresh and funky take on the classic wingtip. These terrific two-tone shoes feature a slight wooden heel, rounded toe, and cut-out detail. These are well suited for those wishing to take a fashion chance. Wear these with jeans, a t-shirt, and slouchy cardigan for a cool and casual look.

## 4. Bloch Luxury Two-Tone Patent Leather Ballerina Flats

**Price:** $165.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9QOxvj)
Ballerina flats are a great wardrobe staple. These peach and white shoes are sweet and stylish. The patent leather adds a bit of polish, and the two-tone detail adds interest to the design. These are understated enough to be worn with a range of outfits.

## 5. Marni Two-Tone Suede Sandals

![Marni Two-Tone Suede Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sl/fq/r6nw9z0n54c8f9c1b98cd167393216_300x223.jpg)

**Price:** $580.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/XYqHOM)
These block heels may not be to everyone’s tastes. This design of shoe, however, is becoming increasingly popular. These sandals feature contrasting teal and black suede straps, wooden block heels, and silver fastenings. Wear these with complementing dark colours.

## 6. Asos Levan Double Bow Flat Point Ballerina Pump

**Price:** $44.83 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/wYT3HV)
The shoes seem to take their cue from those recently seen on Louis Vuitton Fall 2010 catwalks. The large double bow detail is the standout design feature. The body of the shoe has a ribbed satin texture while the bow is made from a lizard-effect trim. These would make a fresh addition to any outfit, from a classic jeans and blazer combination to something a little more risqué like a bustier dress.

## 7. Topshop Luminate Grey Ankle Strap High Heeled Sandals

![Topshop Luminate Grey Ankle Strap High Heeled Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/tn/sujgskku54c8f9c341ee3487344111_300x321.jpg)

**Price:** $145.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/sn9xlq)
These shoes feature contrasting grey and pink straps, Velcro fastenings at the ankle, and a platform heel. The overlapping straps also lend to a cage-like design. These would look extremely chic paired with white tailored trousers and a loose-fitting top.

The two-tone trend is not for everyone, but those who work it well will earn major fashion points. What are your thoughts on this trend, and which of the above terrific two-tone shoes do you like best?
