# 8 High\-Shine Metallic Shoes to Glam up That Dress ...

Metallic Shoes are a trend to be taking notice of right now. The metallic shoes look is slightly futuristic but with all the glamour of contemporary fashion. Metallic shoes are especially great for dressing up an evening look. Below are a few metallic shoes to glam up your favourite going-out dress.

## 1. Brian Atwood Maniac Pumps

![Brian Atwood Maniac Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vq/bn/uobzh6la54c92fe44d67e881786268_340x278.jpg)

**Price:** $665.00 at [mytheresa.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.mytheresa.com/us_en/maniac-pumps-103411.html)
These metallic shoes are certainly glamorous. The rose-gold leather is still noticeably metallic, without being in your face. These heels have a concealed platform and high stiletto heel. Team them with a basic black dress for a simple and chic evening look.

## 2. Topshop Glacier Silver Bow Pointed Court Shoes

![Topshop Glacier Silver Bow Pointed Court Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/r2/uxggd59k54c92fe536c18286555679_350x288.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/x5honI)
These pointed court shoes mix a classic design with modern styling. The pointed toe and bow detail provide a bit of old-school charm, while the high-shine silver finish is futuristically cool. These shoes would look great paired with a brightly coloured outfit.

## 3. Lanvin Metallic Leather Ballerina Flats

![Lanvin Metallic Leather Ballerina Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u0/q3/xb1x5plv54c92fe62e416339757760_350x161.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/iJsyFz)
If heels aren’t your thing, try glamming up your dress with some metallic flats. These ballerina style flats would look great teamed with a structured cream dress. These shoes have a cracked-effect gold finish. They also have a round toe, elastic trim, and concealed heel.

## 4. Alexander Wang Fabiana Metallic Leather Sandals

![Alexander Wang Fabiana Metallic Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ue/wk/yk2te4uv54c92fe71688c315380563_347x346.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/xBC5zB)
These shiny stiletto heels ooze contemporary design. The shoes are made from silver leather and have an almond toe, covered heel, and buckle fastening ankle strap. The focal point of these shoes has to be the detachable cape style heel covers. Team these heels with a shift dress and silver accessories.

## 5. Asos Lovely Bow Trim Metallic Shoes

![Asos Lovely Bow Trim Metallic Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pr/sp/i1sgi3zs54c92fe7e48e4728217501_350x139.jpg)

**Price:** $43.10 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/gef9j6)
Glam up your everyday wardrobe with these metallic shoes. They have a classic ballerina flat design and feature a grosgrain ribbon trim and bow, as well as a small metal bow embellishment. Pair these with a floaty chiffon dress for a real ballerina-inspired take on things.

## 6. Topshop Sienna Silver Metallic Platform Peep Toe Shoes

![Topshop Sienna Silver Metallic Platform Peep Toe Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/b9/bfrvsdjz54c92fe8ac7c0069776803_328x350.jpg)

**Price:** $128.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/6tw7wH)
Take the metallic trend to the next level, quite literally, with these silver platform heels. These shoes have a peep toe design and feature a high platform and slim stiletto heel. They are made form 100% leather and have a dusty metallic finish. Team these with your favourite party dress for a flawless look.

## 7. Lanvin Metallic Cone Heel Sandals

_ **Price:** $375.00 at mytheresa.com_
Coloured metallic shoes are a great alternative to the more common silver and gold finishes. These strappy leather sandals have a pink metallic finish. They have a side buckle closure and metallic gold cone heel. Wear these with a full-skirted dress for a fun nighttime look.

## 8. Nicholas Kirkwood Lace-up Leather Sandals

**Price:** $819.00 at _mytheresa.com_
These lace-up metallic sandals are very fashion-forward. They have a crinkled metallic finish and feature cutout details. They also have dark blue suede shoelaces. Team these with a drape-effect dress for a contemporary finish.

For day or night, metallic shoes are a guaranteed way of glamming up an outfit. From smooth metallic finishes to cracked-leather effects, metallic shoes are an on-trend way to update your favourite dress. Which of these metallic shoes do you prefer?
