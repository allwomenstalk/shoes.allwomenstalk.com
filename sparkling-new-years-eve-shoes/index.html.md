# 7 Sparkling New Years Eve Shoes ...

Sparkling shoes to remember will really get that New Year's Eve party started! Whether you’re meeting your beau or attending a friend’s big bash, you’ll want to dress to impress. And that means a healthy dose of glitter to glam up your little black dress! With a pair of these 7 sparkling shoes on your feet, you can expect a kiss come midnight. Just don't forget to kick up your heels as you lean in for that smooch.

## 1. Diamond Princess Platform Pump

![Diamond Princess Platform Pump](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/1_diamond-princess-platform-pump_352x477.jpg)

For a mere $50 at Charlotte Russe, these sparkling shoes can be yours. Who said you had to break the bank to look like a fairytale princess? The iridescent rhinestones would make Cinderella jealous –and you don’t have to bolt when the clock strikes 12, you lucky lady! I’d pair these with leather skinny jeans and a fitted top to really dazzle ‘em.

## 2. Truth or Dare by Madonna Jarrard

![Truth or Dare by Madonna Jarrard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/2_truth-or-dare-by-madonna-jarrard_320x312.jpg)

Leave it to Madge to create a shiny (and super sexy) pair of glitter booties. The peep-toe is perfect for showing off that fresh and festive pedi! I’d wear ‘em with opaque black tights, a curve-hugging mini, and dazzling earrings. Scoop these up now for $200 on Zappos. You won't regret this investment!

## 3. Chinese Laundry Fall for Glitter

![Chinese Laundry Fall for Glitter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/3_chinese-laundry-fall-for-glitter_352x312.jpg)

Girly girls will fall for this pretty pick! Complete with bow accents and more glitter than a 5-year-old’s art project, they’ll make any little black dress stand out. Pick ‘em up now for $46.86 at Zappos.

## 4. Kate Spade New York Licorice Too

![Kate Spade New York Licorice Too](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/4_kate-spade-new-york-licorice-too_240x208.jpg)

Pack on the sparkle and couture with Kate Spade’s colorful pumps. They’ll add just the right amount of flirt to a demure black frock. At $350 on Zappos, they’re undeniably pricey, but you’ll wear them again and again. For example, I'd use these to make my dark wash skinny jeans and last year's blouses look super pricey. And don’t your toes deserve a treat this season?

## 5. Betsey Johnson Bliiingg

Not a sparkle kind of gal? Never fear, ‘cause Betsey Johnson is here! This pick packs a punch with edgy golden studs. Plus, flat shoes will ensure that you’re not hobbling to your car at the end of a long night. Find these for $80.99 on Zappos –they’re perfect to wear with jeans and a tee for a casual night with friends.

## 6. Michael Antonio Anderson Glitter

Go for the gold with a leg-lengthening pair of wedges. You’ll score all the added height (without any of the pain) and they’ll look simply stunning with a mini skirt, blazer, and opaque tights. These are currently priced at a wallet friendly $38.94 on 6pm.

## 7. ALDO Safko

![ALDO Safko](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/7_aldo-safko_480x256.jpg)

Charm any crowd with these golden bow flats. For a mere $31.99 on 6pm, you’ll get a cozy pair of standout shoes. I’d wear them to spruce up my go-to little black dress or black skinny jeans. They’re prime for tall gals or those of you who can’t wear heels without aches and pains (like me!).

These 7 pairs of New Year’s Eve shoes will help you make a statement at any party. They're pretty much essentials to gals like me, who tend to go for simple dresses and let our accessories do the talking. Which ones are your favorites? Do you have any of your own picks to add?
