# 7 Glitter\-Finish Shoes ...

Take a cue from Dorothy’s adventures in Oz and invest in a pair of glitter-finish shoes this season. They are a great way to add an interesting twist to an outfit. The look can range from glitzy to demure. It’s all about choosing the right pair for you. Check out a few fabulous glitter-finish shoes below.

## 1. Marc Jacobs Glitter-Finish Leather Sandals

**Price:** $575.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/4KzAjG)
The glitter detailing on these platform heels is a chic take on the trend. These sandals feature leather tube straps, square toes, and gold inner soles. The light brown tube straps also feature gold thread detailing. The overall look of these shoes is casually dressy. Worn with a shirt dress or t-shirt and trousers, these would be well suited for day wear.

## 2. Topshop Voila Gold Glitter Two Strap Mary Jane Shoes

![Topshop Voila Gold Glitter Two Strap Mary Jane Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vq/vb/lwkhl9gj54c8fc73cfb62642498566_300x150.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/QP8qJv)
These glitter Mary Jane style shoes are perfectly sweet. The gold glitter finish is subtle and the design is sensible yet stylish. They feature two front straps and a slight heel. These would look great paired with vintage-style dresses and midi skirts.

## 3. Miss KG Harajuku Sparkly Platform Sandal

![Miss KG Harajuku Sparkly Platform Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dk/u9/wshn5v2h54c8fc74a48da957472275_300x240.jpg)

**Price:** $143.44 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/L62CsI)
These glitter-finish shoes belong on the dance floor. These sparkly stilettos have a peep toe design, cross over straps, and buckle fastening at the ankle. The silver glitter finish works well with the nude trims. If you want to tone down the ‘wow’ factor of these shoes, try pairing them with straight-legged jeans and a silky cream blouse.

## 4. Patricia Blanchet Glitter Brogues

![Patricia Blanchet Glitter Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pa/xy/ptz823k054c8fc75912dd593811534_300x225.jpg)

**Price:** $134.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love the frivolity of these multicoloured glitter-finish shoes. They come in a brogue heel design and feature a ribbon lace-up front. The all over glitter detail is simply dazzling. Wear these with a full-skirted little black dress for a fun party outfit.

## 5. Steve Madden Laceless Oxford

![Steve Madden Laceless Oxford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o1/b5/lhla9sbd54c8fc766e73c430197909_300x181.jpg)

**Price:** $79.95 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These glitter shoes are a great way to incorporate the oxford trend into an evening outfit.

The laceless design provides an interesting twist. These shoes also feature wingtip detailing, which adds extra texture to the overall glitter finish. Wear with opaque stockings and a flirty dress for a night time look.

## 6. Miu Miu Glitter-Finish Pumps

![Miu Miu Glitter-Finish Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/ku/h9q8tf7954c8fc777d3f5996864167_300x283.jpg)

**Price:** $595.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/4KIj8W)
The black colour of these pumps makes them perfect for finishing off a range of outfits. These glitter-finish shoes have an almond toe, concealed platform, and patent leather covered stiletto heel. The silhouette of the shoe is timeless and elegant. Pair with neutral coloured cocktail dresses and bright accents for a dazzling evening look.

## 7. Dune Starry Glitter Ballerina Pumps

![Dune Starry Glitter Ballerina Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/i5/mn8vyb7b54c8fc784dae3589160035_300x92.jpg)

**Price:** $17.93 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/L6tNSE)
Black ballet flats are a must in any wardrobe. These fold-up flats have a stretch trim and faux leather details. The fold-up design means that they can easily be stored in a handbag and whipped out to relieve sore feet from a day of heel-wearing. These chic shoes would look great both during the day and at night.

Once you find a pair of glitter shoes that suit you, there’s no turning back. Which of these glitter-finish shoes are your favourites?
