# 8 Pretty Patterned Shoes ...

Patterned shoes are all the rage right now. And if you’re anything like me, they’ll also add some edge to those neutral winter ensembles. I’ve scoured the web for picks that’ll show off your daring side. With these 8 pretty patterned shoes, you’ll soon be the life of your next party. Work it, girl!

## 1. Betsey Johnson Vitas

![Betsey Johnson Vitas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/1_betsey-johnson-vitas_304x312.jpg)

I’m saying meow to these patterned shoes. The leopard print is totally on trend and who could resist these poppin’ pink crisscross straps? Not me! I’d pair them with skintight black skinny jeans, a v-neck tee, and a sexy leather blazer for a fiery and unforgettable going out look. Best of all, you can score these for just $24 on 6pm.

## 2. Lace-up Platform Heel Bootie

![Lace-up Platform Heel Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/2_lace-up-platform-heel-bootie_200x200.jpg)

If you’re a typical girly girl, go bold with platform booties. The floral print will keep you true to your personal style, but with an added dose of daring. And at $48.50 on Charlotte Russe, the price is certainly right! I’d experiment with these a bit, wearing them with tights and pale pink dresses or skinny jeans and a button down. The options are endless.

## 3. Tie-Dye Pointed Wedges

![Tie-Dye Pointed Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/3_tie-dye-pointed-wedges_280x280.jpg)

While everyone else is strutting around in black booties and nude flats, tie-dye will set you apart from the crowd. And these platform wedges will pack on the height for shorter gals. You can find them now for $32.80 on Forever21. I’d wear 'em to my next house party with opaque tights and a flirty mini.

## 4. Kate Spade New York Terry

![Kate Spade New York Terry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/4_kate-spade-new-york-terry_240x112.jpg)

Forget hot guys, ‘cause I’m swooning over these Kate Spade flats. The bright pink cap toe really makes a statement, especially when keeping the rest of your outfit simple and neutral. I’d throw in an oversized tote and sunglasses to complete my glam look. Pick these up for $228 on Zappos Couture.

## 5. TOMS Silver Holiday Women’s Classics

![TOMS Silver Holiday Women’s Classics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/5_toms-silver-holiday-womens-classics_600x552.jpg)

Let it snow, let it snow, let it snow! Finish off your festive winter garb with TOMS’ slip-ons. They’re perfect to wear with jeans and a tee as you’re sipping hot cocoa indoors. As a TOMS-wearer, they’re oh-so-comfy and great to wear while running about town doing errands. Get them for $54 on TOMS.

## 6. Sam Edelman Novato

![Sam Edelman Novato](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/6_sam-edelman-novato_320x320.jpg)

Who knew polka dots could be so sexy? These Sam Edelmans prove that even the girliest among us can look smokin’ with the right pair of haute shoes. I’d save these for a hot springtime date or a winter party indoors. Get them for $61.99 on 6pm.

## 7. Madden Girl Hamlinz

![Madden Girl Hamlinz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/7_madden-girl-hamlinz_480x232.jpg)

Show off your stripes with these allover zebra print loafers. They’re perfect for any animal print lover –and who could resist that super cute pink trim? They’d look extra chic with a skirt and opaque tights for your first day at a new internship. Or, dress them down with jeans and a tee for a day of classes. They’re on sale now for $30.99 on 6pm.

## 8. Slip-on Wrap Floral Flat

![Slip-on Wrap Floral Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/8_slip-on-wrap-floral-flat_434x262.jpg)

Show off your sweet side with these floral print canvas flats. I’d wear them during my commute to work in the mornings. After all, doesn’t every gal need a break from those heels? Snag these on Charlotte Russe for a reasonable $19.50.

These are 8 of my favorite pairs of pretty printed shoes. Which ones are your favorites? Do you have any of your own options to add to the list?
