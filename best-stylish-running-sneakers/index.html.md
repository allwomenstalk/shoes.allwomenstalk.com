# 13 Best Stylish Running Sneakers ...

The right shoes are a must for exercise, but if stylish running sneakers top your priority list, you have many options at your fingertips. Running for exercise is a great way to burn calories, tone your muscles and protect your health, so it’s a wonderful way to stay in shape and love your body at the same time time. But if you’re not willing to give up your trend setting ways, these stylish running sneakers are the perfect combination of awesomeness and function.

## 1. Brooks Women’s PureConnect 2

![Brooks Women’s PureConnect 2](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09041824_7395_535x266.jpg)

The Brooks Women’s Pure Connect 2 is a running shoe you’ll love for years. Available in several color combinations and featuring breathable mesh and lightweight materials, these stylish running shoes get the job done while looking great too.

**Price:** $70 at [brooksrunning.com](https://www.brooksrunning.com/en_us?tid=aff:all:cv3x:usa&cjevent=bfae1a2a0cce11ed831c00880a82b839)

## 2. Nike Free Run+ 3

![Nike Free Run+ 3](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09041839_0475_350x222.jpg)

If you want a flexible running shoe with a great fit, look no further than the Nike Free Run+ 3. It’s also cushiony for comfort and comes in a variety of color combinations. There’s also a removable insole, just in case!

**Price:** $100 at [store.nike.com](http://store.nike.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Nike Flyknit Lunar1+ Women’s Running Shoe

This running shoe from Nike combines a cushioned and almost seamless design with fun colors for stylish shoe you’ll absolutely adore. Not only has this shoe been voted the best by “Runner’s World” magazine, but it’s also a top pick among expert runners.

**Price:** $160 at [store.nike.com](http://store.nike.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Nike Free Tr Fit 3

![Nike Free Tr Fit 3](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09041911_4417_350x223.jpg)

For a fun and funky pattern on your running shoes, the Nike Free Tr Fit 3 is the choice for you. It combines bright colors with lightweight and flexible materials. You’ll look great every time you strap them on and hit the pavement.

**Price:** $100 at [nike.com](http://www.nike.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. New Balance 1080V3

This features a crash pad, cushioning and the ultimate in arch support for a running shoe that will last for a long time. It’s also cute and trendy with bright colors and a fun design.

**Price:** $109.99 at [newbalance.com](https://www.newbalance.com/1080/?prefn1=genderAndAgeGroupCombo&prefv1=Men)

## 6. Asics Gel Nimbus 14

No one will be able to miss you as you run with these awesome shoes from Asics on your feet. They’re available in a few bright and girly colors, but they’re also a great choice if you want a durable and quality pair of running shoes.

**Price:** $140 at [asicsamerica.com](https://goo.gl/VKiR3Q)

## 7. New Balance 870 V2

The New Balance 870 V2 offers arch support and cushioning combined with great colors that you’ll love. This is great pick for runners with mild overpronation.

**Price:** $60.50 at _runningshoes.com_

## 8. Asics Gel Sustain TR

This running shoe from Asics has gel cushioning to keep you comfortable no matter how long you run. It’s also available in several fun colors and is lightweight enough that you might not even notice they’re on your feet.

**Price:** $85 at [jcpenney.com](http://www.jcpenney.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. Adidas Energy Boost Shoes

![Adidas Energy Boost Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10050219_3996_599x261.jpg)

The Adidas Energy Boost Shoes are lightweight and flexible and are designed to last for a long time. Combined with a range of color choices, this is a great shoe if you want to go for a bit before buying a new pair.

**Price:** $150 at [adidas.com](https://goo.gl/Su6O41)

## 10. Mizuno Wave Precision 13

With electric colors and a great looking design, these running shoes will quickly become your favorite pair of footwear. They also come recommended by “Runner’s World.”

**Price:** $109.95 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Women’s Asics Gel-Kayano 20

Featuring the ultimate in cushioning and responsive base, these shoes from Asics are the most luxurious choice out there. Maybe you’ll never want to stop running.

**Price:** $160 at [ladyfootlocker.com](http://www.ladyfootlocker.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 12. Reebok RealFlex 2.0

The Reebok RealFlex 2.0 running shoes conform to your foot so they respond well no matter what terrain you run on. They’re also cute and come in many colors. What more could a girl ask for?

**Price:** $64.99 at [shop.reebok.com](http://shop.reebok.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Skechers Go Run Lightweight Running Shoes

Skechers might make you think of skater shoes, but they also meet the needs of runners everywhere. The Go Run shoes have sensors that respond to your foot strike and the materials are lightweight. And best of all - they aren’t as expensive as they sound.

**Price:** $75 at [skechers.com](http://www.skechers.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
