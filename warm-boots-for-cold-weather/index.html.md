# 8 Warm Boots for Cold Weather ...

Among my must-haves for winter this year, I’ve added something new. Alongside “peppermint cocoa” and “cute fingerless gloves” is “warm boots.” I loathe the style-less UGG franchise, so I’ve spent weeks ferreting out warm boots for cold weather that are super-warm for playing in the snow, but still stylish. Here are my picks…

## 1. Columbia Minx

![Columbia Minx](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_columbia-minx_600x450.jpg)

Available is five sweet colors (including the purple shown here), these boots are warm, comfortable, and cuter than heck. They fit well, and aren’t stiff or uncomfortable like most warm boots. They’re waterproof but breathable, and the uppers are made with Columbia’s new Omni-Heat technology, to reflect your body heat to keep your toes toasty.

## 2. Khombu Saturn Lace

![Khombu Saturn Lace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/2_khombu-saturn-lace_600x450.jpg)

Who says warm boots can’t be stylish, too? These waterproof boots lace up the front and have a 2-inch wedge heel. Wouldn’t they look gorgeous with skinny jeans and an oversized sweater? They’re also available in dark brown.

## 3. Timberland Mount Holly

![Timberland Mount Holly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/3_timberland-mount-holly_600x450.jpg)

I am totally mad for plaid, so these boots caught my eye right off. They’re made by Timberland, too, so they’re not just cute boots, they’re warm boots, too! They’re also eco-conscious, with soles and uppers made of recycled materials.

## 4. Sperry Top-Sider Alpine

![Sperry Top-Sider Alpine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/4_sperry-top-sider-alpine_600x450.jpg)

These warm boots come in three color combinations, but for some reason, I like the white/cherry red the best! I love the detailing (especially the buckle!) and the almost biker-boot styling. I also love that they have a zipper up the inside for easy off and on.

## 5. Sorel Tivoli High

![Sorel Tivoli High](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/5_sorel-tivoli-high_600x450.jpg)

I can’t even describe how comfortable these warm boots are! They’re perfect for playing outside in the cold, but they’re cute, too! I’d wear them with cream leggings and a big comfy sweater… anyone want to help me make a snow fort?

## 6. Keen Hoodoo High

![Keen Hoodoo High](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/6_keen-hoodoo-high_600x450.jpg)

During the summer, I practically live in my Keen sandals, so I was thrilled when I found out they make warm boots, too! These ones are stylish and comfy, and I love the color! Not into purple? They also come in mostly black with aqua trim.

## 7. Merrell Katia Waterproof

![Merrell Katia Waterproof](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/7_merrell-katia-waterproof_600x450.jpg)

Oh my gosh, aren’t these cute? I love the faux fur on the outside, somehow waterproofed to keep it from looking dirty and matted after the first snowfall. The soles are designed for traction, so they’re much more sensible than they look… bonus!

## 8. The North Face Destiny down

![The North Face Destiny down](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/8_the-north-face-destiny-down_600x450.jpg)

Look at those eyelets! I love the over-sized look, along with the shiny black finish. These warm boots are made with down, so they’re extra-cozy. They’re also made to be comfortable for walking, and for playing, so you can enjoy the winter wonderland for hours at a time.

With so many stylish, warm boots, I can’t wait for snow to fall! I especially love the Keens and the pair by The North Face, but which of these warm boots do you like best, and why? How would you wear them, and for what fun winter activity?
