# 9 Trendy T\-Bar Shoes a Fashionista Cant do without ...

**T-Bar Shoes** are a mainstay on the fashion scene. It’s a look that can be quite classically feminine. However, there are many different interpretations of the T-bar style of shoe. **T-bar shoes** usually take the form of classic pumps or ballet flats, but the T-bar design can also be found on a range of footwear such as sandals. It’s a design detail that is quite versatile, as you’ll see from the gorgeous **T-bar shoes **below.

## 1. Chloe Embellished Suede T-bar Ballerina Flats

![Chloe Embellished Suede T-bar Ballerina Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/f3/ainut28854c9362da9703920633004_300x183.jpg)

**Price:** $595.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hVya9O)
These designer Chloe flats are all things dainty and feminine. They have a black suede main and feature a T-bar strap with crystal embellished bows. These shoes are definitely what you’d call an investment pieces. They’d look great paired with a ballet-inspired outfit of a flowing pleated skirt and crisp collared blouse.

## 2. Modcloth No Need to Spree Heel

![Modcloth No Need to Spree Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t0/vx/c8stip7954c9362e7a822181144412_300x278.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A pair of black heels is a timeless part of any wardrobe. These T-bar shoes are an affordable and stylish option. The large bow detail at the bottom of the T-bar strap makes for a playful addition to the overall design. These are a versatile style of shoe and can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

## 3. Topshop Chain T-bar Sandals

![Topshop Chain T-bar Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/hg/pzgmmsx054c9362f4b4ea531742615_300x148.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/IFIPFo)
Get your summer footwear sorted with these T-bar sandals. They have a slightly edgier design, with a silver chain T-bar detail. They also have an ankle strap and toe loop. These sandals would look extremely stylish when worn with a pair of silky black printed pants and a loose singlet.

## 4. Valentino Studded T-bar Pumps

![Valentino Studded T-bar Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/aw/abkljdpm54c936303aa4e962299558_300x322.jpg)

**Price:** $995.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/QKlRe5)
These Valentino T-bar shoes manage to incorporate both classic and modern details into the design. The lace detail is quite traditional, while the stud embellishments on the T-bar strap are more modern. These are definitely for the daring fashionista who has the budget to accommodate for such fancies.

## 5. Opening Ceremony Par Robert Clergerie T-Strap Wedge

**Price:** $690.00 at _openingceremony.us_
These shoes are definitely more trend-based. They have an on-trend glitter finish and have a flatform-style wedge heel. In addition to the glitter main, they also have a patent straps. These shoes ideally cater to a more youthful crowd and would look great teamed with a slinky shift dress and beaten up leather jacket.

## 6. Giuseppe Zanotti Satin Ballet Flats

![Giuseppe Zanotti Satin Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ke/pg/o73sz3i654c93631d6da5016544781_300x151.jpg)

**Price:** $325.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/t3y4b5)
These Giuseppe Zanotti ballet flats are an elegant choice of T-bar shoes. They have a champagne satin finish and feature a clear crystal embellished toe cap. They also feature a delicate bow detail at the ankle. Team these flats with a sheer maxi skirt and cropped sweater.

## 7. Jimmy Choo Ulla Sandals

_ **Price:** $515.00 at mytheresa.com_
If you like your T-bar shoes with a bit of extra height, then these may be to your liking. They come in a neutral brown suede colour and have a thick wood heel and platform. Wear them during the day with cropped chinos, or at night with a fun metallic party dress.

## 8. Musa Metallic Leather T-bar Sandals

**Price:** $100.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/32AmC1)
These leather T-bar sandals are perfect for putting on high rotation in your summer wardrobe. They have a typical T-bar design and come in an on-trend metallic finish. They’re a neutral type of sandal that could easily be worn with a range of outfits.

## 9. Lanvin Satin T-bar Pumps

![Lanvin Satin T-bar Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/ar/jcz4iwmf54c9363439c90680554716_300x290.jpg)

**Price:** $794.50 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/qQU7bX)
A pair of black pumps is a staple in any wardrobe. These design satin T-bar shoes cut a sleek figure with their skinny stiletto heel and pointed toe design. These would look great worn with a metallic cocktail dress, and teamed with an array of oversized jewels.

**T-bar shoes** are absolutely gorgeous, not to mention fashion forward. No matter what type of outfit you’re wearing, there’s a T-bar style of shoe to suit most needs. A pair of **T-bar shoes** in the form of pumps or ballet flats is a classic choice, especially if you’re after something that’s going to have a bit more longevity in your wardrobe.
