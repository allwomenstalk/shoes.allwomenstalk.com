# 8 Animal Print Shoes Youll Love ...

Animal Print Shoes are guaranteed to add extra oomph to an outfit. You can find animal print shoes in a range of styles and designs. Loafers, ballet slippers, boots – there are animal print shoes to suit all tastes. Add a touch of fun and frivolity to your outfit with the following pairs of animal print shoes.

## 1. Miu Miu Zebra Print Suede Brogues

![Miu Miu Zebra Print Suede Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/ga/wlma33oj54c92ef8c76bf671146841_300x149.jpg)

**Price:** $490.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/R7Eqdh)
A pair of lace up flat shoes is a must in any wardrobe. These animal print shoes are an on-trend and stylish choice. They have a black and tan zebra print finish with contrasting black laces. They also have a covered heel. Wear these shoes with tailored shorts and crisp blouses.

## 2. Topshop Adina Leopard High Ankle Boot

![Topshop Adina Leopard High Ankle Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ih/g8/m64mi6vg54c92ef9b7625509057833_300x298.jpg)

**Price:** $160.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/fsXBnc)
These leopard print ankle boots have an exaggerated silhouette. This is mostly due to the concealed platform and stacked heel. These boots have a flattering ankle-length cut and feature exposed zip fastenings to the sides. Team these boots with black skinny jeans and a cream silk shirt for an effortlessly stylish look.

## 3. Topshop Valentine Leopard Print Patent Toe Ballet Pumps

![Topshop Valentine Leopard Print Patent Toe Ballet Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x9/ug/thlrs81p54c92efaac908060387066_300x146.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/SJCSaH)
These ballet pumps are a great choice of every day shoe. They feature a leopard print fabric body and black patent toe. They also have bow details to the front. These could easily be incorporated into a range of outfits.

## 4. Topshop Madison Dalmatian Loafers

![Topshop Madison Dalmatian Loafers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/to/oc/lhdco7nc54c92efb7d0dd428138631_300x145.jpg)

**Price:** $66.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/0uYtuW)
The loafer is still enjoying popularity amongst fashionistas. Here it is reinvented in a striking design. These narrow penny loafers have pony hair effect finish and come in a Dalmatian design. Work a casual look and pair these with a crew-neck tee, full denim skirt, and canvas tote bag.

## 5. Alexander McQueen Leopard-print Calf Hair Pumps

![Alexander McQueen Leopard-print Calf Hair Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/lg/oq9yfdhx54c92efc56fed654236899_300x289.jpg)

**Price:** $955.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/HiyYCc)
A peep-toe heel is timeless. These animal print shoes by Alexander McQueen feature a peep-toe, concealed platform, and high stiletto heel. They also feature contrasting burgundy trims. Wear these with a simple black shift dress, patent handbag, and chunky jewellery.

## 6. Modcloth Happy to Zebra Flat Animal Print Shoes

![Modcloth Happy to Zebra Flat Animal Print Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dd/z7/jcpivcx754c92efd31dbd613205224_300x159.jpg)

**Price:** $37.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These shoes are a fun and affordable option. They feature a black and white zebra pattern and lace-up front. They’d be great for spicing up your day to day wardrobe. These shoes would look great teamed with burgundy coloured jeans and a basic black top.

## 7. Bloch Carina Snake-print Leather Ballerina Flats

![Bloch Carina Snake-print Leather Ballerina Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/rz/kqlo6ty454c92efe0543f995009774_300x155.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hov6I1)
These snake-print shoes are a chic choice. They are made from snake-print leather and feature a contrasting black toe cap. They also feature zig zag stitching and are lined in a light pink suede. When wearing these shoes, accent your outfit with accessories in orange hues for a fashion-forward look.

## 8. Topshop Maccoy Zebra Print Brushed Chelsea Ankle Boots

![Topshop Maccoy Zebra Print Brushed Chelsea Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/gn/au5o4j4l54c92efec2c04312722129_300x202.jpg)

**Price:** $75.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/JllTIa)
The Chelsea boot is enjoying its time in the fashion limelight. These boots feature the design’s signature elastic gusset as well as an eye-catching zebra print. They also have a low heel and pull tab at the ankle. These animal print shoes would easily brighten up a basic ‘jeans and tee’ outfit.

When styling animal print shoes, be sure to keep the rest of your outfit fairly simple. You do want to let your feet do the talking, after all! Are you a fan of these animal print shoes?
