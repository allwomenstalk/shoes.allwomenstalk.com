# 7 Stylish Snow Boots for Winter ...

There’s no better way to keep your feet cozy this winter than with a pair of stylish snow boots. While traditional boots are big and clunky, these fashion forward snow warriors will keep you cozy and fabulous. Below are 7 of my favorite options. It’s easier than ever to achieve toasty toes in the dead of winter!

## 1. Khombu Birch High 2

![Khombu Birch High 2](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_khombu-birch-high-2_480x360.jpg)

Despite their faux fur collars, these stylish snow boots won’t make you look like the Abominable Snowman. They will, however, keep you warm with insulated lining and a no-slip outsole –perfect for trekking through the snow. You can find these for $120 on Zappos along with similar styles.

## 2. Sperry Top-Sider Alpine

![Sperry Top-Sider Alpine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/2_sperry-top-sider-alpine_480x360.jpg)

These stylish Sperry snow boots may be light and bright, but that doesn’t mean they’ll let the slush in! This pair comes equipped with weatherproof protecting, insulated lining, and a non-mark rubber outsole. Find these on Zappos for $170 and prepare to score trendy and toasty boots that’ll last all season long.

## 3. The North Face Shellista Lace

![The North Face Shellista Lace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/3_the-north-face-shellista-lace_275x279.jpg)

If you own or have owned a North Face coat, you’ve experienced the magic of staying comfortable on even the most frigid days. Now, you can treat your feet to the same hot-cocoa-in-bed coziness. Scoop these waterproof, eco-insulated gems up on Piperlime for $160. Aren’t the hot pink laces simply fabulous?

## 4. Ela Women’s Metallic Pull on Snow Boots

![Ela Women’s Metallic Pull on Snow Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/4_ela-womens-metallic-pull-on-snow-boots_272x376.jpg)

Pack on the metallic flair with stylish pull-on snow boots. For a wallet friendly $44.99 on Amazon, you can make a style statement. Pair them with leggings and a skirt for a casual winter look.

## 5. Sorel 88

![Sorel 88](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/5_sorel-88_240x240.jpg)

Just because your besties stick to black and grey doesn’t mean that you should! Unleash your quirky side with a pair of purple snow boots. They’re the same design Canadian competitors wore to Calgary’s 1988 Winter Olympics. How’s that for quality? They’re being sold on 6pm for $94.99 –a small price to pay for brightly hued shoes that'll last and last.

## 6. Kamik Snowfling

![Kamik Snowfling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/6_kamik-snowfling_240x240.jpg)

What’s waterproof, red, and warmer than your old leather boots? Kamik’s Snowfling, which you can buy on 6pm for a reasonable $59.99. They’d look ultra chic with black skinny jeans, a pea coat, and an oversized wool scarf.

## 7. Baffin Madison

![Baffin Madison](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/7_baffin-madison_240x240.jpg)

My final stylish snow boot pick is the Baffin Madison. For $99.99 on 6pm, you can have boots that look a heck of a lot like ones you’d lace up on any winter morning (snow or shine).

These are 7 of my best picks for stylish winter boots. Which ones would you rock? Do you have any suggestions to add?
