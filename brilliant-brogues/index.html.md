# 8 Brilliant Brogues ...

The popularity of androgynous and masculine dressing is here to stay, for a while at least, and so are original, brilliant brogues. An easy way to get in on the trend is to deck your feet out in a pair of them. Feminine brogues are generally slimmer in design and more flattering. Take a look at the selection of brilliant brogues below.

## 1. Asos Marky Patent Leather Traditional Brogue

![Asos Marky Patent Leather Traditional Brogue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/j1/tphwfs9k54c92948af899428244296_350x159.jpg)

**Price:** $62.76 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/7LDrl2)
Nude colours are still as popular as ever. These patent shoes are a great way to incorporate this colour into your wardrobe. The neutral nature of these shoes means that they can be paired with a range of outfits. These would look great paired with a calf-length skirt, fitted top, and floppy felt hat.

## 2. Topshop Murray Grey Chiffon Lace up Brogues

![Topshop Murray Grey Chiffon Lace up Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gk/q6/a0pdq06t54c9294985212788035255_350x176.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/X7ZwtR)
These brogues have quite a feminine design. The muted grey colour would be an ideal way of working the pastel trend this season. They feature grey chiffon laces and scallop detailing at the ankle, side, and toe. Pair these with a full-skirted dress and black patent accessories.

## 4. Dorothy Perkins Black and White Brogues

![Dorothy Perkins Black and White Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/vc/v46m4o4g54c9294a529db982230863_350x178.jpg)

**Price:** $51.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These two-tone shoes are a classic design. These brilliant brogues have a contrasting black and white body, with a black patent toe cap. These are a chic and sensible option for day to day wear. Wear these with shift dresses and cardigans to complete a retro inspired ensemble.

## 5. Miu Miu Crystal Embellished Glitter Finish Brogues

![Miu Miu Crystal Embellished Glitter Finish Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c5/qc/fzgbamqd54c9294b3ba4f783722786_350x173.jpg)

**Price:** $585.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UMZ2MG)
These brogues are sure to turn heads. These brilliant brogues feature a glitter-finish leather body and crystal-embellished heel. As for their design, they have a round toe, seam detailing, and lace-up front. These shoes would work best with a fairly minimal and neutral outfit.

## 6. Topshop Knave Tan Fringed Heeled Brogues

![Topshop Knave Tan Fringed Heeled Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xr/h4/aa5y5pqy54c9294c06d9d537561170_350x198.jpg)

**Price:** $105.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/EA8iHA)
These are a traditional, vintage looking pair of brogues. They have a slight heel and fringing detail on the front. They also feature subtle perforated detailing at the toe and ankle. Wear these shoes to complement a refined and preppy outfit.

## 7. Miss Selfridge Peter Cream and Tan Leather Classic Brogue

![Miss Selfridge Peter Cream and Tan Leather Classic Brogue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t5/jc/xkh1xdcm54c9294cbc24f019604998_342x235.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [missselfridge.com](https://goo.gl/hw7cjf)
These shoes are a fresh and modern take on the classic brogue design. The combination of tan and white leather creates a crisp appearance. The wide ribbon lace is also a nice touch. These girly brogues would be great for dressing up a printed dress.

## 8. Asos Marky Suede Traditional Brogues

![Asos Marky Suede Traditional Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mf/vv/ivlf5nlp54c9294db944e643583666_350x171.jpg)

**Price:** $62.76 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/ZVSejk)
These purple suede brogues are a guaranteed to add a dash of colour to your outfit. The suede material is a nice alternative to more commonly used leather. These brogues feature many classic design details such as a wing tip, lace up front, and perforated accents. Try your hand at colour blocking and pair these purple brogues with complementary colours.
