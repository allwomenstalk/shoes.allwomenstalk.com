# 8 Stylish Espadrilles for Spring and Summer ...

Stylish espadrilles are a great way to get into the swing of spring and summer. They’re an iconic choice of warm weather footwear and can be styled in all sorts of ways. Whether you opt for a breezy flat espadrille sandal or a more dressed up wedge heel, there’s something for everyone this season. Check out the following variety of stylish espadrilles for spring and summer.

## 1. Espadrille Wedge Sandals

![Espadrille Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/1_espadrille-wedge-sandals_328x264.jpg)

Stylish espadrilles like these wedge sandals are a popular choice. Depending on the colour and style, wedge espadrilles can look great teamed with anything from a flirty summer dress to tailored shorts and a tee. You can get these particular Lucky sandals for around $70 at Modcloth

## 2. Espadrille Sandals

![Espadrille Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/2_espadrille-sandals_600x290.jpg)

If high heels aren’t your cup of tea then opt for some flat espadrilles instead. They’re perfect for completing a casual summer look. If you don’t want to spend a fortune on styles by Charlotte Olympia or Michael Kors, check out ASOS and Topshop (pictured here) for more affordable buys.

## 3. Closed Toe Espadrille Wedges

![Closed Toe Espadrille Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/3_closed-toe-espadrille-wedges_600x389.jpg)

Closed toe espadrilles are another classic option. Wear them with shorts and a striped top for a really nautical inspired look. These $80 Topshop espadrilles come in a range of colours, including bright orange, and would be a fun edition to your spring/summer wardrobe. You can find similar styles of espadrilles from a range of sources from designers like Stella McCartney to online stores like ASOS.

## 4. Lace-up Espadrilles

![Lace-up Espadrilles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/4_lace-up-espadrilles_600x297.jpg)

For something a bit different, try out a pair of espadrille lace-up shoes. They can look just like your typical casual sneakers but with an espadrille-style sole. These ones are just under $30 at Forever 21 but you can get similar styles online at places like Land’s End. If you want something dressier than a sneaker, try brogue or oxford-style espadrilles.

## 5. Peep Toe Espadrilles

![Peep Toe Espadrilles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/5_peep-toe-espadrilles_600x200.jpg)

If you don’t like a noticeable espadrille wedge or sole, something subtle like these shoes might be more up your alley. These particular shoes will set you back around $60 from Topshop and have a classic peep toe design with a thin, flat espadrille sole. The fun lime colour would be great teamed with crisp white or faded denim clothes.

## 6. Espadrille Slippers

![Espadrille Slippers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/6_espadrille-slippers_600x273.jpg)

Espadrille slippers might not be everyone’s cup of tea but they seem to be pretty popular when it comes to espadrille shoe designs. These particular Christian Louboutin espadrilles are quite expensive at a little over $400 but you can find more affordable alternatives well under the $20 mark at ASOS.

## 7. Printed Espadrilles

![Printed Espadrilles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/7_printed-espadrilles_600x302.jpg)

Printed espadrilles would be a quirky way to finish off an outfit. When it comes to prints and patterns anything goes, with floral, geometric, and animal prints all popular choices right now. These Kate Spade espadrilles come in a classic animal print but are pretty pricey at nearly $200. Check out Juicy Couture, Soludos, and Dorothy Perkins for a range of cute printed espadrilles.

## 8. Patent Espadrille Wedges

![Patent Espadrille Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/8_patent-espadrille-wedges_600x563.jpg)

These espadrilles are a summery twist on the classic nude pump. They feature a patent nude closed-toe shoe with a light espadrille wedge heel. They’ll set you back around $55 at Dorothy Perkins and would look great with a range of smart, tailored outfits.

These are just a few styles of espadrilles that you should be able to find in stores right now. Are you a fan of the espadrille shoe? Which of these styles do you like best?
