# 8 Beautiful Oxford Booties ...

Oxford booties are cozy, trendy, and absolute must-haves for the city going girl. And with so many options to choose from, you’ll find your perfect pair in no time! I like to wear my oxford booties with opaque black tights, a flirty mini, and a put-together leather blazer. With these 8 pairs of beautiful oxford booties in your closet, you’ll be ready for anything.
