# 10 Tantalizing Tall Fall Boots ...

The sexiest accessories this season are tantalizing tall fall boots. Whether just at the knee or above, boots get teasingly taller for fall, which will have you braving the elements and looking chic too. These 10 tantalizing tall fall boots are sure to make heads turn ‘cause after all these boots were made for walking and that’s just what you’ll do, while you strut your stuff in these sleek styles.

## 1. Steven by Steve Madden Tall Wedge Boots

![Steven by Steve Madden Tall Wedge Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/08/1-madden-447x560_447x560.jpg)

**Price:** $272 at [bloomingdales.com](http://www1.bloomingdales.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A wedge boot is a staple when it comes to footwear fashion. Super comfortable and easy to walk in, a wedge boot is your go-to daytime boot especially if you’re a girl on the move. The foldover cuff detail on this pair of Steve Madden boots creates a sleek cool look that can be worn casually or dressier.

## 2. See by Chloé Leather Platform Knee Boots

![See by Chloé Leather Platform Knee Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/08/2_see-by-chlo-eacute-leather-platform-knee-boots_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $540 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/V0hKDk)
Clean lines and minimalistic design make this See by Chloé boot an elegant add to your fall wardrobe. The sandy leather colour makes this boot super wearable with a variety of colours and styles. A concealed platform gives the illusion of a smaller heel without sacrificing any of the height. Adding this pair to your daytime dressing will instantly give a polished sophisticated look.

## 3. Juicy Couture Morell Boot

**Price:** $428 at [juicycouture.com](http://www.juicycouture.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A polished leather boot in a statement colour like the trendy wine or oxblood is a fall must-have. If you’re not big on tall heel boots, this flat pick from Juicy Couture is an all day wearable option that can take you stylishly and effortlessly from day to night.

## 4. Rachel Zoe Carmen High Heel Boots

The angular seams and buckle embellishment make for a flawless structured look.

## 5. Rachel Roy Desirey Boot

A riding boot is a classic style that should be in every fashionista’s footwear collection. The black leather, strap detailing and buckle accents give this pair of tall flat riding boots an edgy moto vibe. These boots can take you from elegant equestrian to rock goddess in one go.

## 6. Hinge Cassi Tall Boot

![Hinge Cassi Tall Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/08/6_hinge-cassi-tall-boot_600x619.jpg)

## 7. Coconuts Pency Boot

![Coconuts Pency Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/08/7_coconuts-pency-boot_176x399.jpg)

## 8. Aldo Swam Tall Boots

![Aldo Swam Tall Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/08/26_swam_17_6-560x560_560x560.jpg)

## 9. Mee Too Latrice6 Tall Boot
