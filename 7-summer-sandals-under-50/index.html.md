# 7 Summer Sandals under 50 ...

Freshen up your summer footwear choices with some new sandals. The warmer summer months are a great way to show off some skin. Treat yourself to a pedicure and get barefoot ready. Below are just some stylish summer sandals under $50.

## 1. Topshop Hazel off White Floral Straw Sandal

![Topshop Hazel off White Floral Straw Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xa/pz/en5kcrfx54c8fab6560ff807065776_300x137.jpg)

**Price:** $45.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/JihFvH)
These woven straw-effect sandals are delightfully summery. They have a peep toe design, canvas ankle strap, and flower embroidery on the toe. The floral detail adds interest to an otherwise basic sandal design. Pair these with a floaty summer dress and a messy side braid.

## 2. Topshop Hoopa Bronze T-Bar Sandal

![Topshop Hoopa Bronze T-Bar Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zf/cn/cxeu551b54c8fab71177e607871318_300x150.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/DSWQdF)
Summer sandals under $50 can come in a range of designs. These metallic effect sandals would be great for an evening look. They are made from patent leather and have a T-bar design with an ankle strap fastening. These would look great with jeans and a sheer blouse for a casual evening look.

## 3. Modcloth Sea Dweller Sandal

![Modcloth Sea Dweller Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/ut/bkifl1rc54c8fab7cea3e561477862_300x126.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The black, red, and blue petal creations on the front of these shoes are designed to give the appearance of sea anemones. Personally, I think they could also double as flowers. These eye-catching sandals are created from man-made materials and feature an adjustable ankle strap. The prominent black colour makes these suitable for incorporating into a range of outfits.

## 4. Asos Flirt Flat Leather Sandals

**Price:** $44.83 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/QJWNxR)
Tan sandals are a great summer basic. This pair of summer sandals under $50 has the added touch of stud detailing. They also feature a cross-over design, toe post, and ankle strap fastening. Wear these with a pair of colourful tailored shorts and loose blouse.

## 5. Asos Foxy Leather Gladiator Sandal

**Price:** $35.86 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/LG2r9O)
Gladiator sandals have been doing the rounds for the past couple of years, but these pastel ones are a nice twist on the trend. They feature a T-bar design, loop-through straps, and a buckle fastening at the ankle. The pastel blue colour is on-trend for this season. Pair these with some camel coloured cuffed trousers for a casual daytime look.

## 6. Modcloth Painting Shells Sandals

![Modcloth Painting Shells Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yo/uz/ixjphv6t54c8faba247b8614546319_300x161.jpg)

**Price:** $24.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Add a bit of colour to your wardrobe with these spritely summer sandals under $50. The main feature of these sandals are the multi-coloured fringe tassels. These sandals have a flat sole with a textured tread, ankle strap fastening, and a T-bar design. These would work well with a full denim skirt and cotton tank top.

## 7. Forever21 Floral Canvas Sandals

![Forever21 Floral Canvas Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g1/za/svrsp3p554c8fabae6000769589513_300x186.jpg)

**Price:** $12.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Floral prints and summer go hand in hand. These canvas sandals are perfect for brightening up an outfit. They have a sling back design and small flat heel. These would look great paired with a range of items from sun dresses to denim shorts.

Summer sandals under $50 come in a range of colourful and quirky designs. The above sandals are just an example of the range that is available at the moment. Which of the above do you like best?
