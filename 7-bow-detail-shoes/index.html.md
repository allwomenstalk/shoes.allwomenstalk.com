# 7 Bow\-Detail Shoes ...

Bow details can be found on a range of fashion items. From headbands to blouses, there’s something for everyone. One of my personal favourite places for bow accents is on shoes. Bow-detail shoes are a great way of adding a bit of girlish charm to an outfit. Check out a few fun and flirty bow-detail shoes below.

## 1. Topshop Verve2 Black Chiffon Bow T-Bar Pumps

![Topshop Verve2 Black Chiffon Bow T-Bar Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/rw/rem55fbo54c8fabdd4eb8926955822_300x138.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/lL78XL)
These black shoes would make a great wardrobe staple. They feature a T-bar design with a buckle fastening at the ankles. Three delicate chiffon bows run down the top of the shoe. Basic black shoes can be incorporated into a range of different outfits, and these are no different. Style them in any way you wish!

## 2. Miss L Fire Expect Excellence Heel

**Price:** $145.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The vintage-inspired design of these bow-detail shoes is something I adore. They feature dotted cut-outs, zigzag trims, and large bow details at the toes. They also have a peep-toe design and ankle strap fastening. Pair these with a full knee-length skirt for a fun and feminine look.

## 3. Valentino Bow Embellished Patent Leather Pumps

![Valentino Bow Embellished Patent Leather Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ig/rd/tumqdo8054c8fabf72805426231262_300x226.jpg)

**Price:** $745.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/XXhN7K)
These Valentino pumps are about as elegant as they get. They have a large, voluminous bow embellishment at the toe and come in a delicate taupe colour. The patent leather is sleek and sophisticate, and the pointed heel is a classic touch. Wear these with trousers in the daytime and elegant cocktail dresses at night.

## 4. Asos Sugar Bow Court Shoe

![Asos Sugar Bow Court Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/v9/u8vjozjg54c8fac0d3142821138360_300x168.jpg)

**Price:** $80.69 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/wUU2oL)
These pink bow-detail shoes are a pastel pretty option for any wardrobe. They have a small block heel, textured fabric finish, and mock crocodile skin accents. The bow is situated on the front of the toe and has a layered design. These would look great with an outfit consisting of a silk blouse tucked into a high waisted pencil skirt.

## 5. See by Chloe Canvas Bow Detail Flat Sandal

![See by Chloe Canvas Bow Detail Flat Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/fr/u1vzluyh54c8fac196928566071758_300x160.jpg)

These sandals would make a fantastic casual footwear choice. They feature a multi-strap design, elasticised heel, and bow detail at the front of the ankle. These would make a sweet addition to any outfit. Pair them with slouchy pants and loose t-shirt for a casual summer look.

## 6. Topshop Muse Dark Rose Stud Bow Suede Pumps

![Topshop Muse Dark Rose Stud Bow Suede Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/hw/su5qcsbg54c8fac2589b4661881917_300x160.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/tFVist)

The dark rose colour is also a lovely choice. Pair these with jeans and a leather jacket to create a funky looking outfit.

## 7. Forever21 High Gloss Bow Front Flats
