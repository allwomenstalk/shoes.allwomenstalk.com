# 7 Sexy Silver Shoes for Those Nights out ...

**Silver shoes** can look quite glamorous for a night out. A silver shoe can complement a range of outfits, from that glam LBD to a colourful printed number. Silver heeled shoes also currently play into the futuristic trend that’s been all over the catwalks. Whether you’re after a timeless metallic sandal or something more daring, there are a range of silver shoes to suit your outfits.

## 1. Miss KG Glitter Court Shoe

![Miss KG Glitter Court Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/1_miss-kg-glitter-court-shoe_460x384.jpg)

**Price:** $94.00 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/eZ9WyU).
These silver shoes come in a classic design but feature a very on-trend glitter finish. They have a low cut vamp, pointed toe, and slim stiletto heel. These would do well to glam up any night time outfit. Team them with cropped cigarette style pants and a tucked in tank top.

## 2. Marc by Marc Jacobs Leather Wedge Sandals

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Leather Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/2_marc-by-marc-jacobs-leather-wedge-sandals_460x258.jpg)

**Price:** $260.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/O2sP6a)
If you’re after something a little more practical when it **comes to your silver shoes**, try a low wedge shoe. These silver mirrored leather sandals would inject some fashion-forward style to any outfit. Wear them on a night out with a short black shift dress and high voltage accessories.

## 3. Yves Saint Laurent Tribute Silver Shoes

![Yves Saint Laurent Tribute Silver Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/3_yves-saint-laurent-tribute-silver-shoes_460x400.jpg)

**Price:** $995.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9WlEP4)
The cult YSL Tribute shoe has been given a futuristic update with this stunning silver finish. They’re made from a high-shine silver leather and feature a knotted strap detail at the toe. Wear them with a fun printed dress or work a classic ‘Le Smoking’ inspired look by teaming them with a tux-style blazer and pants.

## 4. Miu Miu Sequined Leather Pumps

![Miu Miu Sequined Leather Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/4_miu-miu-sequined-leather-pumps_460x264.jpg)

**Price:** $890.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/U3wZrS)
These mid heel pumps come are quite feminine, what with their bow detailing and demure design. However, the silver glitter finish gives these shoes that extra dazzle. This style of shoe can still be quite sexy, especially teamed with a black party dress and embellished clutch.

## 5. Chinese Laundry Peep Toe Shoe

![Chinese Laundry Peep Toe Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/5_chinese-laundry-peep-toe-shoe_460x461.jpg)

**Price:** $68.04 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/nr0y3q).
**These platform silver heeled shoes** an overall embellished glitter finish. They also have a low cut vamp with peep toe styling. Team these shoes with a tangerine dress, which is this season’s must have colour.

## 6. Topshop Wattage Silver Metallic Wedges

![Topshop Wattage Silver Metallic Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/6_topshop-wattage-silver-metallic-wedges_460x474.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/GK53p9)
Afraid of heights? If you are, then maybe you should steer clear of these shoes. They have a high platform heel which will definitely give you some added height. Perfect for working a futuristic night time look, these silver platform shoes are for the fearless fashionistas.

## 7. Manolo Blahnik Strappy Silver Shoes

_ **Price:** $359.00 at barneys.com_
These silver heeled shoes feature intricate swirl strap detailing. They have an open instep, slim stiletto heel, and adjustable strap at the ankle. Manolo Blahnik shoes are much coveted, and these would make a great investment piece. These shoes would look great at night worn with a floaty sheer dress in a blush colour.
