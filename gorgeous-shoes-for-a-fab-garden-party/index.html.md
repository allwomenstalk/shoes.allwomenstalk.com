# 9 Gorgeous Shoes for a Fab Garden Party ...

Shoes for garden party events can be a tricky area to navigate around. The warmer weather usually means more time spent outdoors, and garden parties seem to be the fashionable places to be seen right now. When choosing shoes for garden parties you want a heel that will withstand uneven surfaces and not sink into grass, yet still look stylish and dainty. Take a look at a few stylish garden party shoes, below.

## 1. Draw the Line Wedge

![Draw the Line Wedge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/1_draw-the-line-wedge_320x232.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These low wedge heels tread in both neutral and colourful territory. They have a taupe ankle strap and wedge feel, but feature a striking coral strap at the toe. These shoes would look exceptionally stylish worn with a structured white sundress and similarly coloured accessories.

## 2. Hidden White Glitter Sandals

![Hidden White Glitter Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/2_hidden-white-glitter-sandals_600x334.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/LgUIW7)
If you prefer to wear flat shoes for garden party events, then take a look at this stylish pair. They have white leather look straps, metallic mirrored heels, and glitter detailing to the front. These are perfect for wearing underneath floaty maxi dresses.

## 3. Jancey Mid Heel Ballerinas

![Jancey Mid Heel Ballerinas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/3_jancey-mid-heel-ballerinas_600x343.jpg)

**Price:** $84.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/kiqk6P)
For those who prefer an enclosed shoe, why not try these mid heel ballerina pumps? They come in a summery apricot colour and feature a suede body with patent caps. These shoes are perfect for wearing with vintage inspired dresses.

## 4. Newark Mid Block Heel Sandals

![Newark Mid Block Heel Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/4_newark-mid-block-heel-sandals_600x420.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/jQchjR)
A tan pair of sandals is a versatile option. These leather sandals feature crossover straps at the front, buckle fastening ankle straps, and a low block heel. If you’re after more neutral and versatile shoes for garden party events then these are exactly that. They’d look great with anything from minimalist shift dresses to sheer maxis.

## 5. Seychelles Wedge in Caramel

![Seychelles Wedge in Caramel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/5_seychelles-wedge-in-caramel_320x298.jpg)

**Price:** $89.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These shoes are another take on the tan sandal. They feature a cork wedge heel and plaited straps at the toes. Wear these with full skirts or dresses and dainty accessories.

## 6. Weaver Raffia Platform Wedges

![Weaver Raffia Platform Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/6_weaver-raffia-platform-wedges_600x643.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/nZyXig)
The raffia detailing on these garden party shoes gives them an added textural element. They have a high platform heel with secure ankle fastenings. These shoes would look great worn with a light colour midi dress.

## 7. That’s so You Wedge

![That’s so You Wedge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/7_thats-so-you-wedge_320x288.jpg)

**Price:** $37.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Colourful shoes are the way to go right now. They’re a fun way to work some colour into your look. These shoes come in a rich purple hue and would look great worn with peach or white dresses.

## 8. River Island Tropical Print Wedges

![River Island Tropical Print Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/8_river-island-tropical-print-wedges_600x435.jpg)

**Price:** $58.00 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/8mYT4N)
It wouldn’t be a garden party without some floral adornments. Work your own unique angle by wearing a tropical floral print on your feet, a la these shoes. Since the print on these shoes is quite busy, stick to a block coloured outfit.

## 9. Jimmy Choo Patent-Leather Espadrilles

![Jimmy Choo Patent-Leather Espadrilles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/07/9_jimmy-choo-patent-leather-espadrilles_460x386.jpg)

**Price:** $395 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/un4i6E)
If you’re willing to wear designer shoes for garden party events then these are a chic option. They have a peep toe, slingback design and come in a classic nude patent finish. The wedge heel makes them garden party appropriate. Since they’re fairly neutral, they could be style in a range of ways.

When choosing suitable shoes for garden party events stick to shoes with low, block or wedge heels. Light and bright colours are ideal for summer, but then again, this will always depend on the rest of your outfit. Which of these garden party shoes do you like the most?
