# 9 Stylish Strappy Heeled Sandals ...

Strappy heeled sandals are perfect for working a dressed up look this spring-summer season. Skinny strapped shoes are right on-trend for now, but thicker straps are still fashionable choices. Basically, there’s a strappy heeled sandal to suit all styles. Get sorted this season with the following stylish strappy heeled sandals.

## 1. Topshop Metallic Heel Sandals

![Topshop Metallic Heel Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/1_topshop-metallic-heel-sandals_560x546.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ObeF0k)
The metallic heels on these strappy sandals are simply stunning. These strappy heeled sandals reflect the trend towards clean minimal designs. They have white leather straps at the toe and ankle, and feature rose gold clasp fastenings. Continue the trend towards minimalism by pairing these shoes with a monochrome shift dress and simple clutch bag.

## 2. Topshop Super Skinny Sandals

![Topshop Super Skinny Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/2_topshop-super-skinny-sandals_560x506.jpg)

**Price:** $94.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/3OS3oS)
Skinny strappy sandals are making a comeback this spring-summer. These silver and black sandals would be great for wearing out at night. Team them with cropped black trousers, a silk blouse, and embellished side bag for a chic night time look.

## 3. ASOS Stiletto Sandals

![ASOS Stiletto Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/3_asos-stiletto-sandals_560x617.jpg)

**Price:** $136.34 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/pXuwQo)
Strappy heeled sandals look great when worn in colour. These sandals come in a powdery blue colour and are ideal for teaming with this season’s pastel colour palette. They feature skinny straps at the toe and ankle, pin-buckle fastening, and a slim stiletto heel.

## 4. See by Chloe Leather Sandals

![See by Chloe Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/4_see-by-chloe-leather-sandals_460x485.jpg)

**Price:** $295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UwNghQ)
Thick, block heeled sandals seem to be a popular choice this season. These black leather sandals feature a wide strap at the toe, slim straps around the ankle, and a wooden block heel. Team them with an A-line skirt and a basic tank top for a cool daytime look.

## 5. Topshop Suede Strappy Heeled Sandals

![Topshop Suede Strappy Heeled Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/5_topshop-suede-strappy-heeled-sandals_560x588.jpg)

**Price:** $124.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ES6YIV)
Black heels are a must for any capsule wardrobe. These sandals take it to the next level, quite literally, thanks to their platform heel. They feature black suede straps and a covered platform, as well as an adjustable ankle fastening. These are a versatile heel that could be worn with a range of outfits.

## 6. Forever21 Glittered Platform Sandals

_ **Price:** $29.80 at forever21.com_
Glitter is still having a huge moment, and these platform sandals are a cheap and cheerful buy. They have a crossover strap design with a platform and thick heel. The light gold glitter finish is eye-catching but not overpowering. These would look great worn with a neutral party dress.

## 7. ASOS Hype Sandals

![ASOS Hype Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/7_asos-hype-sandals_560x449.jpg)

**Price:** $76.35 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/1MZmol)
These strappy heeled sandals have a fairly simple design. They have a peep-toe style strap at the front with a slim adjustable strap at the ankle. They come in a neon pink colour which is also very on trend for the season. These would look great worn with faded denim boyfriend jeans or a chic white dress.

## 8. Dorothy Perkins Nude Glitter Open Sandal

![Dorothy Perkins Nude Glitter Open Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/8_dorothy-perkins-nude-glitter-open-sandal_560x531.jpg)

**Price:** $69.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These pale glitter sandals feature a multitude of crossover straps. They have an open toe with adjustable buckle fastening. The mid heel makes these perfect for those not keen on sky high stilettos.

## 9. Jimmy Choo Mirrored Leather Sandals

_ **Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hF9HV0)
These stylish strappy heeled sandals have a classic slingback design. They have a mid stiletto heel, crossover straps at the toe, and come in a sleek metallic gold finish. This style of shoe is a must-have in any capsule wardrobe and will cater to most, if not all, of your evening looks.

A strappy heeled sandal looks great with a range of outfits. Metallics are great for the evening while neutral finishes are well suited to the day. Which of these strappy heeled sandals do you like best?
