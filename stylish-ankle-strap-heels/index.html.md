# 7 Stylish Ankle Strap Heels ...

**Ankle strap shoes** are a great solution for those who feel like they need extra support when it comes to their heels. I don’t know about you but sometimes I feel as if I’m falling out of my regular heels (although this could also be due to sizing issues!) Shoes with ankle straps can also look quite stylish. The ankle strap shoe looks great teamed with mini skirts and dresses and this season’s must have cropped trouser. Take a look at some stylish ankle strap shoes below.

## 1. See by Chloé Leather Sandals

![See by Chloé Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/1_see-by-chlo-eacute-leather-sandals_460x485.jpg)

**Price:** $295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UwNghQ)
Show off your new pedicure with these stylish **ankle strap shoes**. The minimalist design reflects current catwalk trends, and the somewhat dainty strap detailing is balanced out with a sturdy heel. These shoes are versatile enough to complement a range of warm weather looks.

## 2. Forever 21 Open Toe Platforms

_ **Price:** $26.80 at forever21.com_
These platform shoes are a more affordable and trend based option. They have a vintage inspired design and feature a thick platform heel. These shoes have a faux suede finish and come in either a neutral taupe colour or royal blue.

## 3. Miu Miu Washed Leather Platforms

![Miu Miu Washed Leather Platforms](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/3_miu-miu-washed-leather-platforms_460x434.jpg)

**Price:** $650.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/mlHTa0)
These **ankle strap shoes** have a more feminine design. They have a washed leather finish and feature a sweet half bow detail at the toes. The blue-grey colour of the leather is perfectly understated. These shoes would look great worn with a printed summer dress.

## 4. Topshop Suede Strappy Heels

![Topshop Suede Strappy Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/4_topshop-suede-strappy-heels_460x486.jpg)

**Price:** $124.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/AhLouU)
A pair of black heels is chic and timeless. These shoes would make a great go-to heel for all your dressing needs. They have a suede finish and feature crossover straps at the toes and a platform heel. Wear these with cropped trousers, a slinky tank top, and statement jewellery for a stylish night time look.

## 5. ASOS Ankle Strap Shoes

![ASOS Ankle Strap Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/5_asos-ankle-strap-shoes_460x484.jpg)

**Price:** $50.13 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/l4j8Jq).
Add some cuteness to your look with these polka dot platform sandals. They have a multi strap design with a butterfly style open toe. Balance out the chunky platform heel by teaming these **ankle strap shoes** with a floaty shirt dress.

## 6. Marc by Marc Jacobs Sandal

_ **Price:** $360.00 at barneys.com_
This **ankle strap shoe** features a stylish snake-embossed gold platform and heel. It also features three black leather straps and has an open toe design. The metallic details aren’t overpowering, but are still very on-trend. Team these heels with cropped tuxedo style pants and a silk blouse for fashionable evening outfit.

## 7. Christian Louboutin Leather Sandals

![Christian Louboutin Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/7_christian-louboutin-leather-sandals_460x450.jpg)

**Price:** $895.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AH4jbW)
These **ankle strap shoes** have quite a contemporary design. They are made from tan leather and feature the signature red sole. They also feature dark brown raised trims and white contrast stitching. These heels would do well to complement a range of this season’s hottest prints and colours.

Some people stay away from **ankle strap shoes** for fear that they can cut you off at the ankles and give the illusion of being shorter. The key, however, is to choose shoes with a delicate ankle strap. What do you think of the ankle strap shoe?
