# 7 Stylish Wellington Boots ...

Rainy weather calls for weather-appropriate footwear. A pair of rain boots, or wellington boots, is a useful thing to have. They can be worn in urban and country settings, and if you’re a seasoned festival goer then they might just be a life saver (just think of Glastonbury Festival and its history of mud). A well-made pair of wellington boots will last for years. Take a look at a few stylish wellington boots below.

## 1. Hunter Original Gloss Wellington Boots

![Hunter Original Gloss Wellington Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/is/tda2s1ql54c907085eaab813420062_245x397.jpg)

**Price:** $132.95 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/qvD0q0)
Hunter is probably the most iconic brand of wellington boots. As such, they set the bar when it comes to stylish wellington boots. These knee-high gloss boots take a cue from this season’s pastel trend. They have a cushioned footbed and come with an adjustable buckle fastening. These would look quite chic worn with floral dresses and light denim jackets.

## 2. Jimmy Choo Biker Wellington Boots

![Jimmy Choo Biker Wellington Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/mr/vjcrmkid54c9070923d12730860679_286x398.jpg)

**Price:** $425.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/vsrZQP)
The black rain boot is a timeless piece of footwear. These particular boots are a Jimmy Choo for Hunter design. These boots have biker-style silver buckle-fastening tabs and a designer stamped top. When the weather is looking worse for wear, slip these on instead of your knee-high boots. You’d hardly notice that they were wellies.

## 3. Joules Red Ribbon Wellies

![Joules Red Ribbon Wellies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nc/dp/ia4zexj754c9070a10a7d465483762_363x400.jpg)

**Price:** $52.00 at [topshop.com](https://goo.gl/aYIYhn)
These bright red wellington boots feature grosgrain ribbon detailing at the front. They are a shorter length booth and reach about mid-calf. The grosgrain ribbon ties add a touch of girlish charm to these boots. These bright wellington boots would make for some fun festival footwear.

## 4. Burberry Studded Wellington Boots

![Burberry Studded Wellington Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dv/e0/jer37m1m54c9070b0ec13719841061_288x397.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/CyBP3L)
These studded wellington boots are a little bit rock chick. They are part of the Burberry Winter Storms collection in which classic pieces are given a contemporary twist. These boots feature silver studs at the top and have Burberry’s signature check trim. The high gloss finish on these boots gives them a slick aesthetic.

## 5. Hunter Violet Short Gloss Wellies

![Hunter Violet Short Gloss Wellies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m3/ab/mnjrid3254c9070be3c48391971367_356x400.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Yc0nDb)
These stylish wellington boots come in a rich violet colour. They come in a shorter length and feature buckle detailing and a designer stamped top. The glossy finish is quite stylish. Work a wet weather look and pair these with skinny jeans, a striped top, and an all-weather jacket.

## 6. Hunter Black Carnaby Geometric Wellies

![Hunter Black Carnaby Geometric Wellies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/w9/mpjckx8l54c9070d05239297946532_267x392.jpg)

**Price:** $243.00 at [topshop.com](https://goo.gl/y786gd)
These black wellington boots have a wonderful geometric embossed finish. These knee-length boots are typical of the classic Hunter style. They have a sleek silhouette and feature an adjustable buckle and designer stamp at the top. These give the classic black wellington boot a contemporary twist.

## 7. Marc by Marc Jacobs Short Buckled Rubber Boots

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Short Buckled Rubber Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/ec/u09rvhwn54c9070de1609847383495_411x378.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/IusJaa)
If knee-length boots aren’t your style, then try these ankle-length wellington boots. They have a biker-inspired design and feature a buckle-fastening tab and chain embellishment. These stylish wellington boots are designed to simply slip on. These could easily be paired with a model-off-duty outfit of jeans and a leather jacket.

While some of these stylish wellington boots may seem like a bit of a splurge, they are worthwhile investment pieces. If you’re on a budget, keep an eye out for boots in similar styles. A glossy black boot is timeless. Which of these wellington boots do you prefer?
