# 7 Little Mermaid Inspired Shoes ...

If you’ve been pining for an oceanic adventure, these “Little Mermaid” inspired shoes are any land dweller’s first step. Though they won’t transform your feet into fins, they’ll make you absolutely irresistible to any merman. Channel every girl’s favorite sea siren with these 7 royal picks.

## 1. Nine West Flume

![Nine West Flume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_nine-west-flume_200x360.jpg)

Who said princesses had to play nice? These supple leather booties feature badass metal cap toe detailing and sky high heels, making the hefty $299 price tag worth it. I’d wear them with opaque black tights and a purple frock as a tribute to Ariel.

## 2. Glam Shell Bootie

![Glam Shell Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/2_glam-shell-bootie_600x738.jpg)

The beach may be off limits this winter, but you can still rock a pair of “Little Mermaid” inspired shoes. With these seashells on your feet, you’ll enchant any ocean-adoring prince. They’re available now on ModCloth for $264.99. I’d wear them again and again (and again!) with black skinny jeans and a leather blazer.

## 3. Seagoing My Way? Heel

Let him “sea” you in these stunners. Complete with metallic ocean creatures and a foot-friendly heel, I’d bet that Ariel is simply longing to strut a mile in these shoes! Pick them up for $179.99 on ModCloth and pair ‘em with your favorite gold party dress.

## 4. Barefoot Tess Shoes, Madrid Flats

![Barefoot Tess Shoes, Madrid Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/4_barefoot-tess-shoes-madrid-flats_561x313.jpg)

Just because your feet are longer than most doesn’t mean you can’t shine on in “Little Mermaid” inspired shoes. For $129.99 at Macy’s, you can add these fish scale flats to your closet. They run in sizes 11-15 and can be worn as a neutral.

## 5. Vince Camuto Ellen Ballet Flats

These cozy flats will add shine to any subdued ensemble. Best of all, they can easily be found on Zappos or Dillards for $79. And how adorable would they look with a little black dress or skinnies and a tee?

## 6. Lulu Townsend Ivan Pump

![Lulu Townsend Ivan Pump](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/6_lulu-townsend-ivan-pump_542x536.jpg)

These “Little Mermaid” inspired shoes are what Ariel would have worn had she been a twenty-something with a penchant for club music. These sexy shoes are $69.95 on DSW, so make that investment before those holiday house parties creep up on you. I’d wear this pair with a flowing black frock and a red lip.

## 7. Custom Hand Painted Little Mermaid Pumps

![Custom Hand Painted Little Mermaid Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/7_custom-hand-painted-little-mermaid-pumps_570x505.jpg)

Last but not least, here’s a pick for the fussy ladies out there who won’t settle for less than Ariel-adorned shoes. For $45, you can have a crafty gal on Etsy hand paint a pair of your favorite pumps. Ursula is purple with envy right now!

With these 7 “Little Mermaid” inspired shoes, you’ll be just as marvelous as any mermaid. Which pair is your favorite? Do you have any of your own options to add?
