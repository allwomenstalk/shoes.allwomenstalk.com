# 8 Shoes That Make Me Drool ...

Do you know what I love? I love shoes. I especially love shoes that make me have a reaction. I love shoes that are so awesome, I want them even though I wouldn't necessarily ever wear them. I classify this category as the shoes that make me drool, and I wanted to show you some examples of what I mean when I talk about footwear that can seriously make me salivate.

## 1. Isola Della Berry/Black Mary Janes

![Isola Della Berry/Black Mary Janes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hk/vs/fs2qryan54c8f7d78296c285555703_300x191.jpg)

**Price:** $129.99 at [zappos.com](https://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I am a huge fan of Mary Janes, so they frequently fall into the category of shoes that make me drool. I love these because they're two toned. I think that's incredibly stylish, and I love how they don't actually look like the standard Mary Jane. The big buckle is awesome, and I love the way the color blocking is styled, as well.

## 2. Suedette Platform Knee High Boots

![Suedette Platform Knee High Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/o2/e8t967w354c8f7d85cc0d661652950_300x345.jpg)

**Price:** $39.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Knee high boots always make me drool, no matter what. I don't even care that spring is finally teasing me, I still think these are awesome – and hey, there are still plenty of opportunities to wear boots in the spring. These are great because of the tops, that's where these boots really got to me. I just adore the styling up there, it's tres chic!

## 3. Poetic License Apple Pie Mary Jane Pumps

![Poetic License Apple Pie Mary Jane Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fm/z4/tjql45rq54c8f7d94b569907819096_300x263.jpg)

**Price:** $98.95 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Told you that Mary Janes are my favorites! I am craving bright colors this spring, and I think these sate that craving marvelously well. The pumps actually have a button that is shaped like an apple, which is just too cute – especially alongside all those precious polka dots. How could I possibly resist?

## 4. Amadeus Metallic Heels

![Amadeus Metallic Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dk/xj/gmxtjdjo54c8f7da2da79230261516_300x272.jpg)

**Price:** $121.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These are definitely shoes that make me drool. They are so bold! The shape is incredibly original and highly vintage, which is always awesome. I love the colors as well; the shoes look so iridescent, and that chunky heel is just tops in my book. The whole design on these shoes is just too much.

## 5. Romantic Soles Emitate Heels

![Romantic Soles Emitate Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/ax/mh76vr8d54c8f7daf2a91470881404_300x200.jpg)

**Price:** $39.00 at [zappos.com](https://www.zappos.com/romantic-soles-emitate?oosRedirected=true)
I love these shoes because of this color, a pearly burgundy that just makes me really happy. Aside of that, these aren't too ostentatious or anything, but that's all right. In my estimation, that gorgeous color makes them winners anyway – plus sometimes simple is just as drool-worthy as something complex, you know?

## 6. Leatherette Clogs

![Leatherette Clogs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c9/mp/dayojqjp54c8f7dbb7b1a428651865_300x253.jpg)

**Price:** $24.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I don't know what it is, but I've never quite gotten over the appeal of clogs. They will probably always be the kinds of shoes that make me drool. I think these are particularly delicious, with that high heel and gorgeous slope. Plus, platform clogs are about the only heels I can safely wear; otherwise I'm constantly afraid of falling on my face!

## 7. Wowie Zowie Heels

![Wowie Zowie Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/v1/s8rr3l7p54c8f7dca8149462310333_300x288.jpg)

**Price:** $117.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Oh, these are so delicious. The colors, the sparkles, the bow! I am so in love, I can't even stand it. Like Modcloth says, they really do look like a kaleidoscope! The primary turquoise shade is absolutely scrumptious, and I love the fact that the bow looks like hard candy. I would rock these heels so hard, I can't even tell you.

## 8. Promiscuous Ryan Heels

![Promiscuous Ryan Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/nq/rxqv5bhx54c8f7ddb2d2c239755719_300x265.jpg)

**Price:** $49.00 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love sparkles, can you tell? And this shade of pink – or fuchsia; whatever, Zappos, it's totally pink – always makes me drool, always. I've been watching too many SATC reruns on E!, I think; I want to be like Carrie on a budget, and wear shoes like this with my jeans. I wonder if I could actually pull it off...

The only problem I have is that, when I find shoes that make me drool, I want to buy them all. My bank account doesn't exactly love that. Neither does the Better Half, because my shoes take up the whole house. Does anyone else have this problem with shoe-session?
