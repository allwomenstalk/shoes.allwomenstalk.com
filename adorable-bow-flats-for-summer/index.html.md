# 7 Adorable Bow Flats for Summer ...

When the temperature gets balmy, I always rely on an adorable pair of bow flats to brighten up my look. They’re sugary sweet, highly feminine, and come in a variety of patterns and hues. So, if you’re looking to add a dose of girly chic to your wardrobe, here are 7 pairs of cute bow flats to keep your toes pretty all season long!

Whether you’re heading to a garden party or night on the town, these charming bow flats will polish any outfit. Not only are they crafted out of flexible PVC, these cuties are also watermelon scented. Yum! I’d proudly wear these kicks with my sleekest summer dress.

## 2. ModCloth Divine and Dandy Flat

![ModCloth Divine and Dandy Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/2_modcloth-divine-and-dandy-flat_300x168.jpg)

## 3. Dune Lavish Patent Bow Pointed Flat Shoes

![Dune Lavish Patent Bow Pointed Flat Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/3_dune-lavish-patent-bow-pointed-flat-shoes_300x124.jpg)

**Price:** $99.98 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/QuSdGH).
What’s better than one bow? How about three! With triple layers of bows on each shoe, these patent flats are my new summer staple. I’d wear these anywhere from the office to a restaurant. After all, nude shoes work with just about everything.

## 4. J. Crew Suede Ballet Flats with Bow

![J. Crew Suede Ballet Flats with Bow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/64817_BK0001-300x176_300x176.jpg)

**Price:** $148.00 at [jcrew.com](https://goo.gl/AqSeoh)
Comfy cushioned insoles coupled with luxurious Italian suede make these one pair of bow flats I can’t get enough of. Ladies, these are the perfect pre-vacation treat, especially if you’ve just booked a flight to Rome. I’d stow these in my purse for impromptu dates with foreign hotties.

## 5. Polka Dot Flats

_ **Price:** $16.80 at forever21.com_
Looking pretty in polka dots has never been easier or more fun. I especially love the straps; they’re perfect for keeping a fidgety foot in place. With this pair of adorable bow flats, you can make your usual cutoff shorts and tee duo look put together.

## 6. Target Women's Xhilaration Sandee Bow Flats in Pewter

_ **Price:** $19.99 at target.com_
Featuring flexible outsoles and a shimmering metallic finish, this pair of bow flats will make you shine this summer. Don these with a flirty baby doll dress or your hottest miniskirt for an attention grabbing look. I’d even rock them with my fiercest animal prints.

## 7. Dorothy Perkins Turquoise Snake Bow Pumps

![Dorothy Perkins Turquoise Snake Bow Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/7_dorothy-perkins-turquoise-snake-bow-pumps_300x131.jpg)
