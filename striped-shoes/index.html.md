# 7 Striped Shoes ...

Stripes are a staple in many a fashionista’s wardrobe. While most people wear stripes on their top half, why not think about adding some striped credentials to your feet? Striped shoes will add a nautical twist to an outfit. Take inspiration from a few striped shoes below.

## 1. ASOS Lagoon Stripe Bow Ballet Pump

![ASOS Lagoon Stripe Bow Ballet Pump](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mf/vy/glf78djg54c92bc54dbe9291844357_400x193.jpg)

**Price:** $43.10 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/zuZpXg)
The ballet shoe is timeless. These canvas ballet shoes come with an eye-catching red and white striped body. They also have contrasting trims and a white bow detail at the toe. Team these with navy trousers and a white tank top for a chic nautical-inspired look.

## 2. Carven Striped Canvas Pump

![Carven Striped Canvas Pump](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/u9/jgr8d7yk54c92bc63c013781129992_400x446.jpg)

**Price:** $416.50 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1kxKrg)
These striped shoes have a bold design. They have a black and white striped canvas body, and are complemented by a snake-effect heel. These shoes also have a concealed platform which adds to the exaggerated appearance of the toe. These shoes would make a graphic addition to any work outfit.

## 3. Modcloth Shoestring Licorice Flat in Cherry

![Modcloth Shoestring Licorice Flat in Cherry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/hp/db80kipy54c92bc7441fe416465052_400x180.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These striped shoes are quite quirky. They have a peep-toe design, cut out detailing at the top, and come in a fun red and white striped pattern. They also have matching red laces. Work a casual summer look and pair these with a loose t-shirt and denim shorts.

## 4. ASOS Mister Striped Lace up Brogue

![ASOS Mister Striped Lace up Brogue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ga/jt/f5drh1hd54c92bc82e0a8491760876_400x208.jpg)

**Price:** $60.34 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/xrZPfj)
These striped shoes come in a masculine-inspired brogue design. The body of the shoe features contrasting suede effect and striped panels. They have a rounded toe, lace up front, and low heel. Wear these with Peter Pan style dresses for a vintage-inspired look.

## 5. Topshop Raffia Multi Stripe Canvas Buckle Sandals

![Topshop Raffia Multi Stripe Canvas Buckle Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zp/ri/wp0kdhsh54c92bc91af3f893349849_400x386.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/AbHZv9)
Espadrille shoes are a summer favourite. These grey and white striped shoes feature multiple canvas stripes and a block espadrille heel. There are also buckle details to four of the straps. These would look great paired with crisp white trousers and a navy top.

## 6. Tabitha Simmons Dolly Striped Linen and Silk-Blend Flats

![Tabitha Simmons Dolly Striped Linen and Silk-Blend Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gp/hg/w1juqtp754c92bca353b0061039182_400x183.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/05KB9y)
These lace up flats are a casual summer shoe. They have a cream, red, and black striped body and espadrille trims at the sole. They also have a single lace-up front. Dress these shoes up and team them with some tailored shorts, singlet, and blazer.

## 7. Topshop Blue Striped Hi-Tops

![Topshop Blue Striped Hi-Tops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/yj/lnfuyo0x54c92bcb2e0fb840674874_400x257.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ti5uoD)
Hi-tops are a much loved style of shoe. The look is slightly retro but always relevant. These striped shoes have a fresh blue and white design. They also come with white laces and a white rubber sole. Try a colour blocked outfit and pair these shoes with some red shorts and an equally colourful top.

When wearing striped shoes, keep the rest of your outfit relatively basic. Avoid wearing loud or busy prints near the stripes. What are your thoughts on the striped shoe?
