# 7 Flatform Shoes ...

Somewhere in between your basic pair of flats and a platform wedge lies the flatform shoe. This style of shoe has been seen on many designer runways from Prada to Derek Lam. The ‘flatform’ is compatible with a range of different shoe style from sandals to boots. Check out the following flatform shoes to get a feel for the trend.

## 1. Vena Cava Tenoversix Platform Mary Jane

_ **Price:** $340.00 at openingceremony.us_
These flatform shoes come in a light off-white and tan colourway. This Mary Jane style shoe has an enclosed toe and buckle fastening at the ankle. It was a wooden heel and sole. This type of shoe would work well worn with a full knee-length skirt and loose-fitting tank top.

## 2. Bottega Veneta Leather Studded Sandals

![Bottega Veneta Leather Studded Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2011/05/flatshoes_500x377.jpg)

**Price:** $957.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)These designer studded sandals are a great way to add interest to an outfit. They have a wide front strap and ankle strap fastening. The high platform wedge features leather covered large studs. These flatform sandals could easily accent a daytime outfit of tailored shorts, a silky tank top, and slouchy hobo bag.

## 3. Chloe Leather and Canvas Platform Espadrilles

![Chloe Leather and Canvas Platform Espadrilles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m1/s2/hf3briac54c907433dab0394968917_350x203.jpg)

**Price:** $450.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/I55ZZZ)
These platform espadrille sandals would be great for wearing during the summer. They feature a taupe leather strap at the ankle and a canvas toe. These espadrilles have a round toe, gold flecks throughout, and leather-tipped laces. Wear these under floaty maxi dresses and with oversized sunglasses for a retro-inspired look.

## 4. Opening Ceremony Par Robert Clergerie Goud Patent Strap Sandal

_ **Price:** $765.00 at openingceremony.us_
These bright red flatform shoes really make an impact. These sandals have a slingback design with an added ankle strap fastening. They have a peep-toe opening and four straps along the front of the shoe. These shoes are also available in yellow. Pare these down with a utilitarian outfit of skinny jeans and a blouse, or play them up with a 60s shift dress and geometric prints.

## 5. Belle Sigerson Morrison Wooden Wedge Sandals

![Belle Sigerson Morrison Wooden Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/di/w2e5w1cl54c907451a2c4229513173_350x327.jpg)

**Price:** $295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/aZ7wDL)
These wooden wedge sandals are a street-smart option. These sandals have cross over straps at the toe and an elasticised wrap-around ankle strap. These platform shoes have a slight heel to them, but still maintain a fairly ‘flatform’ shape. Wear these with tailored shorts and a slouchy tee for a chic everyday look.

## 6. Cacharel High Platform Wedge

_ **Price:** $645.00 at barneys.com_
These high platform wedge shoes come in a neutral colourway. They feature suede straps and a wooden wedge. There is a solid strap at the toe and a buckle-fastening strap at the ankle. Team these wedges with a sheer pleated skirt and scoop-back top for a contemporary ballet-inspired look.

## 7. Michael Kors Crocodile-Effect Leather Platform Sandals

![Michael Kors Crocodile-Effect Leather Platform Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zc/jt/ddw1pccf54c9074707732006323327_350x243.jpg)

**Price:** $750.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UncVIh)
These flatform shoes have dark brown crocodile-effect leather straps. These shoes have a rounded toe, ridged rubber sole, and buckle-fastening ankle straps. The taupe suede heel measures approximately 2 inches. These shoes would look great with slim-fitting cropped khakis and a silk blouse.

Admittedly, this trend isn’t to everyone’s tastes. I’m not sure how long this trend will be sticking around for either. That said, it certainly is a quirky and unusual footwear style. What do you think of these flatform shoes?
