# 8 Cozy Winter Loafers ...

With these cozy winter loafers on your feet, you’ll stay stylish and toasty --even when the weather outside is frightful. Whether you favor skinny jeans or skirts and leggings, you can easily work a pair (or two!) of fabulous winter loafers into your wardrobe. Check out 8 of my favorite picks ahead!

## 1. DKNY Aralyn Ballerina Moc

![DKNY Aralyn Ballerina Moc](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/1_dkny-aralyn-ballerina-moc_480x200.jpg)

These winter loafers will never have you doubting a good pair of blue suede shoes again! Complete with decorative tassels and an ultra trendy stacked heel, you’ll be the coolest chick on campus. I’d pair ‘em with skinny jeans and a tee for class or a skirt and blazer for an afternoon at the internship. You can find these now for $98 on Zappos.

## 2. Roxy Lido Wool

![Roxy Lido Wool](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/2_roxy-lido-wool_464x184.jpg)

[zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Wool shoes will get your toes through all that wintry weather. Whether you’re dealing with cold floors or frigid sidewalks, these loafers’ wool uppers and faux fur lining will pack on the coziness. I’d wear them with sweats or jeans to run errands. They’re available now for $44 on Zappos.

## 3. Minnetonka Thunderbird II

![Minnetonka Thunderbird II](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/3_minnetonka-thunderbird-ii_464x256.jpg)

Vintage-inspired and oh-so-warm, Minnetonka’s Thunderbird II moccasins will soon be staples in your winter wardrobe. Their faux suede linings will wick moisture away, keeping those toes toasty and dry. I’d wear them with everything from my favorite casual denim skirt to skinny jeans. Plus, the oxblood hue is a huge hit this season! Get them now for $39.95 on Zappos.

## 4. ALDO Ostlie

![ALDO Ostlie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/4_aldo-ostlie_480x232.jpg)

Meow! Add a dose of fierce to your cold weather wardrobe with leopard print loafers. They’d be perfect for a night out when worn with black skinny jeans, a form-fitting cami, and a sleek leather blazer. They’re available now for $55.99 on Zappos.

## 5. DV by Dolce Vita Faustine

![DV by Dolce Vita Faustine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/5_dv-by-dolce-vita-faustine_464x224.jpg)

Whoever said black loafers were boring never met these studs! Complete with badass spiked heels, this pair will add notice-me edge to any ensemble. I’d wear them with a leather skirt, brightly hued opaque tights, and a v-neck tee for a look that really makes an impression. Get them now for $70.99 on Zappos.

## 6. Tommy Hilfiger Perky

![Tommy Hilfiger Perky](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/6_tommy-hilfiger-perky_480x264.jpg)

These plaid loafers will perfect your preppy look. Their decorative tassel accents add flirty playfulness to the school girl chic patterning. I’d wear them with a pleated skirt and satchel bag. They’re available now for $54.99 on Zappos.

## 7. DV by Dolce Vita Geri

![DV by Dolce Vita Geri](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/7_dv-by-dolce-vita-geri_464x208.jpg)

For another sleek pair to wear anywhere, check out Dolce Vita’s Geri loafers. With their rivet details and cozy cushioned footbeds, you’ll look like a city slicker no matter where you live! I’d wear them on date night with skintight jeans and a patterned, curve-hugging sweater. Find them now for $79 on Zappos.

## 8. Madden Girl Aceee

![Madden Girl Aceee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/8_madden-girl-aceee_440x224.jpg)

Add sparkle and shine to your usual getups with Madden Girl’s fabulous flats. Wear them with any hue –just think of the gleaming gold color as another neutral. They’d look gorgeous with a daytime dress and a gold headband to match. Get them now for $39.99 on Zappos.

These 8 pairs of winter loafers will add style to your step throughout the season. They’ll look just as chic as those lace-up booties you already own –put they’re so much easier to slip on and off! Which ones are your favorites? Do you have any of your own styles or ideas to add?
