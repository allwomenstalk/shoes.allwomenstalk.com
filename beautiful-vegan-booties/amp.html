7 Beautiful Vegan Booties ...

Promise Abigail Wine Red Studded Motorcycle Ankle Boots • Future is Bold Bootie • Blowfish Lady Natural Sandblasted Canvas • Sueded Side-Knot Wedge Bootie • Dollhouse Slammin Black Strapped and Studded Platform Booties • More ...

By Diana

Ethical fashion fans, take note: These vegan booties are an animal-friendly way to spice up your cold weather wardrobe. With 7 trendy options to choose from, your feet will feel fabulous. And at these wallet-friendly prices, who said you had to settle for just one pair of vegan booties?

1. Promise Abigail Wine Red Studded Motorcycle Ankle Boots

Crafted out of vegan suede, these motorcycle booties will keep you looking badass (without harming your animal friends). Complete with silver studs, I’d pair these beautiful vegan booties with skinny jeans and a faux leather jacket for a daring look that’ll leave him speechless. Pick ‘em up for $48 at Lulus.

2. Future is Bold Bootie

These faux-crocodile vegan booties are edgy yet office appropriate. Tone down your look with opaque black tights, a pencil skirt, and a crisp white button down. They’re available now for $76.99 on ModCloth.

3. Blowfish Lady Natural Sandblasted Canvas

Paired with thick woolen socks, you can rock these canvas booties through winter. Then, swap your socks and jeans for tights in spring. This go-anywhere pick is both cozy and chic. And for $49 on Planet Shoes, the price is oh-so-right.

4. Sueded Side-Knot Wedge Bootie

Who said vegan booties couldn’t be tres chic? This fabulous Charlotte Russe pair features a heightening wedge and stylish side knot. Priced at $35.50, I’m planning to add these to my footwear arsenal right away. I’d wear them with opaque tights and a chambray dress.

5. Dollhouse Slammin Black Strapped and Studded Platform Booties

Ladies, meet your new club shoes. With a 5.5” stiletto heel, you’ll look model-tall and effortlessly fierce. These and similar styles can be found on Lulus. The pair you see here sells for $52, leaving you more than enough cash for a cab home.

6. Head to Your Taos Boot

These vegan booties are so southwestern chic. Complete with a hidden wedge and woven embroidery, they’re a unique way to update those old denim tops and dress shorts. Find them on ModCloth for $134.99.

7. Perforated Lace-up Heel Bootie

Go gorgeous this winter with heeled lace-up booties. I love the stunning blue hue –it’ll make your all-black ensembles pop. Find this pair for $50 on Charlotte Russe, along with a selection of heeled booties you’ll drool over this season.

With these 7 options to choose from, you’ll have the coolest feet on the street. Which pairs do you love? Do you have any of your personal favorites to add to the list?
