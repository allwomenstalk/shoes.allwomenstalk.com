# 8 Hottest Studded Boots ...

Studded boots are a great way to work the biker-chic trend. Ever since designer label Chloé introduced the “Susan” studded ankle boot a few years ago, the trend has taken off. That particular shoe sold out almost immediately and spawned a myriad of knock-offs. Nowadays, stud embellishments can be found on a range of different boot designs. Below are a few of the most current and hottest studded boots around.

## 1. Fiorentini & Baker Studded Suede Ankle Boots

![Fiorentini & Baker Studded Suede Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/np/r4huy2nd54c8faad77d7a137116217_300x297.jpg)

**Price:** $640.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/PDSDcu)
These are my picks for being some of the hottest studded boots around. The design is edgy yet understated. The cool sand colour is a great alternative to the more ubiquitous black boot. These boots feature bronze studs around the sides, a stacked wooden heel, and zip fastening at the inner ankle. Pair with slim fitting jeans and slouchy jackets for a street smart look.

## 2. Sam Edelman Stud Platform Ankle Boot

![Sam Edelman Stud Platform Ankle Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/id/jcgb6ziv54c8faae6826b395344804_300x349.jpg)

**Price:** $222.33 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/LurjCC)
These vampish boots are perfect for finishing off a disco-ready outfit. The simple all black design is offset by a stud embellished platform. These boots feature an almond toe, side zip closure, and cone heel. These would look with worn with a fun party dresses.

## 3. Forever21 Cowgirl Studded Boot

**Price:** $17.99 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These inspired boots take a cue from the hottest studded boots around – the Chloé “Susan”. There are similarities in the shape and the multiple strap design. However, these particular boots only feature studding on the straps and are made from a soft suedette material. Wear these boots with floaty cream dresses and distressed denim jackets for a cowgirl-inspired look.

## 4. Camilla Skovgaard Suede and Leather Platform Ankle Boots

![Camilla Skovgaard Suede and Leather Platform Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i7/nl/js9oogac54c8fab01612a425951927_300x389.jpg)

**Price:** $570.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ZlPMZZ)
Camilla Skovgaard is revered for her fashion-forward shoe designs. These gravity defying boots are made from taupe suede and leather. They also feature a rubber island platform, open toe, high-cut cuff, and subtle silver studs at the platform. These sculpturally styled shoes are statement makers, so it’s best to wear these with a fairly basic outfit.

## 5. Bertie Baxter Western Stud Boot

![Bertie Baxter Western Stud Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mt/on/kzmnin8c54c8fab0f10e8225940342_300x312.jpg)

**Price:** $98.62 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/Ttk0kF)
The black heeled boot is somewhat of a wardrobe staple. This boot features a subtle western-inspired cowboy design, a chunky heel, and stud detailing. The studs appear on a curved design to the front of the boot and at the top pull tabs. Pair these boots with a colourful mini dress and opaque tights for a fresh night-time look.

## 6. Modcloth Press Pass Boot

**Price:** $24.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The foldover cuffs on these boots are what really make them stand out. They are made from a faux suede material and feature a wooden stacked heel, rounded toe, and brass side zipper. When putting together your outfit, it’s important to balance out the shape of the shoe. Also, create a longer streamlined silhouette by wearing a dark pair of opaque tights.

## 7. Forever21 Studded Ankle Boots

![Forever21 Studded Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pf/ya/n51hvk6854c8fab2b1045727472428_300x210.jpg)

**Price:** $29.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These taupe ankle boots feature unexpected stud embellishments on the toe caps. They are a demure and feminine take on the studded trend. The design is simple and flattering, which makes them contenders for being some of the hottest studded boots around. Pair these with a sheer pleated skirt and fitted tank top.

## 8. Modcloth do You Believe in Magenta? Boot

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These bright reddish-pink booties are incredibly stylish. They have a velvety texture and feature a fold-over cuff design, cone heel, and inside zipper. The silver stud embellishments on the cuff are a stand out. Pair these with a black leather skirt and floaty blouse for a chic, modern look.

There’s a pair of studded boots to suit all tastes. Which of the above hottest studded boots are your favourites?
