# 7 Colour Block Shoes ...

Try working the colour blocking trend with a pair of multicoloured shoes. Summer is a time to experiment with colour and be a bit frivolous when it comes to dressing. If you usually wear fairly neutral outfits, then colourful shoes are a great way of livening them up. Put your most colourful foot forward in these colour block shoes.

## 1. Topshop Sizzle Pink Suede Colour Block Platform Sandals

![Topshop Sizzle Pink Suede Colour Block Platform Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2011/06/color-block_500x360.jpg)

**Price:** $136.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8u41k6)
These colour block shoes are a stand out. They feature a combination of vibrant coral and yellow suede leather. These shoes have a peep-toe design and platform heel. These shoes would easily complement an outfit of jeans and a basic t-shirt.

## 2. ASOS Heist Bow Platform High Sandals

![ASOS Heist Bow Platform High Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/vl/hzaklmnp54c926848dacf406273275_377x389.jpg)

**Price:** $84.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/Fulv4R)
These platform sandals have a sweet and playful design. They consist of a red suede leather body and pink platform and bow detail. They have buckle fastening ankle strap and chunky block heel. Wear these shoes to complete a ladylike outfit of white mini skirt and button-up blouse.

## 3. Sonia by Sonia Rykiel Dulchinee Strappy Wedge Sandals

![Sonia by Sonia Rykiel Dulchinee Strappy Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/r6/yh1p93zr54c92685a7815765484144_400x298.jpg)

**Price:** $471.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/JmyDyN)
The wedge sandal may not be to everyone’s tastes. These sandals have a certain colour blocking aspect to them. They feature wide red straps at the toe and yellow buckle fastenings and trims. The neutral taupe ankle strap and black heel anchors the entire shoe. These wedge sandals would look great paired with an asymmetric black dress and patent accessories.

## 4. K Jacques Pour Opening Ceremony Barigoule 2-Strap Flat Sandal

_ **Price:** $235.00 at openingceremony.us_
These sandals are a subtle way of working the colour blocking trend. If bright colours aren’t your thing then try a monochrome palette. They feature two straps across the top of the foot and a slingback strap. These basic yet stylish sandals are a great choice of summer footwear.

## 5. ASOS Mind Suede Colour Blocked Lace up Flat Shoe

![ASOS Mind Suede Colour Blocked Lace up Flat Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/yk/nu45ls9q54c926879a0da094160125_400x184.jpg)

**Price:** $44.32 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/p0FPdw)
Pastel colours are still a popular choice, and these colour block shoes are a great way to work the trend. These suede lace-up shoes have a slim sole, round toe, and low heel. The body of the shoe consists of pastel blue, pink, and grey panels. The pastel colours also add a feminine touch to a somewhat menswear inspired design. Team these with a pair of cream pegged trousers and neutral sleeveless top.

## 6. Topshop Winston Colour Block Espadrille Wedges

![Topshop Winston Colour Block Espadrille Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2011/06/espadrille_500x333.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/7fmZWi)
The espadrille is a summer staple. These espadrille wedge sandals come in a spritely colour blocked design. They have orange peep-toe and pink ankle strap. The ankle strap also features a metal buckle fastening. Wear these espadrilles under floaty maxi dresses and with floppy straw hats.

## 7. Dune Gizella Multi Coloured Platform Sandals

![Dune Gizella Multi Coloured Platform Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/b9/p1x59n0954c926888b449202473940_386x400.jpg)

**Price:** $166.19 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/V6wO6A)
These suede colour block shoes feature multiple cross over straps. The red and pink straps intertwine and form at a double buckle fastening at the ankle. The high block heel and chunky platform sole adds extra height to the design. Pair these shoes with your favourite black party dress for a fun nighttime look.

Some designs may seem a bit more out-of-the-ordinary than your everyday shoe. If the brighter hues are not to your tastes you can always experiment with pastels or muted tones. Which, if any, of these colour block shoes do you like best?
