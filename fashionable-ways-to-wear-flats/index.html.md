# 8 Fashionable Ways to Wear Flats ...

There are tons of **ways to wear flats** with every outfit! Whether you are wearing your little black dress or wearing skinny jeans, the ways to wear flats are completely endless! I've actually got the scoop on all of the different ways to wear flats to make any outfit of yours awesome.

## 1. Skinny Jeans

Skinny jeans are the perfect jeans to wear with any flats that you have. Whether you are tucking these jeans into your flat boots or you are thinking about pairing them with some ballet flats. Skinny jeans look fantastic with any flat shoes you put them with!

## 2. Bubble Skirts

One of the greatest **ways to wear flats** is actually by pairing them with a kick butt bubble skirt. I personally love bubble skirts. I think that they are so friggin' cute, look great on any body type and are ideal to wear with flats!

## 3. Jeggings

Although jeggings are similar to skinny jeans, I actually think that jeggings look super cute with any and all flats! For me, I always pair my jeggings with my pink ballet flats. They look chic, totally stylish and add in a pop of color!

## 4. Coordinate Colors

Speaking of colors! One of the greatest **ways to wear flats** is to actually coordinate the colors of your flats with say your skirt or maybe even your purse. That way you can tie in your shoes with your entire outfit! It's chic!

## 5. Little Black Dress

For me, I like my patent leather flats to be paired with my LBD. It adds in a pop of shimmer and glow and they look awesome! You could also add in a pop of color and wear bright blue flats with your LBD!

## 6. Sun Dresses

Spring time is right around the corner right? What do you wear in the spring time? Sun dresses! One of the greatest **ways to wear flats** is to pair them with your favorite sun dress! You could add in a colored flat or you could even do a pattern to add some spice!

## 7. Trouser Pants

I actually do this all the time at the office. You don't have to wear those uncomfortable pumps all the time, you can actually wear your flats with your trouser pants! Just pick out flats that are on the professional side. Leather is always a good option!

## 8. Pencil Skirts

Finally, one of the last **ways to wear flats** is with your pencil skirts! This is again, a great way to incorporate your flat shoes into your your style at the office! Again ladies, make sure that you get something in leather!

As you can see, there are tons of different **ways to wear flats**! All you need is a little imagination and a ton of creativity to be stylish! I love all of these different ways to wear flats and I've incorporated all of them into my style. So ladies, what are some of the creative ways that you wear your flats?
