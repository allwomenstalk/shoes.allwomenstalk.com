# 7 Tasselled Shoes ...

Loafers, oxford-styles and other tasselled shoes are becoming an increasingly popular style of shoe. Thanks in part to a surge in preppy and conservative styles of dressing. A common design detail of these shoes is the tassel. These days, an abundance of shoes feature tassel accents, incorporated in a range of designs. Below are just a few tasselled shoes to take inspiration from.

## 1. Jeffrey Campbell Wingtip Flats

![Jeffrey Campbell Wingtip Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w8/os/de2ncv8k54c92cb967a0c180982181_300x173.jpg)

**Price:** $104.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These peach and white loafers are fresh and colourful – perfect for spring. These shoes feature classic brogue detailing, fringing, and tassels at the front. The design is timeless but with a youthful twist. Personally, I think these shoes would look great with anything and everything!

## 2. Lillybee Brown Tassel Flats

_ **Price:** $168.00 at simplysoles.com_
The ballet-style design makes this shoe a great option for your everyday wardrobe. The tan leather would work well with a range of outfits. The tassels on the front of each shoe create just the right amount of detail. They’d look effortlessly chic paired with jeans and a striped tee.

## 3. Topshop Graze Navy Suede Tassel Heel Loafers

![Topshop Graze Navy Suede Tassel Heel Loafers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ct/qz/exllra3e54c92cbb19ef1901968941_300x235.jpg)

**Price:** $128.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/C8TDMu)
These tasselled shoes take the loafer trend to the next level, quite literally! The high crepe heel adds to the appearance of a more sophisticated shoe. The navy suede is a material that you wouldn’t normally expect, but one that works quite well with the overall design. These would work great with a range of more polished outfits. Think shift dresses and cardigans or [high waisted skirts](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-to-love-high-waisted-skirts/) and blazers.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Canvas and Leather Brogue Bootie

**Price:** $395.00 at [bergdorfgoodman.com](http://www.bergdorfgoodman.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I’m fairly confident that these shoes will not be to everyone’s tastes. Personally, I can’t take my eyes off of them. Something about the design is so compelling. The combination of white, black and peach leather is quite complimentary. These tasseled shoes combine the oxford and ankle boot trends to create something quite unique.

## 5. Steven Mirandda Cognac Leather Loafers

**
Price:** $73.00 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These slip on shoes incorporate the tassel trend quite well. As well as the tassel accents, they feature perforated and top stitch details. The rich leather material is a great choice for the body of the shoe. These would make a preppy addition to any outfit.

## 6. Jeffrey Campbell Tassel Platform Heels

![Jeffrey Campbell Tassel Platform Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/aa/mxo6h81j54c92cbcb9234045931145_280x300.jpg)

**Price:** $144.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These are definitely a more modern and trend-based interpretation of the tasselled shoe. These platform heels are equal parts feminine, preppy, and edgy - if that’s possible in a shoe. They feature fringing and tassel details, as well as an adjustable ankle strap and buckle. Pair these with a printed dress and some opaque tights.

## 7. Asos Madge Leather Tassel and Bow Loafer

![Asos Madge Leather Tassel and Bow Loafer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gq/cv/mo0rxt8w54c92cbd9cb4a475622060_300x130.jpg)

**Price:** $62.76 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/YIi3rW)
Tasselled shoes don’t get much more traditional than this. The design of this shoe is timeless. Despite usually being a more masculine style of shoe, this design is slim and quite feminine. Wear these with full skirted dresses and cardigans. These would be a great shoe to have on high rotation in your wardrobe.

There are a range of different tasselled shoes available. They can range from platform heels to traditional flat loafers. Which of the above designs are your favourites?
