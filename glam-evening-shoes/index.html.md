# 7 Glam Evening Shoes ...

**Evening Shoes** can really make or break an outfit. Evening shoes are just a little bit more ‘special’ than your everyday shoes. They’re a little bit dressier, a bit more glamorous. Metallics are a firm favourite, but glitter finishes and coloured leather are also on-trend for the season. Get inspired by these glam evening shoes below.

## 1. Modcloth Sparkle Heels

![Modcloth Sparkle Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/01/1_modcloth-sparkle-heels_300x293.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Nothing says evening wear than some sparkle. Glitter shoes are still as popular as ever and these heels are a chic way to wear the look at night. The simple, enclosed style of shoe is a perfect canvas for the pale gold glitter finish. These would look great worn with cropped tuxedo style pants.

## 2. Jimmy Choo Lamé Covered Sandals

![Jimmy Choo Lamé Covered Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/01/2_jimmy-choo-lam-eacute-covered-sandals_300x352.jpg)

**Price:** $1095.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Xv6IPL)
Another take on the metallic and glitter trend, these designer sandals feature a thick platform and staggeringly high heel. They feature a twisted front strap detail and slim ankle strap. Wear these **evening shoes** with a sheer pleated midi skirt for a feminine look.

## 3. Topshop Game High Pointed Court Shoes

![Topshop Game High Pointed Court Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/01/3_topshop-game-high-pointed-court-shoes_300x299.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/KV4bq5)
Metallic seems to be the buzz word when it comes to accessorising. These stylish court shoes come in a wonderful rose gold finish. They have a pointed toe and slim stiletto heel. Wear these with a full-skirted cream dress and similar metallic accessories for a chic night time look.

## 4. River Island Plait Trim Platform Stilettos

![River Island Plait Trim Platform Stilettos](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/01/4_river-island-plait-trim-platform-stilettos_300x331.jpg)

**Price:** $113.54 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/Gu0ase)
Add a pop of colour to your evening outfit by adding a pair of red **evening shoes**. These strappy sandals feature gold metallic plait detailing, and have a gold-tone zip fastening at the back of the ankle. These heels would look great worn with some silk trousers, a loose singlet, and crisp blazer.

## 5. Charlotte Olympia Metallic Leather Slingback Pumps

![Charlotte Olympia Metallic Leather Slingback Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/01/5_charlotte-olympia-metallic-leather-slingback-pumps_300x341.jpg)

**Price:** $905.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/BAfwxT)
These slingback heels are the perfect combination of contemporary and classic styling. The round toe and slingback strap are quite a classic design, while the metallic platform is a not to modern trends. Wear these **evening shoes** with a floaty cream dress and collar necklace for an on-trend look.

## 6. Carvela Glitter Pointed Court Shoes

![Carvela Glitter Pointed Court Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/01/6_carvela-glitter-pointed-court-shoes_300x277.jpg)

**Price:** $215.86 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/D9qybM)
Are you sick and tired of glitter shoes yet? Well, you better be prepared because they’re here to stay for a while yet. These pointed court shoes feature a heavier glitter finish, which gives them a bit more edge than the other ones already mentioned on this list. These would look great teamed with a more edgy black dress.

## 7. Modcloth Rhinestone Heels

![Modcloth Rhinestone Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/01/7_modcloth-rhinestone-heels_300x336.jpg)

**Price:** $84.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These strappy **evening shoes** are perfect if you’re trying to recreate a more vintage look. They have a T-bar design with intricate strap details at the toe. They also feature rhinestone embellishments throughout. Show these shoes off by wearing them with an asymmetrical hem.

The type of **evening shoes** you wear really depends on the outfit and occasion. Stick to basic styles of evening shoes if you want something versatile. Which of these evening shoes do you like best?
