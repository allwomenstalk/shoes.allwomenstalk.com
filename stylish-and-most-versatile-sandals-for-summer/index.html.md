# 9 Stylish and Most Versatile Sandals for Summer ...

**Sandals for summer** are a staple of any wardrobe. They’re an easy footwear option and perfect for showing off your [summer pedicure](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/fun-summer-pedicure-ideas-to-make-your-feet-stand-out/). When looking for a sandal for summer, you want something that’s going to be versatile and that will be easily incorporated into several outfits. For this reason, I tend to stick to sandals in black, brown, or metallic colours. Take a look at some stylish summer sandals below.

## 1. Topshop ‘Hoopla’ Leather Strap Sandals

![Topshop ‘Hoopla’ Leather Strap Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/1_topshop-hoopla-leather-strap-sandals_500x236.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/7XUJi1)
You can’t go past a simple leather sandal for versatility. **These black summer sandals** have a simple design, with a wide strap at the toe and thinner straps at the ankles. I’d wear these with anything and everything this summer. Lucky they’re also available in tan!

## 2. Topshop ‘Florida’ Simple Buckle Sandals

![Topshop ‘Florida’ Simple Buckle Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/2_topshop-florida-simple-buckle-sandals_500x281.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/kET6yB)
These sandals feature a crossover straps and buckle detailing. They also have a low wood-effect heel. These tan buckle sandals would look great worn under floaty maxi skirts, palazzo pants, with shift dress, or with denim shorts and a tee. The possibilities are endless.

## 3. Ancient Greek Sandals ‘Myrtis’ Leather Sandals

![Ancient Greek Sandals ‘Myrtis’ Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/3_ancient-greek-sandals-myrtis-leather-sandals_500x209.jpg)

**Price:** $195.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/BPSA9o)
If you like your sandals handmade then these are definitely worth a look. They feature crossover front straps, square rubber heels, and wing shaped buckles at the ankles. These are quite the versatile summer sandals and would look great with a range of warm weather outfits.

## 4. ASOS ‘Flutter’ Leather Flat Sandals

![ASOS ‘Flutter’ Leather Flat Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/4_asos-flutter-leather-flat-sandals_500x214.jpg)

**Price:** $32.23 qat [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/wZ5nbB).
A simple strappy sandal is truly versatile. These have a two part design with wide straps at the ankles and toes. They also feature pin-buckle fastenings and a flat sole. Wear these with a printed sundress for an effortlessly chic look.

## 5. See by Chloé Leather Sandals

![See by Chloé Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/5_see-by-chlo-eacute-leather-sandals_500x290.jpg)

**Price:** $250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/mZU76i)
If you want to get the most wear out of your **sandals for summer, choose ones** with fuss-free designs. These are made from light brown leather and feature a side strap at the toe, low stacked heel, and a buckle-fastening ankle strap. Wear these with a denim shirt dress and colourful bag for a youthful and on-trend outfit.

## 6. Topshop ‘Nico’ Low Heel Sandals

![Topshop ‘Nico’ Low Heel Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/6_topshop-nico-low-heel-sandals_500x308.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/12bDrO)
These stylish brown sandals feature a low wooden heel. They have crossover straps at the toe, a buckle-fastening ankle strap, and gold piping details. These shoes would look stylish worn with a pair of dark denim jeans, a singlet, and an oversized clutch bag.

## 7. Ancient Greek Sandals Braided Leather Sandals

![Ancient Greek Sandals Braided Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/7_ancient-greek-sandals-braided-leather-sandals_500x211.jpg)

**Price:** $195.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Wzsfub)
The best part about summer footwear is when it easily slips on and off, making it easier to go barefoot on the beach or grass. These handmade sandals have a braided thong detail, toe rine, and wide foot strap. They’re simple and stylish, and perfect for summer.

## 8. Topshop ‘Heroic’ Gladiator Sandals

![Topshop ‘Heroic’ Gladiator Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/8_topshop-heroic-gladiator-sandals_500x228.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/BGEpYp)
Glam up your summertime look with some metallic sandals, like this pair from Topshop. They feature plaited straps to the front, smooth straps at the ankle, and an adjustable buckle fastening. Wear them to glitz up your daytime look or as a sensible alternative to your dancing heels.

## 9. Dorothy Perkins Nude Metal Trim Sandals

![Dorothy Perkins Nude Metal Trim Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/03/9_dorothy-perkins-nude-metal-trim-sandals_500x252.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These sandals feature thin, rope-like straps. They come in a lovely blush pink colour and feature metal trims throughout. **These sandals for summer** would look great worn underneath maxi dresses and skirts.

If your summer sandals are going to be on high rotation, then it’s important that you look for a pair that’s well made. There’s nothing worse than having a pair of sandals break on you! Which of these sandals for summer would you want in your wardrobe?
