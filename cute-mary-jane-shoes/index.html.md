# 10 Cute Mary Jane Shoes ...

Mary Jane Shoes always trendy, always sexy, always a good buy and I think I’ve never met a lady that hasn't been affected, or should I say madly in love, with their retro charm! Are you a fan of Mary Jane shoes as well? Well, then you’ll simply love these 10 suggestions! Make some space in your shoe closets, ladies, because these Mary Jane shoes are a candy you simply won’t be able to resist!

## 1. ASOS PEPPER Mary Jane Shoes

![ASOS PEPPER Mary Jane Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i9/yo/tpady04z54c937e44def0730177108_391x396.jpg)

**Price:** $82.63 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/JhE5Iq)
A perfect combination of low key and in your face, these glittery Mary Jane shoes might not be an ideal part of your business attire but they could sure compliment every evening outfit you can come up with. They will make your simple dress pop, they will make you the star of the evening and no – you don’t have to ask yourself if they are a bit too much because they most definitely are not!

## 2. ASOS SANDY Mary Janes

![ASOS SANDY Mary Janes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pi/rw/exltel9254c937e53bfce851698813_400x351.jpg)

**Price:** $132.20 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/tlRtvG)
Speaking about work attire – don’t you think these stylish, elegant Mary Janes are a perfect thing to wear both in and out of work? This chunky heel is ultra trendy, plus you could definitely live with that extra comfort a heel such as this one provides. Black strap and two slim, lady-like bows really stand out on this shiny red leather and since this color goes so well with all those neutral tones most business gals choose (or have to) wear to work, I bet you’ll really rock them whatever you choose to wear them with!

## 3. KG Bella Bow Front Mary Janes

![KG Bella Bow Front Mary Janes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w8/e8/uacebdfl54c937e62b921701993259_358x400.jpg)

**Price:** $231.35 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/TL9GC8)
Available in purple and green as well as white and black, these sexy Mary Jane shoes look tastier than any sweet I’ve had a chance to try recently. I’d choose the purple/green color combo simply because I think it looks much better and waaay more interesting than a standard fool proof black’n’white one but, hey, feel free to fall in love with both because these shoes definitely deserve it.

## 4. Stormont Lane Mary Jane Shoes

![Stormont Lane Mary Jane Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hf/ct/sbdbs6xl54c937e72af3b898077877_400x314.jpg)

Classic, black and low-key, these shoes really are traditional, charming Mary Janes I’ve mentioned in my intro. The heel is perfect for running errands, taking care of business or dealing with your small children looking like a glam, Hollywood mom. A girl can definitely have it all!

## 5. Vivienne Westwood for Melissa Croco Mary Janes

![Vivienne Westwood for Melissa Croco Mary Janes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/bc/pzkoch0u54c937e81c0cf359300090_400x334.jpg)

Peeps around me are still trying to make peace with my Lady Dragon shoes! For what it’s worth, I think these are fabulous!

## 6. Steve Madden Victory Mary Jane Pumps

![Steve Madden Victory Mary Jane Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/vr/n2nf4ip054c937e930796672586140_357x395.jpg)

I mean, sometimes less is actually more than more (regardless of how wacky that sounds) and when you have a sexy, stiletto heel like this one and a concealed platform covered in soft suede, you’ll just rock the place regardless of what you choose to pair these fabulous shoes with!

![Taccetti Mary Jane Black and Pink Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lc/h8/z6782zrk54c937ea34242347255448_357x398.jpg)

## 8. Jessica Simpson Cheetah Mary Janes

![Jessica Simpson Cheetah Mary Janes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/kw/jr110wap54c937eb3383a638779915_363x386.jpg)

Pretty much every expensive shoe has its budget-friendly replacement, we’ve already established that – didn’t we? Well, in case you’ve got your eyes on these fab Taccetti Mary Jane shoes above and you’re thinking something like, “Gosh, I wish the price was just a bit lower and the colors just a bit different”, here’s something you might want to choose instead. The difference is, of course, obvious although I must admit these are more “wearable”.

## 9. CALL IT SPRING Nude Mary Janes

![CALL IT SPRING Nude Mary Janes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i1/yc/bljqhjl254c937ec24b29913078888_400x363.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Every thought about getting nude Mary Jane shoes? Well, I have a perfect pair right here and rocking them this season won’t break your bank either! Some say nude is the new black, others claim it’s actually better than black because it makes the legs appear longer and more perfect so definitely consider these two important style facts before you decide to go shopping for yet another pair of black shoes. Oh, yeah, I’ve almost forgot – in case you decide black is your color of choice after all, definitely don’t panic as they are available in black as well and, quite honestly, look just as fabulous.

## 10. Rocket Dog Osaka Mary Janes

![Rocket Dog Osaka Mary Janes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/co/su/duuhad9k54c937ed3686a659707257_400x304.jpg)

**Price:** $59.95 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Raise your hand if you have at least one pair of Rocket Dog shoes! Comparing to these trendy sky-high platform pumps, these babies seem like… well… babies! However, looking at the shoe and having it on your foot are two completely different things so if you’re going for lady-like, comfy and stylish this would be the shoe for you. Oh, let me just say that Rocket Dog shoes make feet appear so tiny that you literally won’t believe your reflection in the mirror. Be careful though as they run a bit small!

Oh… seeing all these great Mary Jane shoes in one place is definitely making my wallet itchy! Do you feel that way about Mary Jane shoes too or you have a soft spot for something different?
