# 7 Stunning Spring Shoes Youll Love ...

I don’t know about you, but I can’t wait to ditch trainers and snow boots in favor of Spring shoes – there’s just so much more choice when it’s warmer! I’ve already started packing my jumpers and thermals away in favor of gorgeous Spring dresses, khaki trousers and pretty blouses, and I’m loving all the new jewelry, too! Here’s the hottest Spring shoe styles we’ll be wearing this season…

## 1. Edgy Flats…

![Edgy Flats…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/1_edgy-flats_333x500.jpg)

These gorgeous T-bar flats are the ideal Spring shoes, combining a soft, comfortable ballet style with silver studding. They look great worn with cute teadresses, jeans and even for work – and you’ll never believe how comfortable they are. These are $150 from Marais, and you can be sure you’ll be wearing them all year round.

## 2. Peep Toe Boots…

![Peep Toe Boots…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/2_peep-toe-boots_296x280.jpg)

Not ready to ditch boots for good yet? Then give your wardrobe a Spring update with some ankle boots. This striking pair features a chic peep toe edge, and the mesh is thick enough to keep your feet warm without overheating. I love the subtle studs and cute gold buckle, too! $129 from Zara.

## 3. Colorful Clogs…

![Colorful Clogs…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/3_colorful-clogs_456x375.jpg)

It’s not Spring without a pair of flamenco-inspired shoes, and these Rachel Comey sandals are just perfect. Not only do they have a traditional shape, making them flattering as well as comfortable enough to dance in, but the velvet strap is comfortable and the bright orange pattern is perfect for adding a blast of color to any outfit. They are subtle enough to be worn with almost anything, and you won’t want to take them off. They are a little expensive though, with this design costing $437 from Opening Ceremony and Bird.

## 4. Sneakers…

![Sneakers…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/4_sneakers_600x400.jpg)

Fear not if you don’t want to give up your sneakers yet – this Spring has a whole host of feminine, stylish sneakers. This monochrome design is ideal for dressing up a casual jeans outfit, or rocking the sports luxe look. I love the laser-cut floral pattern in the side, and being waterproof, they are perfect for wearing during Spring showers. They’ll even go with outfits in any color, and they cost just $95 at Nina West. Dreamy.

## 5. Bright and Beautiful…

![Bright and Beautiful…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/5_bright-and-beautiful_328x322.jpg)

I LOVE this floral pattern. It’s bold and eye-catching, without being too overpowering, and the heel is just the perfect height…can you tell I’m in love?! I’ll be teaming these $90 Aldo beauties with my little black dress for evenings, bikinis and crochet cover-ups, and simple maxi dresses.

## 6. Easy Wedges…

These Dolce Vita wedges are just perfect for getting you in the mood for Spring. The pastel color is right on trend, and ideal for teaming with everything from white jeans to black mini dresses, and the cross-straps are right off the catwalk. Perhaps most importantly, they are oh-so-comfortable to wear, even on cobbled pavements. These are the perfect choice for exploring cities! £189 from Dolce Vita.

## 7. See through…

![See through…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/03/7_see-through_319x288.jpg)

There’s nothing like dramatic shoes to add a stunning finishing touch to an outfit, and these beauties are perfect. Unfortunately, the price tag isn’t – these stunning blue, yellow and green glass shoes cost $663.16 from Sergio Rossi. But there’s plenty of copycats across the high street, and the look is very easy to wear – just add simple block-colored dresses, or wear with white jeans and a pretty blouse. Spring shoes = sorted.

I love investing in gorgeous Spring shoes. They are a great way to update the wardrobe for the new season, and give last year's favorites a fresh new look. Which shoes would you love to get your hands on?! Let me know!
