# How to Choose the Right Nursing Shoes for All the Nurses out There ...

More often than not, nurses are subjected to long hours of standing and doing rounds during their shifts. This is why being a nurse requires special gear that would ensure her comfort.

The strain on the legs and feet is just one of the many complications this profession can bring about, and that’s why it is essential for a nurse to find the footwear that would give her the feeling of ease and comfort.

**Important Factors in Choosing The Right Nursing Shoes:**

Some might think that looking for nursing shoes is as easy as shopping for everyday footwear, but it isn’t that simple. Keep in mind that these are not the usual shoes you use in your daily life, but ones that serve special purposes during a long, tedious shift. There are some factors to consider when choosing the right nursing shoes, and we will share them with you in this article!

## 1. Weight

This factor is probably the one that you must consider with all seriousness. Remember that you would have to wear these shoes for many hours every day. If you wear something that’s too heavy, it might be uncomfortable and hurt your feet. This might not be such a big problem for those who are just sitting at a desk during her shift, but if you circulate, you should opt for lighter, more comfortable shoes.

## 2. Slip Resistance

Nurses are always subjected to different situations in the hospital. One particular situation that is very common is running errands during emergencies. There will also be times when you have to walk on surfaces with spilled liquids. For these times, you should have a reliable pair of shoes that will protect you from the fall on a smooth and slippery surface.

## 2. Functionality

More than your personal comfort, you should also look for work shoes that serve the function of your position. As a nurse, this means a reliable, lightweight pair that allows you to move quickly and freely. In this context, you also have to make sure that your shoes are the right size for you.

## 3. Size

Making sure that you are wearing the right shoe size will definitely increase your pair’s functionality. In choosing the correct size, you have to make sure that you are able to wiggle your toes inside. Choose a pair that allows your feet to breathe and one that doesn’t feel too tight or too loose.

## 3. Price

Admit it, when it comes to work shoes, some of us are reluctant to go the extra mile and pay top dollar. Although there are brands that sell these pairs cheaper than others, there are also high-quality brands that offer [comfortable nursing footwear](https://comfortingfootwear.com/best-nursing-shoes-in-white/) that will serve you for a long time.

## 5. Benefits of Finding the Perfect Pair

Wanting to find the perfect shoes, especially for work, is not a sign of vanity. It actually tells that you are a professional who wants to perform at your best, given any situation. As a nurse, you cannot afford to miss the amazing benefits of finding the best nursing shoes.

**These are just a few benefits:**

● The perfect pair lets you move around easily. If you are stuck with nursing shoes that are not giving you that benefit, then it is possible that they prevent you from doing your job in the most productive way.

● Good quality nursing shoes can relieve foot pain. If you are suffering from pain during your work hours, this might cause you to do less than you have to. This might also affect your mood and decrease your productivity. With the right shoes, your feet will feel comfortable, and you will always find delight in the things that you do.

● Investing in a relatively more expensive pair of work shoes will allow you to save more money than constantly replacing cheap alternatives that wear out fast. There are high-quality brands that give replacements and refunds if the shoes you bought didn’t work out for you.

**“Good shoes take you to good places.”**

As this famous saying goes, the perfect pair of shoes will take you to the professional heights. Attending to your patients and doing it effectively are every nurse's main objectives.

There is nothing wrong with spending extra time finding the best gear that will help you flourish in your field.

Now that you are aware of the things to consider looking for the best nursing shoes and some of its benefits, go on and start looking for your perfect pair today! Remember that knowing what works best for you means that you also know how to give your best to your patients.

You are doing one of the most vital jobs in the world, and nothing beats having the perfect work shoes to help you do your best every day.
