# 8 Pointed Pump Picks for Fall ...

This season signaled the return of the classic pointed pump shoe silhouette for fall. The return to basics this fall not only meant a revival of the timeless shape, but also a contemporary chic update with fall’s favorite trends, including print and pattern play and edgy adornments. Opt for these 8 pointed pump picks for fall to create high style statements all season long.

## 1. Dune "Bernie" Leopard Pointed Pumps

![Dune "Bernie" Leopard Pointed Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/09/1_dune-bernie-leopard-pointed-pumps_600x521.jpg)

**Price:** $145 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/6wosGm)
Wild exotic animal skins graced the runways for fall, and the best pointed pumps for the season wouldn’t be complete without the fashionista favourite: a leopard print shoe. The classic slip on style combined with faux animal hair and leopard print makes this pointed pump an essential for any girl's shoe collection. As a timeless staple, you can’t go wrong with an animal print pump and can definitely count on this piece lasting you through the seasons for a style that is always chic.

## 2. Kate Spade "Lisa" Polka Dot Pumps

![Kate Spade "Lisa" Polka Dot Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/09/2_kate-spade-lisa-polka-dot-pumps_529x395.jpg)

**Price:** $328 at [katespade.com](https://www.katespade.com/sale/)
Dress up in true feminine fashion with these polka dot pumps from ladylike designer Kate Spade. Paying homage to retro dressing styles from various eras, these pumps are definite showstoppers you can strut around in all season long. The mesh design of this pump gives off a nude appearance. You'll look and feel like you’re wearing nothing at all on your feet, making it super lightweight and comfortable.

## 3. Alice + Olivia Printed Patent Pumps

![Alice + Olivia Printed Patent Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/09/aliceandoliviadevon_paintedfloral_2_442x345.jpg)

**Price:** $295 at [aliceandolivia.com](http://www.aliceandolivia.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Florals are must-have trends that don’t stop after the summer months. Fall florals feature darker palettes with pops of bold colour to brighten up your look. These pumps are perfect for either casual or dressy occasions, so don’t be shy with your print mixing this season. Add some floral fantasy to your fall wardrobe with these patent pumps from Alice + Olivia for a modern update that will have you blossoming until Spring.

## 4. Game2 Pointed Boucle Pumps

![Game2 Pointed Boucle Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/09/32G11CWHT_large-373x560_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $48 at [topshop.com](https://goo.gl/AGr2ah)
Black and white is a classic colour combination that is both effortless and chic, and always in style. Glam these pumps up with the perfect little black dress as the slight sparkle in the thread of the shoe will have you shining all night long. The boucle fabric of these pumps will have you transitioning seamlessly from fall to winter for season long style.

## 5. J.Crew Everly Cap Toe T-Strap Pumps

![J.Crew Everly Cap Toe T-Strap Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/09/12095_WE8727_418x418.jpg)

**Price:** $318 at [jcrew.com](https://goo.gl/eQsR66)
Geometric shapes and patterns gave everyone a lesson in print-mixing this season. The combined geometric pattern and elegant T-Strap make this J.Crew pump refined and fun all at the same time. The modern mix of the two refined and fun elements give these pumps a contemporary chic feel that will dress up your daytime office wear or your off-duty denim.

## 6. Rachel Roy Gingham Pumps

![Rachel Roy Gingham Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/09/cn5320557-420x560_420x560.jpg)

**Price:** $225 at [piperlime.gap.com](http://piperlime.gap.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Traditional houndstooth is a print that will come back every season without fail. Houndstooth print is reminiscent of the 60s mod era and has a vintage yet classic feel to it. These pumps are revived with smart leather detailing that will add a touch of simple sophistication to any look. Don the menswear-dressing trend this season with these pumps for some feminine flair.

## 7. Aldo «Perusia» Studded Pumps

![Aldo «Perusia» Studded Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/09/26_perusia_61_6-560x560_560x560.jpg)

**Price:** $100 at [aldoshoes.com](http://www.aldoshoes.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A bold red pump is a lust-worthy item every woman must own. For an edgy, avant-garde remix, these studded pumps are eye-catching statements that will have others stopping dead in your tracks. The fierce mix of fiery red and studded embellishments makes these pumps a super sexy heel for nights out on the town. Dress these pumps up for a dazzling look from head to toe.

## 8. H&M Metallic Cap Toe Pumps

_ **Price:** $34.95 at [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Cap toe pumps are ruling the shoe world both on and off the runways. For a cool, sleek pair opt for cap toe pumps with a metallic finishing and a metallic heel to tie the look together. The simple and understated design of these H&M pumps makes them the perfect go-to shoes for all day wear.

Not only are these pumps super chic, they are also known for their ability to elongate your legs, creating the appearance of longer and leaner limbs, which serves both style and function. How will you be wearing your pointed pumps this fall?
