# 8 Wonderful Wooden Heeled Shoes ...

Wooden heeled shoes are a trend that seems to stay season after season. They can take the form of a low clog or an attention-grabbing platform wedge. There’s a wooden heeled shoe for nearly all tastes and budgets. Below are just a few wonderful wooden heeled shoes for you to browse.

## 1. Forever21 Wooden Platform Heel

![Forever21 Wooden Platform Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/op/uz67kmvm54c8f7c7bd3a8340644418_250x205.jpg)

**Price:** $28.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These sky-high heels are extremely on-trend for the summer months. These shoes are made from a suedette material, feature a wooden platform heel, and have antique-look hardware accents. The peep-toe design and ankle straps complement the platform heel quite nicely. I’d wear these with tailored cuffed pants or floaty sundresses.

## 2. Topshop Wonda Black Leather Buckle Wooden Wedges

![Topshop Wonda Black Leather Buckle Wooden Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xd/gh/nw6scpc554c8f7c875211899965690_250x183.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/MFv77H)
These shoes are a great alternative to the high heeled, platform wedge. They feature a small heel and are great for wearing during the day. There are two straps at the front of the foot and two straps around the ankle. The black leather is accented by silver-look hardware. These would look great paired with a rich colour palette and denim.

## 3. See by Chloe Strappy Wedge Sandal

![See by Chloe Strappy Wedge Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/cq/e0gq5rns54c8f7c939b95601261503_250x203.jpg)

**Price:** $260.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This wedge sandal manages to combine minimal design with a bohemian flair. My favourite design feature has to be the wraparound ankle strap. It also has two criss-crossing leather straps at the top of the foot, hammered details on the inner and outer sides, and a rubber sole. Wear these underneath maxi-length skirts or dresses.

## 4. Rachel Comey Mars Short Boot

![Rachel Comey Mars Short Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n8/sb/fuxq357r54c8f7c9f1131435909622_250x165.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These ankle boots would make a fantastic addition to any wardrobe. They are a sleek and classic design, and would easily take you between seasons. These shoes are made from 100% leather and feature a chunky wooden heel. I’d wear them with anything and everything – from skinny jeans and thick overcoats to loose dresses and floppy sunhats.

## 5. Topshop Jeeves White Block Heel Brogues

![Topshop Jeeves White Block Heel Brogues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ar/f4/e725ytnz54c8f7cac3367311678130_250x175.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/LQJ7Nh)
These shoes are perfect for adding a preppy twist to your outfit. The chunky wooden heel is a welcome addition to the more common flat brogue. It also adds a feminine twist to what is otherwise a very menswear inspired piece. The combination of cream leather against the grained wood makes the overall design quite palatable.

## 6. Vena Cava Tenoversix Platform Mary Jane

![Vena Cava Tenoversix Platform Mary Jane](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g7/kv/vn9jvgqj54c8f7cb9a80b172972046_250x151.jpg)

**Price:** $340.00 at [openingceremony.us](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.openingceremony.com/products.asp?menuid=2&catid=16&subcatid=83&productid=33191)
These are such unique looking shoes. They feature a low wooden heel and sole. The colour combination of off-white and nude leather is quite fresh. The toe is done in off-white leather and the heel cover is in nude leather. For a casual look, wear with tailored shorts and loose fitting singlets.

## 7. Zara Wooden Heel Shoe

![Zara Wooden Heel Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d0/bk/pw2chy6j54c8f7cc5dca4373962847_250x172.jpg)

**Price:** $89.00 at [zara.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.zara.com/webapp/wcs/stores/servlet/product/11719/en/zara-S2011/61144/305540/WOODEN%2BHEEL)
The wide crossover strap at the front of the foot is quite sophisticated and sleek. It features an adjustable slingback heel. The contrast of dark tan leather and the wooden heels works quite well together. These would look great paired with a crisp white shirt dress or tailored pants.

## 8. Topshop Rockie Tan Leather Wooden Heeled Slingback Sandals

![Topshop Rockie Tan Leather Wooden Heeled Slingback Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h7/od/t1t6jguv54c8f7cd149bc794097938_250x191.jpg)

**Price:** $125.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/2nC1m2)
These strappy sandals are a terrific staple shoe. They are made from leather and feature a wooden stacked heel. They feature criss-cross straps at the front of the foot and an ankle strap with buckle fastening. The tan leather would work well with a neutral colour palette.
