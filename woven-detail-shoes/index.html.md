# 7 Woven\-Detail Shoes ...

These days, shoes can come in an endless range of designs. Added embellishments can really shape the visual appeal of a shoe. Woven details seem to be a mainstay on the shoe scene. They can add a vintage touch to the overall design of the shoe. They can also create interesting textures and patterns. Check out a few woven-detail shoes below.

## 1. Topshop Kelsey Tan Woven Lace up Shoes

![Topshop Kelsey Tan Woven Lace up Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fx/ex/on2t8vwi54c8fd2fbd5b6546653093_400x193.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/0v6IDa)
These vintage style brogues have an all-over woven body. The woven detailing variates from a smaller weave on the toe cap to a larger weave on the main body of the shoe. They are made from leather and feature a slight wooden heel. These would look great paired with full skirts and pretty blouses.

## 3. Bloch Elise Woven Leather Ballerina Flats

**Price:** $190.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/vLbljs)
The woven aspect of these shoes provides an interesting twist to the classic ballerina design. These woven-detail shoes come in a metallic finish which would be suited to both day and night. They have a rounded toe, cut out trim, and a wooden sole. These are versatile in the way that they could be teamed with a range of outfits.
