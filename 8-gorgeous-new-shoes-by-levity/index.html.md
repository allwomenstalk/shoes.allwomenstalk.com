# 8 Gorgeous New Shoes by Levity ...

I’m always on the lookout for new shoe brands, and recently, DSW (Designer Shoe Warehouse) started carrying an exclusive line called Levity. Now I’m completely hooked on their sandals and booties, and once you see them, you will be too. Here are 8 gorgeous new shoes by Levity.

## 1. Levity Sax Wedge Sandal

![Levity Sax Wedge Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/od/xzgk2fgq54c8f9b2b2ac9180767544_534x274.jpg)

**Price:** $49.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/qKa40Y)
How incredibly cute! I love the wood detail on the cork wedge and platform! The light brown, tan, or black leather upper is a nice contrast, too, and will look great with almost anything in your spring and summer wardrobe! It’s available in most whole and half sizes from 5 to 11, so there’s bound to be a perfect fit for you!

## 2. Levity Sadie Wedge Sandal

![Levity Sadie Wedge Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/eb/b4vc95ya54c8f9b3c8781532466164_534x334.jpg)

**Price:** $69.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/MgRiKT)
This cute little Levity shoe is more than a wedge… it’s almost a sock, too! The upper is a traditional lather sandal, with a stacked wedge heel… and then a departure from the bland and everyday! There’s a fabric part around the ankle that’s so on-trend and unique! Pair with cropped denim for a hot spring time look!

## 3. Levity Sam Bootie

![Levity Sam Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/ic/ytxyqsou54c8f9b4e7dc7728426683_534x356.jpg)

**Price:** $89.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/kBAEhf)
So hot! Choose from dark grey or sleek black, either way, this bootie is so sexy! It boasts a quilted leather upper, peep-toe, rubber sole with tread for traction, and a zipper at the back for easy off-and-on. The heel measures 4½ inches, with an additional ¾-inch platform. These would look amazing with a fitted LBD…

## 4. Levity Suzie Leather Bootie

![Levity Suzie Leather Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/fu/stkepd4l54c8f9b5e4df5852983358_534x351.jpg)

**Price:** $79.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/VNraff)
These booties are so unique! They feature a dark or light brown leather upper with clever cut-outs, a peep toe, and an amazing covered cone heel. There’s also a zipper along the top of the foot, so though this pair of shoes runs a little narrow, you’ll get a perfect fit!

## 5. Levity Snap Wedge Sandal

![Levity Snap Wedge Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o4/gv/vq31henm54c8f9b6f06ae088705738_534x338.jpg)

**Price:** $59.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/o1Oj2j)
Here’s another sexy wedge by Levity, perfect for spring and summer! It’s the classic gladiator style, with loads of criss-crossed straps across the toes and up the ankle, with a stacked wedge heel and a zipper behind the heel. Choose from dark or light brown, and try them with a flirty sundress!

## 6. Levity Stop Pump

![Levity Stop Pump](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/u7/nzjs7u0n54c8f9b806ef0717692692_534x340.jpg)

**Price:** $59.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/jGkmx5)
These shoes are a modern update on the classic platform pump, and they’re gorgeous! Pair them with trousers for work or a floral-print sundress for an outdoor wedding. Choose from brown or black. Both have the same top-stitching detail, 4½ inch wood-look heel, and almond-shaped toe with a bow detail.

## 7. Levity Sky Sandal

![Levity Sky Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/lw/tp4jg0hj54c8f9b935500762809431_534x339.jpg)

**Price:** $79.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/nUPW7b)
The sky’s the limit with these chic peep-toe Levity heels! They come in pebbled black or tan leather, with the same brown wood heel and cute cut-outs on the sides. There’s a zipper in back so they’re easy to take off and put on, and they’re available in a range of sizes. These would look great with a pair of light-wash skinny jeans and a loose Boho tunic!

## 8. Levity Sell Sandal

![Levity Sell Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/ve/h02irsfk54c8f9ba6045a542994829_534x330.jpg)

**Price:** $59.95 at [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/jo38zb)
I love these bird-cage sandals mostly for their striking cone heel… isn’t that pretty? They’re available in brown or black, and both feature the same 3-inch heel and elegant, shapely styling. I’d wear these with wide-leg trousers and a ruffled blouse. How pretty!

Now that you’ve seen just a few of their shoes, can you see why I’m in love with Levity? They’re so modern and chic, but comfortable… and they’re very reasonably priced! Which of these new shoes by Levity do you like best, and what would you wear with them? Please share!
