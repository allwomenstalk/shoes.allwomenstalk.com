# 7 Incredibly Stylish Rugged Wedge Booties for Fall ...

To channel the season’s earthy and natural mood, try these incredibly stylish rugged wedge booties for fall. Lug-soled wedge booties offer a rugged and outdoorsy feel to fall footwear this season to capture the essence of fall in a shoe. Embrace the great outdoors with these incredibly stylish rugged wedge booties for fall.

## 1. Leather Wedge Bootie

![Leather Wedge Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/1_leather-wedge-bootie_600x578.jpg)

Leather is a total utilitarian and stylish option when it comes to selecting the perfect rugged wedge bootie for fall. Pair your leather wedge bootie with denim and cool fall layers for a cozy and chic natural feel. Wedge booties serve comfort and function for stylish purpose that’s suitable for the great outdoors during the changing temps of fall. Wedge booties like the Ash one pictured above are a rugged, outdoorsy footwear option for fall that’s comfortable and chic. Other incredibly stylish rugged wedge booties can be found in the $75 to $700 range with styles found at Kelsi Dagger, Tory Burch, See by Chloe and Camilla Skovgaard.

## 2. Lace-up Wedge Bootie

![Lace-up Wedge Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/2_lace-up-wedge-bootie_327x321.jpg)

For a true rugged, outdoorsy feel select a lace up wedge bootie with autumnal accents like leather, wool or shearling to capture the essence of fall in your new footwear. Keep your fall cool by pairing a lace up wedge bootie with true seasonal duds like a chunky knit sweater or army-style parka to embrace fall’s cozy vibe.

## 3. Colored Wedge Bootie

![Colored Wedge Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/3_colored-wedge-bootie_600x542.jpg)

A simple, clean lined wedge bootie is ideal for the minimalist this fall season. For a bootie that is minimal, opt for rich and warm colors to add a bit of edge to its rugged style. Deep oxblood is considered the new black for fall so incorporate this trendy tone into your footwear collection in the shape of a simple rugged wedge bootie.

## 4. Suede Wedge Booties

![Suede Wedge Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/4_suede-wedge-booties_340x398.jpg)

Suede wedge booties offer a rugged yet stylish and chic way to wear wedge booties for fall. Dress up a pair of suede wedge booties with sleek accompaniments like leather leggings for a cool urbanite aesthetic this fall. Don’t forget to seal your suede booties with footwear protection to shield them from the seasonal elements and maximize your shoe’s longevity.

## 5. Leopard Print Wedge Bootie

![Leopard Print Wedge Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/5_leopard-print-wedge-bootie_600x766.jpg)

A staple in some form or another is the classic and timeless leopard print. Work a rugged leopard print wedge bootie into your fall footwear collection by sporting it with skinny denim and a tough moto jacket for a fierce edge. Let your wild side shine with a leopard print wedge.

## 6. Shearling Trim Wedge Bootie

![Shearling Trim Wedge Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/6_shearling-trim-wedge-bootie_500x401.jpg)

Shearling, like fur, is a super luxurious and plush way to add a textural element into your fall wardrobe. Shearling has a more cool and casual feel to it that allows you to wear it with off-duty denim or chic cargos for a footwear option that’s versatile in the fall season. A shearling trim wedge bootie offers versatility that perfectly suits autumns changing weather.

## 7. Buckle Wedge Bootie

![Buckle Wedge Bootie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/7_buckle-wedge-bootie_600x812.jpg)

For an edgy and tough alternative to earthy and outdoorsy styles, a buckled wedge bootie still has rugged appeal that’s extra cool. A rugged wedge bootie is ultra-sleek and chic in a black monochromatic finish with high polished metallic buckle accents. To keep your look sleek and sophisticated, stick to streamlined pieces with an edge like leather paneled leggings for tough girl cool.

Become one with nature this season with rugged wedge booties for fall that achieve autumn’s earthy directive. Outdoorsy, rustic wedge booties are cool weather must-haves that add cozy and comfy charm to your new fall wardrobe. Remember to look for a lug sole to enhance your wedge bootie’s rugged appeal for a chic outdoorsy vibe. How will you sport rugged wedge booties for fall?
