# 7 New Wedges I Love ...

Wedges are incredibly hot this spring, and it’ not hard to see why - they look fabulous with everything in your closet, from cropped jeans to short skirts and everything in between. They’re comfortable, too, so you’re going to want to wear them all the time! It seems like so many of my favorite labels are offering wedges, so I’m having a hard time restraining myself from buying far more wedges than any one girl should. Here are 7 new wedges I love.

## 1. Chinese Laundry "Come Together" Wedges

![Chinese Laundry "Come Together" Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o2/nf/s6rkpv4f54c8f7beaccf6661100349_450x285.jpg)

**Price:** $68.99 at [shopruche.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
I love the warm peachy-pink color of these wedges by Chinese Laundry. Wouldn’t these look pretty with a little denim romper? I adore the clever little cut-outs on the uppers, too, and the cork heel… and the buckle on the ankle strap ensures a perfect fit.

## 2. Poetic License "Magical Sunset" Wedges

![Poetic License "Magical Sunset" Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p1/dw/q13zn53u54c8f7bfb3ce0983126774_450x231.jpg)

**Price:** $97.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
I must have these wedges! They’re not espadrilles, but rather ballet flats plus! They feature a floral-print upper with a matching covered wedge, and a trove of glittering jewels on the toes. I’d wear these with dark-wash skinny jeans and a tunic, or with a pair of cuffed linen shorts.

## 3. Springtime Brunch Espadrille Wedges

**Price:** $39.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
These pretty wedges feature thick braided straps in dove grey, perfect for wearing with any of your neutral dresses or with grey trousers and a ruffled blouse. They have a 4½ inch heel and a 1-inch platform, and the strap at the ankle is adjustable, so they’ll fit perfectly.

## 4. The Leaves' Lyrics Grey Wedges

![The Leaves' Lyrics Grey Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n6/c9/cz94im0x54c8f7c1bb350530249317_450x267.jpg)

**Price:** $32.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
How gorgeous! These are the best of both worlds, heels and wedges, with a dark grey upper, floral-print detail at the peep-toe, and a lovely textured wedge heel. Wear these with a floral-print romper or with a tunic and leggings this spring.

## 5. Restricted "Carley" Mary Jane Wedges

![Restricted "Carley" Mary Jane Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tq/gn/u0t2hxf154c8f7c2b82cf603653233_450x263.jpg)

**Price:** $70.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Are these Mary Janes, or oxford, or wedges? Perhaps they’re all three, and they’re absolutely darling! Wear these with a menswear inspired outfit to the office, or with a vintage skirt and patterned leggings for date night.

## 6. Blowfish Easy Street Denim Wedge

![Blowfish Easy Street Denim Wedge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/ph/f44c418b54c8f7c3b149d997403395_450x238.jpg)

**Price:** $62.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Lately, Blowfish has been using a lot of denim in their shoes, and I love the look! These wedge sandals feature dark brown denim uppers with a comfortable covered Velcro closure at the ankle and a criss-cross detail at the peep toe. Wear these with every casual outfit you own, for a day of shopping or a walk in the park.

## 7. Blowfish “Beacher” Wedges

![Blowfish “Beacher” Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d8/x8/qoxek4y054c8f7c4aa997441795149_450x295.jpg)

**Price:** $62.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
What fun! These wedges are also made by Blowfish of nubby canvas, with bootie styling and a sweet peep toe. I adore the tiny cut-out detail at the sides and the little bow in the back. What a great way to welcome spring!

Any of these wedges would make an ideal addition to your spring wardrobe… you’ll have fun wearing them all season long! I especially love the floral-print ones by Poetic License... which wedges do you love best, and how would you wear them? Please share!
