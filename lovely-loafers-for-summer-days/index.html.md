# 7 Lovely Loafers for Summer Days ...

**Loafers** used to be very "sweet old man"… however this year they have been given an upgraded style and gone very "chic young woman." I am very much in love with the Loafer this year as I see them as a shoe that can take me from Spring, Summer, Fall and Winter. Staying with fresh colors, cool prints and fancy details this Summer the Loafer is where its at. Here are my 7 favorite Loafers for Summer...

## 1. ModCloth | Shop until You Lemon Drop Flats

For $54.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Does anything say summer quite like a pair of bright yellow shoes? And these comfy, cute loafers are all sunshine and lemon drops. I love the styling... dressy enough to pair with a retro skirt, but casual enough to wear with capris. So cute!

## 2. ASOS | River Island Canvas Chain **Loafers**

![ASOS | River Island Canvas Chain Loafers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/image1xl_290x145.jpg)

For $39.39 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/z5OOTc)

These coral creations are my favorite. Added details like the Gold Chain and Leather Tassels makes them Uber Chic but their loafer vibe say Comfortable Casual. There is nothing like a shoe that says you’re laid-back luxury kinda girl.

## 3. Jildor | Sam Edelman Aster Loafer

![Jildor | Sam Edelman Aster Loafer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/Untitled-5-300x211_300x211.jpg)

For $150.00 at [jildor.com](https://www.jildorshoes.com/?AID=11999617&PID=6147293&cjevent=8fabc8410bf311ed81fd03920a82b82c)

**These lovely loafers** to me say "I must be going… my yacht is waiting." Made of lush faux snakeskin with a shiny metal toe detail, these loafers are 0n-trend and would look great with jeans or with a pair of pinstripe, high-waisted pants and a white blouse.

## 4. Jildor | Stuart Weitzman Geek Loafer

![Jildor | Stuart Weitzman Geek Loafer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/Untitled-4-300x233_300x233.jpg)

For $162.50 at [jildor.com](https://www.jildorshoes.com/?AID=11999617&PID=6147293&cjevent=afd6f88a0d2311ed820c00d60a82b824)

These lush patent leather loafers are the best of two hot summer trends - wonderful wedges (note the heel) and menswear-inspired loafers. Choose from beige or black, either way, you have a pair of lovely loafers!

## 5. Jildor | Shelia Loafer Leopard Satin

![Jildor | Shelia Loafer Leopard Satin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/Untitled-1-300x189_300x189.jpg)

For $161.21 at [jildorshoes.com](https://www.jildorshoes.com/?AID=11999617&PID=6147293&cjevent=b3ca3cb00e7111ed82ac405a0a82b820)

Leopard Loafers are the number one shoe on my wish list at the moment. A fun and sassy print that can change any basic outfit to a standout ensemble. A fun way to add a little animal in your life.

## 6. Jildor | Steve Madden Conncord Loafer

![Jildor | Steve Madden Conncord Loafer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/Untitled-2-300x194_300x194.jpg)

For $99.00 at [jildorshoes.com](https://www.jildorshoes.com/?AID=11999617&PID=6147293&cjevent=8437fe3a0da911ed82ac3f9b0a82b820)

These **lovely camo-print loafers** are fun and edgy which is probably why I love them so much. Who doesn’t need a little studded, sparkly Steve Madden in their lives? I can see these loafers paired casually or dressed up... the sky’s the limit.

## 7. Wet Seal | Raffia Loafer

_ For $19.50 at wetseal.com_

**At less than $20 these Loafers** are a great addition to any wardrobe. Hitting a few current trends with a Chevron print and in a neutral Tan color, I believe these Raffia Loafers are a fabulous buy and are simple enough to go with almost anything.

Whether you’re personally a fan of the Loafer or not I think you can agree they are a fabulous way to look chic and stay comfortable. A great option over a basic flat the lovely loafer gives you some edge and style. What are you favorite Loafers for the Summer ahead?
