# 7 Super Summery Braided Sandals ...

Braided sandals are popping up everywhere this season. This fabulous Grecian-inspired style is a great combination of rugged explorer, street-style maven and feminine awesomeness. Who doesn't love a good sandal? Braided sandals will look wonderful on your feet throughout summer and are available in a variety of style and color options to fit any outfit or occasion. From t-straps to chunky boho, these styles will complement everything from a maxi dress to denim shorts. Check out these seven style options.

## 1. Stacked Platform Coolness

![Stacked Platform Coolness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o8/fu/x34182ad_452x330.jpg)

Kicking off our list of braided sandals is this pair of Saraa woven platform wedges in Max Red from Sole Society – fantastic casual weekend coolness. Wear this pair with a neutral tone pallet and let the color pop! Pair them with tailored khaki shorts and a flowing shirt or hike up the coolness factor a notch by swapping the khaki shorts for denim. Check them out at [solesociety.com](https://goo.gl/HUo7cF).

## 2. Casual Simplicity

![Casual Simplicity](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z4/tc/q6y6vczo_300x125.jpg)

Check out this cute and subtle olive colored Braided Boho Sandal from Forever 21. The small sole and simple style go great with a geometric print maxi dress – the ultimate summer staple. The color is cool and the metallic bits will catch the light as you walk. Treat yourself to this affordable pair at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 3. Woven Elegance

![Woven Elegance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a5/zj/est1lgr9_400x288.jpg)

This cream colored pair with braided accents from Charles & Keith is a lovely alternative to the earthy greens and browns we usually see. Dress them up with a simple shift or dress them down with shorts and a tee for a casual museum stroll. Add an oversize purse to complete the look. Check these beauties out at [charleskeith.com](http://www.charleskeith.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 4. Tropical Flair

![Tropical Flair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lp/wy/x5r8qm8c_320x273.jpg)

This cute pair of t-strap back-zip sandals from Yes Style has a great low wedge and zero platform toe. The beauty is in the subtle details – and there is plenty of it. From the braided t-strap to the rhinestone accents, gold trim and textured sole, this sandal is full of little bits that add interest but do not distract from the overall style. Check out the details for your self at [yesstyle.com](http://www.yesstyle.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 5. Vintage Feel

![Vintage Feel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wr/d6/p0td6mqz_290x108.jpg)

These Bertie Jar woven tan leather vintage braided sandals from ASOS are super comfy. Wear these with nearly everything for a stylish, comfortable walking shoe. Pair them with denim pants, flowing shirt and a great purse for a bit of weekend cool, anytime. Find them at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/mDTdj8).

## 6. Splurge Worthy

![Splurge Worthy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/am/s0jj5ilt_336x327.jpg)

Diane von Furstenberg sure knows how to style a great shoe. This lovely pair of Selma braided high heel sandals is perfect for a girls' night out, museum visit or any excuse really. Check them out at [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and be prepared to splash some cash on these two-toned beauties.

## 7. Thrifty Cuties

_ Wrapping up our list is this cute little pair from Target. Affordable and stylish, these brown Merona Erin braided sandals are also comfy casual. Pair them with a cute sundress and eye-catching bangles. Polish off your look with a crisp hairstyle and large sunglasses. Voila: summertime casual. Check them out at target.com_.
