# 7 Stylish T\-Strap Shoes ...

T-strap shoes have been spotted on the feet of many a trendsetter recently, such as Alexa Chung, Emma Stone, and Olivia Palermo. Shoes with T-straps come in all sorts of designs this season and are perfect for working a ladylike look. From the ballet-style pump to the platform heel, there’s a T-strap shoe to suit almost every occasion. Want to dip your toes into this trend? Then take a look at these stylish T-strap shoes!

## 1. Bow T-strap Shoes

![Bow T-strap Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_bow-t-strap-shoes_600x312.jpg)

T-strap shoes tend to be quite feminine. Add some bow details and you’ve got an even girlier pair of shoes! These Topshop shoes are obviously inspired by the Valentino and Chloé versions but are much more affordable at only $48!

## 2. Patent T-strap Shoes

![Patent T-strap Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/2_patent-t-strap-shoes_600x251.jpg)

Ditch your basic ballet pumps this season for some flat T-strap shoes. A basic colour like black makes them perfect for teaming with a range of outfits and the low heel means you can wear them all day long. These patent shoes cost around $44 from ASOS and are also available in nude and silver.

## 3. Low T-strap Heels

![Low T-strap Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/3_low-t-strap-heels_320x244.jpg)

Want a pair of stylish T-strap shoes that’ll take you from day to night? A pair of low heeled sandals just might be the answer. These ones from Modcloth have a low stacked heel and come in a chic patent finish. You could easily wear them with blouses and full skirts during the day or with a slinky shift dress at night.

## 4. T-strap Sandals

![T-strap Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/4_t-strap-sandals_600x638.jpg)

For something a bit more glamorous, try a heeled, T-strap sandal. Shoes like the YSL Tribute sandals, pictured above, are a cult favourite. They’d look great teamed with anything from cocktail gowns to tuxedo-style pants. If you can’t afford designer don’t worry because you can get your hands on more affordable styles from stores like ASOS and Forever 21.

## 5. Thick T-strap Shoes

![Thick T-strap Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/5_thick-t-strap-shoes_600x341.jpg)

Channel your inner prepster with these white T-strap shoes. They have more of a Mary-Jane design with a thick t-bar strap and buckle fastening. They’d look great worn with this season’s must-have tweed skirts and cropped pants. The shoes pictured here are $60 from Topshop and similar styles can also be found at ASOS and Modcloth.

## 6. Brogue T-strap Pumps

![Brogue T-strap Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/6_brogue-t-strap-pumps_600x638.jpg)

Brogue pumps are perfect for working a vintage inspired look. These shoes have a classic black and white brogue design with a dainty T-bar strap. These Tory Burch shoes will set you back a little over $400, but rest assured you can get your hands on a similar pair at a fraction of the cost just by browsing sites like Amazon.

## 7. Thin T-Strap Heels

![Thin T-Strap Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/7_thin-t-strap-heels_600x606.jpg)

Strappy heels are a stylish choice for when you want to get dressed up. These off-white T-strap shoes have metallic detailing and a slim stiletto heel. They’d look great styled up with a printed party frock or dressed down with jeans. If you like the look of these particular shoes, they’re from ASOS and on sale for around $70.

Try a little something different this season with these stylish T-strap shoes. No matter what your personal style, there’s bound to be a T-strap shoe to suit you. What are your thoughts on this shoe style? Will you be donning a pair of T-strap shoes this season?
