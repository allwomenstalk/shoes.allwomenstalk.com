# 8 Pretty High Heel Party Shoes to Rock ...

Strut your stuff during the holidays in these pretty high heel party shoes to rock. Whether you’re stepping out on the town or celebrating at a chic cocktail soiree, own the party by putting your best foot forward in statement shoes that add high holiday glamour to all your festive ensembles. From shimmering sparkle to lovely lace, you can become the belle of the ball in one of these pretty high heel party shoes to rock the season in style.

## 1. Glitter High Heels

![Glitter High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_glitter-high-heels_600x550.jpg)

Make your grand entrance into the holiday season with a festive pair of glittery high heel party shoes to rock. Adding a little sparkle and shimmer into your outfit sets a fun and playful tone for the holidays that’ll have you dancing the night away in your fancy footwear. Let your statement party shoe, like the Charlotte Olympia heels above, shine in the spotlight with minimal accessories and a chic cocktail frock. You can rock other party heels from John Lewis, Aldo, Sam Edelman, Esteban Cortazar, BCBGMaxAzria, Zara and Roger Vivier in the $70 to $1,200 price range this holiday season.

## 2. Velvet High Heels

![Velvet High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/2_velvet-high-heels_600x654.jpg)

For a luxurious take on the season, opt for a velvet high heel to make you feel like royalty during the holiday festivities. Follow the season’s opulent directive by selecting a velvet party shoe with a lavish combination of gem embellishment and striking pop of color. For added drama, pair your plush party heels with a rich mix of satin and silk to up the glam factor at any event during the holidays.

## 3. Lace High Heels

![Lace High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/3_lace-high-heels_600x578.jpg)

For equal parts sexy and sweet, a lace party shoe is a delicate and feminine option to don during holiday soirees. Lace offers a romantic yet seductive touch that’s timelessly elegant and sweetly sophisticated no matter the season. Keep it neutral or look for decadent, opulent color combinations.

## 4. Spiked High Heels

![Spiked High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/4_spiked-high-heels_390x360.jpg)

Get killer holiday style with a sexy spiked high heel. To transform your holiday look this season, switch up traditional festive shoes with an edgy and tough spike that’s more rock goddess than pretty princess. Spike and stud encrusted heels offer a spectacular strong, bold statement while adding a touch of shiny, gleaming metallics to still keep your outfit holiday appropriate.

## 5. Ankle Cuff High Heels

![Ankle Cuff High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/5_ankle-cuff-high-heels_600x805.jpg)

When your dress commands attention, keep your footwear simple and subdued during the holidays with an ankle cuff heel. Ankle cuff heels are a sleek and sexy way to keep footwear elegant and effortless for all occasions. This sleek and structural design may be minimal but it adds standout appeal to any look with a cool, chic aesthetic.

## 6. Metallic High Heels

![Metallic High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/6_metallic-high-heels_520x658.jpg)

A molten-hot metallic high heel never fails to dazzle during the holidays. For high holiday glamour, inject a dose of brilliance with gleaming metallics that sparkle in shimmering shades of gold, silver, bronze and pewter. Radiate style in high-octane finishes and polished metals that make metallic shoes blazing hot for day or nighttime wear.

## 7. Strappy High Heels

![Strappy High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/7_strappy-high-heels_300x300.jpg)

Step into a pair of strappy high heels for festive chic, contemporary footwear that gives your holiday look an alluringly glamorous twist. A strappy heel is a party favorite and night-on-the-town necessity for a sleek and sexy edge during all your fashionable fetes. Basic black is sleek and chic but don't be afraid to experiment with color.

## 8. Bow High Heels

_ Put the final finishing touch on your festive soiree ensemble with a pair of bow heels and complete your pretty holiday package. Ultra-ladylike details like satin bows offer a femme fatale fix of seductive glamour and drama. For feminine refinement, wear your bow-embellished heels with a fit and flare party frock to create a silhouette with elegant allure.

Step out in style with these 8 pretty high heel party shoes to rock during the holiday season. Complete all your party-perfect looks with fancy footwear that injects festive holiday glamour, day or night. What are you go-to heels for the holiday season?
