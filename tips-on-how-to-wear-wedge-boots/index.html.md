# 7 Tips on How to Wear Wedge Boots ...

How To Wear Wedge Boots – this may be a question you’ll find yourself asking this season. The wedge boot is hotter than ever and now’s the time to work this trend into your wardrobe. If you’re new to the wedge boot, finding ways to wear the trend may prove difficult. You won’t be the only person wondering how to wear wedge boots this season. Take a look at the following tips on how to wear wedge boots.

## 1. In the Evening

Team your favourite pair of wedge boots with your favourite party dress for a fun evening look. When looking at how to wear wedge boots this way, opt for sleeker designs in dressier finishes. Glitter and sequin finishes are all the rage this season, so if you’re brave, a wedge boot in a sequin finish is a great way for making a statement. If you want something more subtle, you can’t go past a black leather boot.

## 2. Ankle Length

Ankle length boots are one of the most popular styles of wedge boots. They seem to be the most flattering length for this type of shoe. A black leather ankle boot is a smart and versatile choice. Ankle boots can come across as a bit clunky, so make sure you keep the rest of your outfit fairly streamlined.

## 3. Knee Length

While not as popular, knee length wedge boots are still a chic choice. A knee length wedge boot has the potential to create an exaggerated silhouette, so team them with clean lines. Team them with tights, an oversized tunic or shirt dress, and crisp blazer for a fashion-forward look.

## 4. With Skinny Jeans

For the most part, wedge boots look unflattering when worn with flared or bootleg pants. Stick to straight leg or skinny jeans. By doing this you are balancing out the bulkiness of the wedge boot. You can wear your wedge ankle boots with a pair of skinny jeans, either in neutral or this season’s must have coloured denim.

## 5. Under Long Hemlines

Wedge boots look great under longer hemlines. It’s important though that you get the lengths right, otherwise you might end up tripping over your skirt – which could end in disaster! Work a grunge-inspired look and team a pair of ankle-length wedge booths with a column maxi skirt and an oversized knit.

## 6. With Tights

When it comes to how to wear wedge boots, the key is to keep your outfit streamlined. Wedge boots are bulky at the best of times, so your main goal is to create a clean silhouette. It’s not feasible to wear skinny jeans all the time, so a good pair of tights is a must when it comes to dressing up your wedge boots.

## 7. With Fuller Skirts

If you want to wear your wedge boots with a full skirt, make sure that the skirts are on the short to mini end of the scale. Full skirts that hit at the knee can be unflattering when worn with wedge boots. Wear them with opaque tights and a trench coat during the cooler months. When looking at how to wear wedge boots this way in the summer, stick to strappy or espadrille styles.

When it comes to how to wear wedge boots, the key is with experimentation. Working out what styles and outfits work best is your only guaranteed way of ensuring that you pull off the wedge boot successfully. What are your best tips on how to wear wedge boots?
