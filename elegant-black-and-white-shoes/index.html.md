From the runways of Michael Kors to the streets of New York City, black and white shoes are all the rage for spring. And why shouldn’t they be? They’re effortlessly classy and can dress up almost any outfit in a cinch. Here are eight black and white shoes I can’t wait to add to my warm weather wardrobe. My inner city slicker couldn’t be more pleased!
