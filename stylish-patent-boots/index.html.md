# 7 Stylish Patent Boots ...

Patent Boots are a chic way to spice up your wardrobe. This season’s shoe is all about having a strong silhouette – think chunky heels and slightly squared toes. A chunky heel is a great way to complement a feminine look, or complete an androgynous aesthetic. Black patent boots are timeless, but coloured patent leather is a fun twist on the trend. Take a look at some stylish patent boots below.

## 1. Topshop Pomp Patent Chelsea Boots

![Topshop Pomp Patent Chelsea Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ih/l5/njlv3gts54c93072d4d26293452457_350x326.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/3aeTq1)
The Chelsea boot has been quite popular this past season. Here it comes in a high-shine patent leather. These boots feature the signature elastic inserts at the sides as well as stud detailing on the back. Wear these boots under chiffon maxi dresses and with loose fitting blouses.

## 2. Alexander Wang Arizona Boot

![Alexander Wang Arizona Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yz/rc/lxz4gefx54c93073a90bf375801599_350x291.jpg)

**Price:** $550.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This lace-up boot features a patent toe cap and suede shaft. It’s a directional take on the classic combat boot design. These boots have a stacked heel, cord laces, and inner side zips. Work a cool off-duty look and team these boots with coloured jeans and boyfriend style blazer.

## 3. Modcloth a-Boot Town Patent Boots

![Modcloth a-Boot Town Patent Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tk/yc/dvxb0svw54c930748dc82392290477_275x346.jpg)

**Price:** $57.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after a leather alternative, try out these patent ‘vegan’ boots. They have a high lace-up design and feature a faded pink patent finish. It also has an inside zipper for ease of fit. Wear these with skinny jeans, a floaty blouse, and crisp jacket for a cool weekend look.

## 4. Gianvito Rossi Patent Leather Booties

_ **Price:** $325.00 at mytheresa.com_
These patent boots have a sleek silhouette. They come in a pastel green-grey leather and have a rounded cap, covered block heel, and hidden zipper closure. They also have a slightly squared toe. Work a fashion-forward look and wear these with a full midi skirt, jersey tank top, and cropped jacket.

## 5. Robert Clergerie Hut Patent Ankle Boot

![Robert Clergerie Hut Patent Ankle Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kb/k2/gll3qmq354c930762cdcd089118596_350x291.jpg)

**Price:** $775.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Right now, the focus seems to be on ankle boots with a slightly squared toe and block heel. This expensive designer option comes in a sleek black patent leather finish. They have a buckled ankle strap, decorative gold metal trim, and covered zip. Balance out the ankle length of these boots by teaming them with a pair of cropped trousers.

## 6. ASOS Alpini Patent Chelsea Ankle Boots

![ASOS Alpini Patent Chelsea Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zu/yp/j5e95i9e54c93076e20d0202896867_350x266.jpg)

**Price:** $82.33 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/eoiQRE)
These boots also draw from the classic Chelsea design. They feature the classic Chelsea-style elastic panelling, almond shaped toes, and a low block heel. They also have a wrap around ankle strap with stud fastenings. Wear these boots with some distressed jeans and a slouchy knit jumper for a casual everyday look.

## 7. Lanvin Patent Wedge Bootie

_ **Price:** $550.00 at mytheresa.com_
These brown wedge booties are a great neutral piece to have in your wardrobe. These patent boots have a flattering rounded toe cap and a covered wedge heel. They also have a hidden inner zipper closure. Wear these boots with leggings, a tank dress, and suede jacket for a stylish look.

Where real patent leather footwear can be quite expensive, there are always cheaper alternatives in the form of patent leather-look materials. Patent boots also have the potential to show dirt more easily, so be sure to wipe them down after each wear! Overall, patent boots are just one way to mix up your usual look.
