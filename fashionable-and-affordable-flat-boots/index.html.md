# 9 Fashionable and Affordable Flat Boots ...

Flat boots are a fashionable and functional footwear option. A flat boot looks great worn with anything from jeans to dresses. They’re perfect for adding a bit of edge to an outfit. You don’t have to spend up big for a pair of stylish flat boots either, with plenty of affordable options available!

## 1. Black Ankle Boots

![Black Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/1_black-ankle-boots_257x168.jpg)

A pair of ankle-length flat boots will serve you well this season. They’re a versatile style that will work well with a range of different outfits. These ones from ASOS come in a classic Chelsea style and will set you back about $70 – which is a bargain if you consider how expensive leather boots can get these days! Similar styles of black boots can be found for the same price or cheaper from stores like Dorothy Perkins and Aldo.

## 2. Printed Boots

![Printed Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/2_printed-boots_600x346.jpg)

Up the ante this season in a pair of printed boots. A leopard print pattern in the style of these River Island boots from ASOS will give your outfit that wild edge. Forever 21 are also doing a great leopard print Chelsea boot this season and stores like Modcloth offer a fun range of floral print boots.

## 3. Buckled Boots

![Buckled Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/3_buckled-boots_312x232.jpg)

Work a cool off-duty look with a pair of flat boots with buckle detailing. A range of different designers have their own version of this look and you’ll be bound to find buckled boots at places like Topshop and Urban Outfitters. These ones are from Forever 21 and only cost $35, so you better get in quick before they sell out!

## 4. Glitter Boots

![Glitter Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/4_glitter-boots_320x264.jpg)

Thanks to the big brands like Miu Miu, glittery shoes are still enjoying plenty of popularity. You don’t have to spend the designer dollars to stay on-trend though, with a pair of glitter flat boots like these ones from Modcloth only setting you back a cool $50.

## 5. Equestrian Boots

![Equestrian Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/5_equestrian-boots_350x500.jpg)

When it comes to flat boots, the Equestrian style is a classic. This knee-high style of boot looks great worn over skinny jeans or with tights. These particular boots are from Modcloth and cost $60, and you’ll be sure to find similar styles in the same price range from stores like Forever 21.

## 6. Lace up Boots

![Lace up Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/6_lace-up-boots_256x230.jpg)

Lace up boots are another popular choice when it comes to flat boots. These red lace up boots are only $40 from Modcloth and also come in a range of different colours. Wear them with a flirty dress and army style jacket for a street smart look.

## 7. Studded Boots

![Studded Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/7_studded-boots_600x395.jpg)

Stud embellishments will definitely give a pair of flat boots that extra edge. Taking a cue from those famous Chloé Aztec boots, these Topshop boots feature studding at the heel and will set you back about $80. Check out stores like Urban Outfitters and ASOS for similarly affordable styles.

## 8. Western Boots

![Western Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/8_western-boots_600x554.jpg)

Cowboy boots are a classic when it comes to low or flat boot styles. However, if you’re not willing to don a pair of cowboy boots, you can still inject some Western flair into your outfit with these cowboy-esque tan boots from Dorothy Perkins. You’ll also find similar styles in stores like Forever 21 or online at Modcloth.

## 9. Biker Boots

![Biker Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/9_biker-boots_600x538.jpg)

Plenty of designers like Jimmy Choo and Miu Miu feature biker-style flat boots in their collections. However, you don’t have to break the bank to get your hands on a pair. These ones from Aldo will only set you back around $55. Be sure to check out places like Topshop and Urban Outfitters for affordable biker boots as well.

Give your feet a break from heels and try donning a pair of flat boots this season. There’s a range of different styles available to suit all your outfit needs! Which of these stylish flat boots do you like best?
