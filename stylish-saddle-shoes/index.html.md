# 7 Stylish Saddle Shoes ...

Saddle shoes can be a stylish choice of footwear, especially if you’re wanting to work a preppy or vintage inspired look. With the rise in popularity of brogue and Oxford-style shoes, it was only a matter of time before the saddle shoe took the front seat. Typified by contrasting ‘saddle’ panels, this masculine style of lace-up shoe is perfect for ‘toughening’ up a pretty dress this summer. The following are a few stylish saddle shoes you may want to try out this season.

## 1. Pinup Couture Saddle Shoes

![Pinup Couture Saddle Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/1_pinup-couture-saddle-shoes_320x192.jpg)

**Price:** $37.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These black and white lace-ups are the perfect examples of typical saddle shoes. They feature contrasting black and white panels, perforated trims, and white laces. Make these shoes work in a more modern way by teaming them with colourful cropped jeans.

## 2. Delightful Dress-up Flat

![Delightful Dress-up Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/2_delightful-dress-up-flat_322x176.jpg)

**Price:** $37.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These are a more feminine take on the classically masculine saddle shoe. They are made from faux leather materials and come in a sweet blush and white colour combination. They have perforated detailing and also come with ribbon laces. Wear these with a pretty dress and vintage-style straw hat for a cute summer look.

## 3. F-Troupe Mesh Saddle Shoes

![F-Troupe Mesh Saddle Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/3_f-troupe-mesh-saddle-shoes_600x290.jpg)

**Price:** $173.99 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/tgkgNM)
The mesh panels on these shoes make them the perfect enclosed shoes for summer. They come in a sweet pink colour and feature a low stacked heel. Style these up with a white shirt dress and slouchy side bag for a street smart look.

## 4. Boardwalk Boogie Flat

![Boardwalk Boogie Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/4_boardwalk-boogie-flat_330x209.jpg)

**Price:** $38.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These dainty looking saddle shoes feature contemporary cut out details. They have a rounded toe, perforated detailing and a high gloss finish. These would look great teamed with some check shorts, a coloured blouse, and retro sunglasses.

## 5. Sartore Saddle Shoe

_ **Price:** $299.00 at barneys.com_
These saddle shoes are probably closest in design to the typically masculine style. They feature a rounded toe, navy and white panels, perforated detailing, and a thick wood and rubber sole. Balance out the bulkiness of these shoes by teaming them with lightweight dresses.

## 6. Frye Delia Saddle Lace-up

![Frye Delia Saddle Lace-up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/6_frye-delia-saddle-lace-up_600x315.jpg)

**Price:** $162.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The striking pointed toe on these saddle shoes gives them a smarter appearance. They come in a vintage-inspire brown and cream colourway and feature otherwise classic saddle shoe detailing. These shoes would look great worn with tailored shorts, a basic singlet and a crisp blazer for a smart, polished look.

## 7. Rachel Antonoff for Bass Heeled Saddle Shoes

![Rachel Antonoff for Bass Heeled Saddle Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/7_rachel-antonoff-for-bass-heeled-saddle-shoes_320x264.jpg)

**Price:** $108.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Saddle shoes don’t always have to be the low-heeled variety. Here, they come in a retro-inspired heeled design. The polka dot panels are a playful twist on the classic black and white design. Wear these shirts with a midi skirt and button up blouse to complete the retro inspired look.
