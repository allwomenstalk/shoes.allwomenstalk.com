# 7 Cute Slippers to Cosy up in This Winter ...

It's the time of year when you just want to curl up in front of the telly in your cute slippers, and choosing the perfect pair is quite a task - they're going to be keeping your feet cosy all winter long and for the rest of the year too! I love sinking my feet into a snuggly pair of slipper boots or a furry pair of slippers, and here's my pick of 7 cute slippers that are perfect for cosying up in - hopefully you'll find a pair you like here too.

## 1. Kitty Cable Boots by Fatface

![Kitty Cable Boots by Fatface](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13073952_5831_583x583.jpg)

I love Fatface's range of clothing, and these cosy, cute slippers look perfect for lounging around the house in - I find slipper boots so much cosier than other styles and these cable knit boots with fur trims are gorgeous! You're sure to stay warm all winter long with these beauties.

## 2. Faux Fur Bunny Boot Slippers by M & S

![Faux Fur Bunny Boot Slippers by M & S](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13074537_0294_279x260.jpg)

These are super-cute furry slipper boots with bunny ears! Oh, and a little nose. Adorable! With faux fur detail and a flat grip sole, they're ideal for snuggling up in the house.

## 3. Fair Isle Mule Slippers by M & S

![Fair Isle Mule Slippers by M & S](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13073952_0481_279x141.jpg)

Fair Isle jumpers and skirts are everywhere this season, so why not add some knitted Fair Isle slippers to the mix? These dark blue and white mules have a festive feel that makes them perfect for winter, and their slip on style means they're ideal for popping on and off as you go about your day.

## 4. Cozy Knit Slippers by Ugg

![Cozy Knit Slippers by Ugg](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/If/Le/LmMr2q1ABUwz3njbMKUcRmOn_556x247.jpg)

We're all aware of Ugg boots, but did you know they do a great range of slippers too? They're certainly not cheap, but these gorgeous cozy knit slippers are fully lined in sheepskin, with a cable knit outer that makes them super-cute! I love the cream ones, as I think they look really fresh and feminine, but they come in black too for a more wintry feel.

## 5. Snowman Knitted Boot Slippers by River Island

![Snowman Knitted Boot Slippers by River Island](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13073951_7091_289x246.jpg)

Ahhh so cute! Knitted faux fur lined slipper boots with a cute snowman print - gorgeous! I would love to relax at home in these slippers as I love anything with an unusual print. These would make a great belated Christmas gift for any relatives or friends you haven't yet seen over the festive period.

## 6. Nutmeg Bootie Slippers by ASOS

![Nutmeg Bootie Slippers by ASOS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13073952_7714_289x224.jpg)

These Fair Isle knit slipper boots come in a choice of two colors, navy or pink. I love the knitted upper, faux fur lining and cute pom poms - you could wear them all year round for cosy feet whatever the weather!

## 7. Panda Slippers by for Feet's Sake

![Panda Slippers by for Feet's Sake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/13073952_5584_349x390.jpg)

These slippers from ModCloth probably aren't to everyone's tastes, but I love how cute and odd they are! The slip on style and pom pom on the rear reminds me of traditional Greek slippers, whilst the fun panda knit and pink tassel just adds some extra cuteness.

I already have my favorite slippers on ready for cosying up at home, but if you haven't chosen a pair yet, hopefully this list will give you some ideas! Personally I love slipper boots as I find them cosier and easier to wear - I keep them on no matter what I'm doing around the house, like right now, as I'm writing! What style of slippers are your favorites - are you a mule or bootie girl?
