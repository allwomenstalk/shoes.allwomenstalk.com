# 7 Cute Cut\-out Shoes for Spring ...

Cut out shoes are a cheeky way to show some skin come spring. From high end designers to cheap chain store finds, cut out details are a must-have on your shoes this season. Whether you prefer ladylike ballet flats or preppy lace-ups, your chances of finding a pair with cheeky cut out details will be pretty good. Check out a few cute cut out shoes, below.

## 1. Cut out Oxford Shoes

![Cut out Oxford Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/1_cut-out-oxford-shoes_320x194.jpg)

Cut out oxford shoes are great for working a preppy look. These pastel multicoloured shoes give the illusion of cut outs thanks to clear panels at the sides. At around $140, these Rachel Antonoff for Bass shoes are pretty pricey, but you can find similar cut out shoes on a budget at Modcloth, Nasty Gal and F-Troupe.

## 2. Cut out Heels

![Cut out Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/2_cut-out-heels_326x304.jpg)

Make a statement in a pair of bold coloured cut out heels. They’d look great teamed with flippy skirts and dresses come spring. These berry heels by Restricted at Modcloth have a Mary Jane design with circle cut out details at the toe. They cost around $60, which is pretty reasonable considering cut out heels by Brian Atwood and Christian Louboutin will set you back a couple of hundred dollars.

## 3. Cut out Ballet Flats

![Cut out Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/3_cut-out-ballet-flats_298x98.jpg)

Ballet flats are a wardrobe staple. Cut out details can give an old favourite a new twist. A pair of cut out ballet flats would look great with a range of different outfits, from your most casual garb to something a bit dressier. You can grab a pair of cut out ballet flats for as little as $15 at Forever 21 or splurge a bit more on styles by Melissa and Tory Burch.

## 4. Cut out Ankle Strap Shoes

![Cut out Ankle Strap Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/4_cut-out-ankle-strap-shoes_336x456.jpg)

These sweet shoes are perfect for getting about in this spring. They have pretty cut outs at the toe and ankle, and come with a dainty strap across the ankle. The mint colour is on-trend for spring and would look great with pastel or neutral outfits. These shoes by Jeffrey Campbell will set you back around $100 but you can find more affordable alternatives from Sole Society.

## 5. Gold Cut out Shoes

![Gold Cut out Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/5_gold-cut-out-shoes_320x290.jpg)

Add a some glitz and glamour to your spring outfits with a hint of gold. These glittery cut out shoes from Modcloth are a great find. You could team them with anything from flirty sundresses to chic tuxedo-style trousers. You can also find a range of gold cut out shoes by American Apparel, Melissa, and Oscar de la Renta.

## 6. Cage Cut out Shoes

![Cage Cut out Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/6_cage-cut-out-shoes_600x290.jpg)

If you like your shoes with a preppy, Alexa Chung-inspired vibe then these cage cut out shoes may just be for you. They’d look great teamed with pinafore dresses, skinny jeans, or distressed denim shorts. These particular shoes are from Topshop and you can find similar styles at American Apparel and ASOS.

## 7. Cut out Wedge Shoes

![Cut out Wedge Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/02/7_cut-out-wedge-shoes_600x416.jpg)

Cut out wedges are a great option for both day and night. They would instantly smarten up a casual outfit and would look just as fitting with a sleek cocktail dress. These chic black wedges are by Jimmy Choo and currently on sale – down to $300 from $650 – at Bergdorfs. More budget-friendly cut out wedges can be found by Jeffrey Campbell and Forever 21.

A shoe with cut out details is a great way to update your spring outfit. From ballet flats to high heels, there’s a style of cut out shoe to suit all tastes. Which of these cut out shoes are your picks for spring?
