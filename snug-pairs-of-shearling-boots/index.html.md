# 7 Snug Pairs of Shearling Boots ...

With the temperature dropping at a steady pace, cozy shearling boots are must-have additions to your closet. From sky-high wedge booties to badass lace-up boots, there are endless shearling-lined or trimmed booties to choose from. Start strutting (in warmth, of course) after checking out these snug, stylish shearling booties and boots!

## 1. Lined Wedge Booties

![Lined Wedge Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_lined-wedge-booties_240x240.jpg)

One gorgeous pair of shearling-lined booties is this option from Burberry. Though you won’t find these at a bargain basement price, they’ll add style to any ensemble. Just call ‘em an investment piece! Pair them with opaque tights, a flirty mini, and a sleek black blazer for a trendy look you’ll love.

## 2. Cuffed Booties

Another steamy pair of shearling boots is Promiscuous’ Ismay. They’d look ultra haute with skinny jeans and a chunky sweater. Look for neutral colors like this one and compose a sleek but trendy ensemble. Thanks to the wedge, you'll still have stability and they're so hot you'll melt any ice or slush while you're strutting anyway.

## 3. Cuffed Tall Leather Boots

![Cuffed Tall Leather Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/3_cuffed-tall-leather-boots_480x360.jpg)

A supple leather exterior and toasty shearling lining is one coupling that’s both chic and cozy. These Ara Opal boots are a blessing for your toes and calves. Plus, they’re so versatile that you could wear them with anything from a casual dress to jeans and a tee.

## 4. Shearling-Trimmed Platform Boots

![Shearling-Trimmed Platform Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/4_shearling-trimmed-platform-boots_480x360.jpg)

With these daring shearling boots, you’ll make one unforgettable style statement. These LOVE Moschino platforms are prime for a night out on the town. They’ll take any club getup to new heights –literally.

## 5. Studded Harness Boots

![Studded Harness Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/5_studded-harness-boots_480x360.jpg)

Hey, stud! These Blowfish Kenessa CSH shearling boots are both badass and warm. Who said you’d have to sacrifice comfort for killer style? With the combination of the color and the studs, you can go a little bit country or a little bit rock 'n roll, depending on your mood.

## 6. UGG Tall Boots

![UGG Tall Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/6_ugg-tall-boots_480x360.jpg)

These aren’t last year’s plain old UGGs! The brand’s Rommy tall boots will keep you warm (even as everyone else is shivering in their sneakers). Comfort is key but they look utterly delish as well, so think about making another fashionable footwear investment this winter!

## 7. Baggy Leather Lace-Ups

_ One last pair of snug shearling boots is the Palladium Baggy Leather S lace-ups. They’d add kickass flair to your girlie girl dresses and leggings. Plus you can tell they're comfortable and cozy just by looking at them!

These are 7 of the cutest, coziest shearling booties on the market today. Which shearling boots do you love? Do you have any of your own styles to add?
