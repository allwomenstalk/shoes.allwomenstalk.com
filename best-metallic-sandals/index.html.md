# 13 Best Metallic Sandals ...

What girl could go wrong with a pair of metallic sandals? They’re neutral so you can wear them with virtually anything in your wardrobe. With so many pairs out there, it can be hard to decide which are worth the money. Here is a list of top-rated metallic sandals to choose from.

## 1. Tabbie T-Strap Sandals

![Tabbie T-Strap Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/08082203_7982_417x231.jpg)

If metallic sandals with a classic construction are what you’re after, you can’t go wrong with these T-strap versions for J.Crew. Ankle straps and a durable sole round out this great pair of summer footwear.

**Price:** $118 at [jcrew.com](https://goo.gl/SXO77G)

## 2. Belle by Sigerson Morrison Bobo 2

![Belle by Sigerson Morrison Bobo 2](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10044743_6224_521x219.jpg)

The Bobo 2 by Belle by Sigerson Morrison features a wedge heel and a lovely braided accent on the upper portion of the sandal. The additional materials are camel colored, creating a seamless look that’ll pair well with anything in your closet.

**Price:** $148 at [sigersonmorrison.com](https://goo.gl/XCTyGZ)

## 3. Sam Edelman Gigi Sandal

![Sam Edelman Gigi Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/08082139_6056_479x247.jpg)

If metallic snake print is your style, the Sam Edelman's Gigi Sandal in 'Gold Boa' will quickly become your favorite pair of shoes. A cushy sole makes them comfortable and stylish! They come in many other colors too, so you may want to stock up.

**Price:** $65 at [zappos.com](https://www.zappos.com/p/sam-edelman-kids-gigi-mini-little-kid-big-kid-saddle/product/9692894/color/616)

## 4. Cufff by Steve Madden

![Cufff by Steve Madden](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/08082137_3405_479x231.jpg)

The Cufff by Steve Madden combines the comfort of a thong design with the style of a metallic cuff. Brown and gold make this a pair of sandals you can wear with jeans, a dress or even with your swimsuit cover-up at the pool.

**Price:** $47.99 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Nice by Joie

![Nice by Joie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/08082315_4805_335x195.jpg)

This awesome metallic sandal is a thong slide and has a metallic upper that attaches to the toe slide with a stylish knot. These will soon become your go-to sandals since they are cute and comfortable.

**Price:** $125 at [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Navajo by Jack Rogers

![Navajo by Jack Rogers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10044805_5805_504x268.jpg)

Jack Roger's Navajo is the perfect mix of style and old-school design. It’s fashionable with a Native American feel on top. The metallic color is simple to pair with colors of any hue.

**Price:** $ 78.95 at [shoebuy.com](https://goo.gl/y5wwYG)}

## 7. Ancient Greek Sandals Medea Metallic Flat Sandals

![Ancient Greek Sandals Medea Metallic Flat Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/08082352_0876_335x214.jpg)

This sandal is a handmade design that has a fancy leather upper that may remind you of a tribal design. Its beautiful color and quality construction means you’ll be wearing these wonderful shoes for years and years to come.

**Price:** $240 at [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Tory Burch Emmy Metallic Leather Logo Thong Sandals

![Tory Burch Emmy Metallic Leather Logo Thong Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10044823_2674_460x345.jpg)

Tory Burch’s Emmy Metallic Leather Logo Thong Sandal will be your top pick if you enjoy wearing logos. The sandal is metallic on the upper portion as well as the cushy padded sole. Bling everywhere you look!

**Price:** $195 at [neimanmarcus.com](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. Loeffler Randall Star Sandal

![Loeffler Randall Star Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10044744_9099_599x345.jpg)

These metallic sandals feature an awesome tie design with lovely stars along the straps. When you put these beauties on, you’ll be the talk of the town.

**Price:** $175 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Agnyss by DV by Dolce Vita

![Agnyss by DV by Dolce Vita](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/08082601_1515_350x221.jpg)

A nautical theme and shiny silver color make these a great pair of sandals for a seaside getaway, but they work any day you want to wear them too. The strap is adjustable and features an easy to use buckle.

**Price:** $50 at [lordandtaylor.com](http://www.lordandtaylor.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Asos Formula Woven Sandals

![Asos Formula Woven Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10044742_7431_299x97.jpg)

A great combination of affordability and style, these sandals from ASOS are perfect. The criss cross design coupled with the ankle cuff make these a pair of shoes you’ll never stop loving.

**Price:** $32.67 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/1NaV5c).

## 12. K. Jaqcues Buffon Metallic Sandals

![K. Jaqcues Buffon Metallic Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10044843_1910_458x321.jpg)

These super stylish sandals from K. Jaqcues feature a beautiful lizard print on both the ankle straps and the thong strap. Calfskin, leather and a buckle make this a quality and easy to wear pair of shoes.

**Price:** $275 at [lyst.com](http://www.lyst.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Michael Michael Kors Viola Espadrille Sandal

![Michael Michael Kors Viola Espadrille Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10044743_6270_459x393.jpg)

These beauties from Michael Kors are a high wedge design that look awesome with dresses. Metallic detailing on both the ankle strap and the heel turn this shoe into something that can go from casual to formal with ease.

**Price:** $125 at [michaelkors.com](http://www.michaelkors.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
