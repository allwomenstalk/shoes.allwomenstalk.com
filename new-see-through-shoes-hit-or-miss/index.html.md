# 8 Trendy See\-Through Shoes for Women  Hit or Miss?

See-through shoes are making a major comeback this season, proving that the transparent trend is here to stay. We’re seeing it appear in everything from high-end runways to everyday street style. While it used to be all about see-through jewelry and accessories, we’re now seeing a massive shift toward transparent footwear. Check out a few of the newest see-through shoes to hit the shelves and learn how to style these [summer shoe trends](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/summer-shoe-trends/) like a pro.

| Style | Best For | Price Range |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Ballet Flats | Casual daytime wear | $ - $$ |
| Pumps | Night out / Formal | $$ - $$$$ |
| Jelly Sandals | Beach or poolside | $ |

## 1. Mesh Paneled Ballet Flats

![Mesh Paneled Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/1_mesh-paneled-ballet-flats_600x219.jpg)

Ease into the see-through shoes trend with a two-toned ballet flat. These shoes from [Forever 21](https://www.forever21.com) have a fun, colorful design and are very budget-friendly. The see-through panels work perfectly against the colored trims and caps. Wear them with faded denim jeans and a white t-shirt for a crisp, springtime outfit. If you love this silhouette, check out more [ways to wear ballet flats](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-wear-ballet-flats/) this season.

## 2. Clear Slingback Pumps

![Clear Slingback Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/2_clear-slingback-pumps_600x365.jpg)

Clear shoes are quite quirky and a great way to show off a fresh pedicure. Play up their quirkiness by choosing shoes with colorful or embellished accents. These shoes by [Sophia Webster](https://www.sophiawebster.com) come with a bright pink toe cap and polka-dot slingback heel. While high-end designer pairs can be an investment, you can often find similar bargain buys at Urban Outfitters or Charlotte Russe.

## 3. Clear Metallic Pumps

![Clear Metallic Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/3_clear-metallic-pumps_600x475.jpg)

For a night out on the town, try a pair of chic pumps on for size. You can’t go wrong with metallics, and these shoes feature clear panels down the sides that create a sleek, modern silhouette. Wear them with a tuxedo-inspired look for a contemporary take on evening wear. They are a fraction of the cost of what you’d pay for a luxury pair of Manolo Blahniks but still deliver that high-fashion impact.

## 4. Clear Jelly Bow Shoes

![Clear Jelly Bow Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/4_clear-jelly-bow-shoes_600x276.jpg)

If you’re after a casual yet dressy take on clear shoes, then these jelly bow shoes are worth a look. These flat shoes are entirely clear and feature oversized glitter bow details at the toes. Slip them on with a sundress for a sweet spring look. Brands like [Stuart Weitzman](https://www.stuartweitzman.com) offer premium versions, but you can find more affordable alternatives from contemporary labels like Fiebiger.

## 5. Mesh Paneled Peep Toe Booties

![Mesh Paneled Peep Toe Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/5_mesh-paneled-peep-toe-booties_600x580.jpg)

Work a fashion-forward look in a pair of see-through mesh booties. These are perfect for transitioning between seasons. Pair them with:

- Cigarette-style pants

- A silky blouse

- An embellished clutch

This creates a chic nighttime look that stands out. These booties are often available at Forever 21 for a much lower price than similar styles by Maison Margiela or Jeffrey Campbell.

## 6. Spiked Sandal

![Spiked Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/6_spiked-sandal_600x414.jpg)

Work an edgy angle with a pair of clear spiked sandals. Choosing sandals with clear panels gives the illusion of spikes floating on your skin, which is a bold fashion statement. These [Jeffrey Campbell](https://jeffreycampbellshoes.com) sandals are a cult favorite, but you can find nearly identical versions at budget-friendly retailers if you're looking to save.

## 7. Embellished PVC Slingbacks

![Embellished PVC Slingbacks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/7_embellished-pvc-slingbacks_600x389.jpg)

These clear shoes have a hint of Cinderella’s glass slippers to them. The clear slingback design is minimal and polished, but the embellished wedge heel adds a bit of glamour to the look. These [Valentino](https://www.valentino.com) shoes are a luxury staple, offering a high-end take on the PVC trend. You can also find similar, more affordable versions at major department stores like Nordstrom.

## 8. Glitter Gladiator Jelly Sandals

![Glitter Gladiator Jelly Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/8_glitter-gladiator-jelly-sandals_600x265.jpg)

Channel your childhood with a modern twist in a pair of jelly sandals. They are incredibly versatile and can be worn with anything from cute pinafores to a pair of distressed jeans. These clear glitter jelly sandals are a summer must-have and can be found at retailers like Topshop, Modcloth, or Nasty Gal.

Despite being see-through, these shoes can definitely pack a style punch. What do you think of these see-through shoes? Are they a hit or a miss for your wardrobe? Let us know which pair is your favorite!
