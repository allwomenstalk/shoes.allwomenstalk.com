# 7 Cute Shoes by Chinese Laundry ...

One of my favorite shoe brands is Chinese Laundry, and I’m always excited to see what’s new at their website, so I check it once or twice a month. They offer everything, from flats to platforms to boots, and everything in between, so there’s always something at the site to pique my interest and leave me wanting more! Here are some of my latest finds, the ones that currently reside near the top of my must-have list… these are 7 cute shoes by Chinese Laundry.

## 1. Hot Cakes Heel

Hey there, hot stuff! These are the platform heels for you to wear to strut your stuff. They’re available in cream or black, with clever cut-outs across the upper and a 5-inch stiletto heel. The black would look smashing with patterned tights and a slinky LBD, or with skinny jeans and a graphic-print tunic.

## 2. Lyndia Heel

![Lyndia Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v1/x0/z12w87py54c8f1dec69a0443174573_203x247.jpg)

## 3. Niko Heel

![Niko Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zs/e4/hdiuhpj554c8f1df8df2c232010608_224x248.jpg)

Gorgeous! Color-block leather abounds in blue and purple or red and yellow on these sleek pointed-toe pumps. I love the shine of the sleek patent leather details, and the eye-catching color combinations! Either pair would be beautiful with an LBD or as the focal point of any girls’-night-out ensemble.

## 4. Smooth Talker Wedge

**Price:** $69.00 at [chineselaundry.com](https://www.chineselaundry.com/home?utm_source=CJ&utm_medium=Affiliate&utm_campaign=6147293)
Get your groove on with these tall, chunky wedges, featuring three color choices (red, tan, or black), a cork-look heel, pretty peep toes, and thick ankle strap. Wear with cuffed denim shorts and a tucked-in tee, or with a cropped blazer and skinny jeans. The red ones look like so much fun!

## 5. Garlind Flat

**Price:** $49.00 at [chineselaundry.com](https://www.chineselaundry.com/home?utm_source=CJ&utm_medium=Affiliate&utm_campaign=6147293)
Add a little sass to your skinny denim and floral frocks with these zipper-edged black ballet flats, with elastic in the back for a perfect fit and a perky bow on the toe. I adore the zipper detailing around the bow, and the tiny, comfortable 1-inch heel. I wish these came in bright blue or purple!

## 6. Gisenna Flat

**Price:** $59.00 at [chineselaundry.com](https://www.chineselaundry.com/home?utm_source=CJ&utm_medium=Affiliate&utm_campaign=6147293)
How sweet and girlie! Add another layer of charm to your feminine wiles with these darling little skimmer flats, cove red in sheet rosettes. They’re soft and sweet, frothy and frilly, ideal for wearing with a menswear-inspired outfit or with your favorite spring dress.

## 7. Voltage Sandal

**Price:** $89.00 at [chineselaundry.com](https://www.chineselaundry.com/home?utm_source=CJ&utm_medium=Affiliate&utm_campaign=6147293)
These booties are an update of the classic granny shoe, with stacked heels and a modest platform, available in tan, brown, or black. They lace up, just like the original, but they also feature very modern cut-outs and a slip-resistant lug sole. Very urban!
