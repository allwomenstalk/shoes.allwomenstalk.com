# 7 Stylish Ways to Wear Riding Boots This Fall ...

As you’re preparing your credit cards for a fall shopping spree, you’ll need to know how to wear riding boots. Besides being a cold weather staple, riding boots can add some sleek to last year’s leftovers. Here are 7 ways to keep your boots on trend this fall. Giddyup!

## 1. Go Girly

If you’re a [girly girl](https://inspiration.allwomenstalk.com/signs-that-youre-far-from-a-girly-girl/) wondering how to wear riding boots, you’ll be pleased to know that you can pack on the ruffles, lace, and pearls. For example, I’d start with a frilly pink dress and then spice it up with sheer black tights and riding boots. You’ll still retain your feminine charm, but with a fashion forward edge.

## 2. Make It Military

Rock two trends at once by merging military-inspired staples with your leather riding boots. I’d wear my go-to black leather boots with an olive green military blazer and indigo skinnies. It’s perfect for casual Fridays at the office or dinner and drinks with the girls. I’ll salute to that!

## 3. Cool and Casual

Another way to wear riding boots is to keep your look simple. Riding boots can spruce up a casual look, making a cardigan, opaque leggings, and a satchel bag fit for a shopping trip. Throw your hair into a messy bun, swipe on lip gloss, and get those errands done!

## 4. Pretty in Preppy

**Good news:** You don’t have to cast away your belts, blazers, and shirt dresses if you’re planning to live in riding boots this season. Wear them together for a preppy lunch date look your beau will love.

## 5. Be Boho

Another way to wear riding boots is to go boho. Break out your favorite midi skirt, a patterned head wrap and a stack of bangles. The riding boots will keep your flirty and free ensemble in check.

## 6. City Chic

**Calling all city slickers:** One super stylish way to wear your riding boots is to pair them with black shorts, tights, and a Peter Pan-collared button down. These warm boots can take your summer pieces straight through fall, saving you money for those fancy Manhattan dinners.

## 7. Warm and Cozy

You’ll definitely want to know how to wear riding boots once the temperature really takes a dive! Once the frigid weather hits, I pair this classic with a chunky pullover and thick leggings. When venturing outside, a warm, lined pea coat is a must-have. Stay toasty!

These are 7 of the top ways to wear riding boots this fall. How will you wear yours? Do you have any of your tips to add?
