# 7 Stylish Sneakers ...

A good pair of sneakers is a must for any wardrobe. They can come in handy for long days spent walking or for finishing off a casual outfit. For the fashion conscious out there, a good-looking pair of sneakers is just as important. Below are just a few stylish sneakers for you to browse.

## 1. Ted Baker Raesa Satin Lace up Trainer

![Ted Baker Raesa Satin Lace up Trainer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wg/wo/gtao4g6u54c8f7cfdfc4d461830390_300x139.jpg)

**Price:** $98.62 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/O5hdL1)
These dusky pink sneakers are perfect for complementing a girlish outfit. They are made from a combination of satin materials and feature a contrasting toe cap. The shiny toe cap is a welcome addition to the overall design. These stylish sneakers would look great paired with denim pieces.

## 2. Converse Studded Canvas Sneakers

![Converse Studded Canvas Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yd/am/rk8p5to454c8f7d0ac984378923692_300x190.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9ddXxZ)
Add some attitude to your outfit with these studded sneakers. These hi top sneakers are a classic Converse design, and the stud detailing adds a modern and edgy twist. The stud detailing is subtle enough, however, to not overpower the overall design of the shoe. This clean white shoe will work well with a variety of different outfits.

## 3. Lanvin Metallic Cracked Leather Sneakers

![Lanvin Metallic Cracked Leather Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/n4/slxx5f7654c8f7d176dde699122657_300x171.jpg)

**Price:** $565 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/lSSHmE)
These sneakers are on the pricey side, but they’re the closest you’ll get to having your feet dripping in gold. The gold cracked leather is the stand out feature of this classically designed sneaker. The taupe grosgrain laces offset the metallic material perfectly. Play it cool and keep the rest of your outfit casual when wearing these sneakers.

## 4. Modcloth It’s Not Just a Plaid Shoe

![Modcloth It’s Not Just a Plaid Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eq/nx/g52bm2x054c8f7d245cb8262280903_300x144.jpg)

**Price:** $24.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These sneakers would make a preppy addition to any outfit. The plaid pattern material comes with the addition of a few hints of metallic thread. The flat, low-cut design is timeless. Make like you’re going to play tennis at the country club and pair these with a classic oxford tee and a short full skirt.

## 5. Forever 21 Floral Canvas Sneakers

![Forever 21 Floral Canvas Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x4/aa/kut11dus54c8f7d333245181558809_300x142.jpg)

**Price:** $10.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after a sneaker with a more quirky appearance, then try these on for size. The contrasting floral material works well with the blue denim-look body. These sneakers are made from canvas and have a textured rubber sole. Have fun with your outfit when wearing these stylish sneakers and pair them with rompers or flirty sun dresses.

## 6. Topshop Tracey Black Canvas Lace Hi Tops

![Topshop Tracey Black Canvas Lace Hi Tops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nc/t5/dl8cqnla54c8f7d3ea1d2572301278_300x190.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Fprf5n)
These sneakers would make an excellent and stylish wardrobe staple. They are made from black canvas material and feature gold-look buckles and zips. The chiffon laces are a delicate touch. The timeless color combination of gold and black means they could easily finish off a range out outfits.

## 7. Nike Flash Macro PRM Trainer

![Nike Flash Macro PRM Trainer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iv/c6/dvbwe9w454c8f7d4a5a50417706954_300x111.jpg)

**Price:** $89.65 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/RtSSck)
These Nike sneakers are sporty and stylish. Graphic panels line the body of the shoe, creating a unique stripe appearance. They also feature contrasting toe caps and the trademark ‘swoosh’ logo on the side. These stylish sneakers would work well paired with jeans and a slouchy blazer or cardigan.

These might not be the best sneakers for training or working out, but they’ll definitely do the trick for day-to-day activities. If it’s comfort dressing you’re after, these stylish sneakers will definitely do the trick. Which of the above do you prefer?
