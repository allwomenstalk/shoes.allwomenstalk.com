# 8 Marvelous Menswear\-Inspired Shoes ...

Menswear-inspired shoes are a marvelous (and oh-so-trendy) way to breathe life into last year’s pieces. Whether you rock blazers and satchel bags on the regular or are never seen without wearing a skirt, this trend is for everyone. Here are eight of my favorite menswear-inspired shoes on the market now. Some are heeled while others are flat and bow-adorned, but they’re all equally fabulous!

## 1. Gianni Bini Tux Menswear-Inspired Oxfords

![Gianni Bini Tux Menswear-Inspired Oxfords](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/1_gianni-bini-tux-menswear-inspired-oxfords_352x168.jpg)

These menswear-inspired shoes are glittery and gorgeous. They’re perfect for the gal who needs a dose of shimmer in her life! I’d pair them with black tights, a flared skirt, and a button down blouse for a look that’s daring but feminine. Get them now on Dillard’s for $49.99.

## 2. Gomax Women’s Oksana-04 Oxford Pump

![Gomax Women’s Oksana-04 Oxford Pump](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/2_gomax-womens-oksana-04-oxford-pump_352x395.jpg)

Whether you’re preparing to hit the club or meet your beau for date night, you’ll look stunning (and tall!) with these beautiful blue oxford pumps on your feet. I’d wear these sexy 4-inch heels with skintight leather skinny jeans and an embellished tunic for an ensemble he’ll never forget. Buy them now on Amazon for a mere $36.22.

## 3. Seychelles Women’s Dearest Ankle Boot

![Seychelles Women’s Dearest Ankle Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/3_seychelles-womens-dearest-ankle-boot_220x258.jpg)

Don’t these look like a boot-loving girl’s dream shoe? Go boho by pairing them with a flowing maxi dress, infinity scarf, and vintage pea coat. Or, wear ‘em to class with skinny jeans and an off-the-shoulder black blouse. Find these now on Amazon for $101.96.

## 4. Cole Haan Women’s Alisa Oxfords

![Cole Haan Women’s Alisa Oxfords](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/4_cole-haan-womens-alisa-oxfords_353x288.jpg)

These maple sugar shoes are ultra sweet and effortlessly chic. And the neon blue accents give them a bit of edge –pretty perfect, huh? I’d wear ‘em with grey tights and a pretty patterned dress for a city slicker spring look. Get these now on Macy’s for $129.99.

## 5. DV by Dolce Vita Women’s Marcel Loafer

![DV by Dolce Vita Women’s Marcel Loafer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/5_dv-by-dolce-vita-womens-marcel-loafer_500x250.jpg)

If you’ve been scouring the Web for a lovely pair of loafers to go with your blazers and skinny jeans, the search ends with this find. The mint green hue is both fresh and fashionable while the tassels keep things fun and funky. Buy this pair now on 6pm for $43.49.

## 6. BC Dime Store Oxford

![BC Dime Store Oxford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/6_bc-dime-store-oxford_391x422.jpg)

These two-tone oxford booties will keep your toes trendy all season long. I’d wear them with opaque tights and a patterned frock for a shopping date with the girls. Find this pair now on Swell for $72.99.

## 7. Restricted Roosevelt Flat

![Restricted Roosevelt Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/7_restricted-roosevelt-flat_300x168.jpg)

These oxfords are the real deal, topped off with a girly ribbon bow accent. Take that, boys! I’m planning to wear mine to class with a casual dress, tights, and a touch of lip gloss and mascara. Throw in a fitted blazer to take your menswear-inspired look up a notch. You can find these sweet shoes on DSW for $42.95.

## 8. Melter Loafer Black Leather

![Melter Loafer Black Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/01/8_melter-loafer-black-leather_600x423.jpg)

These black leather loafers come complete with gold tone studs, which add just the right amount of badass to a classic style. I’d pair them with a pleated leather mini skirt, button down shirt, and colored opaque tights for a going out ensemble that rocks. Find them now on Jildor Shoes for $112.
