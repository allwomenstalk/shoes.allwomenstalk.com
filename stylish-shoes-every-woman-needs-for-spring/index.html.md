# 10 Stylish Shoes Every Woman Needs for Spring ...

As March is quickly coming to an end and winter temps are slowly increasing it can only mean one thing--spring time! Everybody can't wait to pull out their trendy sandals and comfy flats and put the winter boots away, FOR GOOD! Here are just some of the spring shoes every girl has to have to make her spring more stylish than ever!

## 1. Casual

![footwear,shoe,leg,spring,sandal,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/p4/goric_footwear_shoe_leg_spring_sandal_500x500.jpg)

Nothing beats a pair of washed-out boyfriend jeans with some brown leather sandals. These casual sandals are perfect for the spring temps and will look great with any outfit! Total Boho-Chic.

## 2. Poka-dot Wedges

![footwear,clothing,leg,shoe,dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/jf/phqpd_footwear_clothing_leg_shoe_dress_600x600.jpg)

If these don't scream out "SPRING", I don't know what does. These polka-dot wedges are perfect for the warmer weather and would look great with an Easter dress or even some white jeans.

## 3. Ballet Flats

![color,footwear,white,black,blue,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/lo/b2uvd_color_footwear_white_black_blue_464x531.jpg)

Ballet flats are perfect for the spring time. I love these! The browns/cheetah prints go perfect with the purple bows, especially with the dark skinny jeans! Adorable.

## 4. Pastel Chucks

![footwear,white,blue,pink,shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/lt/pqc7e_footwear_white_blue_pink_shoe_475x713.jpg)

You can never go wrong with a pair of Chuck Taylors. The pastel colors are super cute and perfect for spring. These purple ones are to-die for!

## 5. Open-Toe Booties

If you want to dress it up this spring you can add these open-toe booties to any outfit. They would be cute with a pair of jeans, a cute floral skirt, or even a sundress!

## 6. Nude Heels

![footwear,white,high heeled footwear,leg,beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mi/vs/pjmyh_footwear_white_high-heeled-footwear_leg_beauty_433x650.jpg)

Nude heels never go out of style - especially in the Spring. Pair them with a stylish sundress or keep it casual and comfy with just a pair of washed jeans. You can never go wrong.

## 7. Floral. Floral. Floral

![footwear,shoe,leg,spring,sandal,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/vg/ma196_footwear_shoe_leg_spring_sandal_400x397.jpg)

Boat shoes have always been a must for spring shoes. What's even better is FLORAL PRINTED boat shoes! These are adorable!

## 8. Jack Rogers Sandals

Silver or Gold sandals with a pair of white jeans is the perfect combination for spring!

## 9. CheetaH-licious

![color,red,blue,yellow,footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p9/uq/m4rpk_color_red_blue_yellow_footwear_600x900.jpg)

I am obsessed with these cheetah Vans. They are the perfect accessory to any outfit and would even look adorable with a pair of orange jeans.

## 10. Turquoise Sandals

![footwear,clothing,blue,jeans,shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bq/ac/sk6lq_footwear_clothing_blue_jeans_shoe_500x750.jpg)

These shoes made a HUGE statement standing alone. I love how she rolled up the jeans to show off the shoes more. This outfit would even be cute with just a plain white T-Shirt. Genius.
