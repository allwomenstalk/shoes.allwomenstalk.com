# 7 Elegant Bridal and Evening Shoes from Stuart Weitzman ...

Like Christian Louboutin’s signature red sole, shoes from Stuart Weitzman are known for their unusual materials, including wallpaper and even 24-karat gold! Celebrities spotted in a pair of Stuart Weitzman’s are Kourtney Kardashian, Jennifer Lawrence, Blake Lively, Emma Stone, Jennifer Garner, and Rita Ora. Shoes from Stuart Weitzman don’t come cheap. Expect to pay over $400 a pair, and even up to the high hundreds.

## 1. Boodle

![Boodle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/em/aj/qm6ee2z8_480x312.jpg)

This gorgeous pair of shoes from Stuart Weitzman comes in gold as featured, plus black and beige. Not entirely detectable from this image, but the bow is beautifully constructed. The heel is 3 ½ inches. The shoes are so special, that I would consider getting them first, and then building my evening look around them.

## 2. Lollipop

![Lollipop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t5/sp/f9dikpab_480x312.jpg)

These shoes also come in silver, but this color, called Flesh Cipria, caught my eye. The heel is in the same material as the shoe, and the height is 4 inches. I like this color (but not what he named it) for a bridesmaid or a wedding attendee, and the silver for a glamourous night on the town.

## 3. Bow Goes up

![Bow Goes up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/bq/obw6eo7v_480x312.jpg)

The color is royal, the material satin, on this sexy evening T-strap sandal. This one is 4 1/4 inches tall. I like the side-view with the thin double strap thing going on. The design is complex and beautiful. Wear this with any color and it will be the focal point of your outfit.

## 4. Showbiz

![Showbiz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/so/dl/cr9alrl6_480x312.jpg)

This is the pale gold and the only thing about this that’s pale is the color. It alternates between shimmery leather and mesh, and it has a cool back zip closure. From a distance, the mesh part won’t be noticeable and it will look like the shoe has actual cut-outs. Nice trick, Weitzman!

## 5. Truelove

![Truelove](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uo/au/e7z31ts8_480x312.jpg)

Now you certainly didn’t think I would get to #7 without showing a black shoe, did you? Well, ‘Truelove’ indeed! Specifics include: back zip closure, 4 ¼ inch heel, and braided straps. These satin heels are luxurious in silver as well. I couldn’t just save these for a special occasion. They’re far too sexy and spectacular not to wear more often.

## 6. Lyrics

![Lyrics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/ge/frs394ys_480x312.jpg)

Lyrics is a beautiful bridal shoe in ivory satin, and it does come in four other colors, including navy. This shoe is a comfortable 3 ½ inches, and should allow you to dance your big night away in relative ease. I love the mesh inset with rhinestone accents that give just enough oomph to this feminine shoe.

## 7. Sinful

![Sinful](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/v9/l0gvcoad_480x312.jpg)

Let’s really party! I love the name ‘Sinful’ for these 4 ½ inch heels that you can rock in either silver or pale gold. I love a thin T-strap on a sexy, fancy evening shoe and this one is just perfect. The metallic leather offers such a high shine, it could double as a mirror!

These shoes are from the bridal and evening collection of Stuart Weitzman, but he also makes affordable (under $100) shoes for casual and business wear. I’m not that big on red shoes, but the patent leather Mary Janes are a beautiful exception. Those are available in a variety of colors and textures and worth checking out at Zappos, which has all of the shoes featured in this article. Have you ever tried a pair of Stuart Weitzman shoes? Would any of these be a contender for your shoe wardrobe?
