# 8 Comfortable and Stylish Flats for Spring ...

Oh spring, how we love thee! The warmer weather, the flowers, the birds, and the fact that we can finally put our warm clothes and boots away and make way for pretty sandals and flats. Here's guest contributor [Lorna Burford](https://plus.google.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) with her picks for some rather stylish flats for spring.

Spring will be right around the corner very soon and one of the things that's always on my to-buy list is flat shoes, comfortable ones to be exact. As the winter draws to a close and the weather warms up, I often find myself wanting to go out a lot more and walk around, especially to the parks to see the changing scenery, the blossoms on the trees and flower buds making their way through the soil. Since I am very much into fashion, being a long time [fashion and personal style blogger](https://goo.gl/iv50bB), my footwear always needs to look good, be on trend and go with my outfit, so I have also chosen a few options that are fun as well as classic!

## 1. Bloch Ballet Flats

![Bloch Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/hf/de/drnnvqd6_599x324.jpg)

Bloch makes some incredibly comfortable flats! They are known for being a dance shoe company, but they apply that comfort to their street wear shoes as well. I personally own 3 Bloch flats and they are elasticated and really soft, making for a very comfortable shoe. These are in black, so they will be very versatile.

## 2. Tory Burch Eddie Flats

![Tory Burch Eddie Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j6/jj/mp/iz51zj67_600x399.jpg)

Tory Burch's Eddie flats are known for being really soft and easy to wear. They are one of those classic flats that come in a variety of neutral colours and are always commented on for looking great. They are easy to walk in and very comfortable. These fabulous brown ones will go with practically anything!

## 3. Tieks Foldable Flats

![Tieks Foldable Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/se/cm/vcpx36xx_599x425.jpg)

Tieks have a great reputation for being one of the most fantastic soft and flat shoe retailers. Their classic flats come in a huge variety of colours, from neons to pastels to classics and are spoken about widely for being comfortable and easy to fold up to carry around with you.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Ballet Flats

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wc/ds/yf/y39k4bzj_599x359.jpg)

The last classic pair of flats I have chosen here is the Marc by Marc Jacobs two tone ballet flats. These are a cream and black mix and look really soft, supple and comfortable, as well as having a neutral colour palette to go with any outfit! Especially skinny jeans!

## 5. Marc by Marc Jacobs Neoprene Pink Mouse Flats

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Neoprene Pink Mouse Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/rh/ml/nwl2vek2_459x257.jpg)

Now on to something a little more fun. These Marc by Marc Jacobs neoprene neon pink mouse flats are adorable! Everyone needs some fun shoes in their closet and these will definitely spruce up any outfit! They look comfortable because they are neoprene and they look oh-so-stylish! You simply can't go wrong with those!

## 6. Tory Burch Abbe Crochet Espadrilles

![Tory Burch Abbe Crochet Espadrilles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/f9/wl/ly3dwgvs_459x349.jpg)

The Tory Burch Abbe crochet espadrilles are a newer style for spring! Much like the famous Chanel espadrilles, these have lace crochet all over them instead, so your feet can breathe. Espadrilles are always comfortable and since these are white, I can imagine them looking amazing with shorts, dresses, skirts and jeans!

## 7. Yosi Samra Leopard Foldable Flats

![Yosi Samra Leopard Foldable Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xn/sa/nw/r8gbk7ml_459x210.jpg)

Yosi Samra is another brand that I love for flat shoes! They fold up into a ball, have an elasticated back and are really comfortable and easy to walk in. This particular pair in leopard print is adorable! They would look great with black and dark blue, with skinny jeans or shorts! Leopard print is always stylish!

## 8. Marc by Marc Jacobs Bull Dog Loafers

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Bull Dog Loafers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eg/t5/lm/yhit8j2f_459x212.jpg)

Last but not least, the Marc by Marc Jacobs French Bulldog Loafers that are incredibly cute! Loafers are a great way to dress up a day time look as they add a little bit of structure and strength to an outfit. Combining the black and white with the bulldog print is such a fun way to liven up an outfit!
