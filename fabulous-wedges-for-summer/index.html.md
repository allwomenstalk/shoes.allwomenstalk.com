# 7 Fabulous Wedges for Summer 2012 ...

If you don’t have your **wedges for summer 2012 yet, my** darling, what are you waiting for? All of the designers have them ready to wear, and they look fantastic with everything from wide-leg jeans to flirty skirts and midi-dresses. Not sure which wedges to want? I can help. I’ve scoured the web and found these, 7 fabulous wedges for summer 2012. Where to start? How about here…?

Try these lemon-zest patent leather wedges for summer 2012 with a floral-print top and a pair of natural linen shorts… so pretty! Or be daring and wear them with something pure white, with bright gold accessories to match the buckles at your ankles.

![Michael Kors Ariana Ostrich-Effect Leather Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/2_michael-kors-ariana-ostrich-effect-leather-wedge-sandals_460x514.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/b5G03O)
Be taller instantly with these 5 inch heels with another inch-and-a-half platform. The ostrich-inspired texture adds interest, and the color is so sweet! I love how these wedges for summer 2012 look with black or white... or, as the designer suggests, black and white polka dots.

## 3. Proenza Schouler Leather and Cork Wedge Sandals

![Proenza Schouler Leather and Cork Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/3_proenza-schouler-leather-and-cork-wedge-sandals_460x354.jpg)

**Price:** $995.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/LcXImg)
Part wedge, part gladiator, all chic! Wear these with a crisp white blouse and a pair of denim shorts, or with a maxi dress and wood bead necklace. I love the earth tones… gorgeous!

## 4. Jimmy Choo Pearl Striped Leather and Suede Platform Wedges

![Jimmy Choo Pearl Striped Leather and Suede Platform Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/4_jimmy-choo-pearl-striped-leather-and-suede-platform-wedges_460x546.jpg)

**Price:** $1,095.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/sgwe5X)
I have so many fond childhood memories of Fruit Stripe Gum and of rainbow sherbet, **and these wonderful wedges for summer 2012** take me back. Choose one of the brilliant hues, and play it up with a pair of shorts to match and a plain white top.

## 5. Missoni Leather and Crochet-Knit Platform Sandals

![Missoni Leather and Crochet-Knit Platform Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/5_missoni-leather-and-crochet-knit-platform-sandals_460x351.jpg)

**Price:** $785.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/RyN7Dg)
These cleverly detailed Boho-inspired sandals are the **wedges for summer 2012**! I love the blues and greens, all embroidered on the wedges… they’ll look great with everything from a linen shirt-dress to a pair of jeans to a white top and linen shorts.

## 6. Charlotte Olympia Meredith Striped Canvas Wedge Sandals

![Charlotte Olympia Meredith Striped Canvas Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/6_charlotte-olympia-meredith-striped-canvas-wedge-sandals_460x581.jpg)

How do I love these? Let me count the ways - the black and white contrast with the red detail at the bottom? The sweet bow at the toe? The towering but totally walkable wedge? Or the dainty ankle strap?

## 7. Yves Saint Laurent Saint Malo Patent-Leather Wedge Sandals

![Yves Saint Laurent Saint Malo Patent-Leather Wedge Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/7_yves-saint-laurent-saint-malo-patent-leather-wedge-sandals_460x434.jpg)
