# 7 Essential Shoes for Your Wardrobe ...

There are some essential shoes that every woman should own. These shoes are the classic styles that will last you year in and year out. They’re versatile enough to be worn with a range of different outfits and won’t succumb to trends. Once you have some of these essential shoes in your wardrobe, you’ll be set for any occasion. Take a look at some essential shoes for your wardrobe, below.

## 1. Basic Black Pumps

Every woman needs a basic black pump in her wardrobe. These essential shoes can be dressed up or down, worn to work or for play, and will match nearly any outfit combination your wardrobe can throw at them. Choose a shoe with a classic almond toe shape, slim stiletto heel, and in a quality leather or leather-look finish. You can also up the ante with shoes in a sleek patent finish or lush suede.

## 2. Ballet Flats

Ballet flats are another essential shoe for your wardrobe. They are elegant and ladylike, and can be worn with dresses, jeans, and everything in between. Ballet flats are perfect for wearing during the day but can still look polished. When it comes to choosing ballet flats for your wardrobe you can’t go wrong with versatile colours like black, blush, or tan.

## 3. Tall Boots

Tall boots are a must for your autumn and winter wardrobes. They’ll protect you from the elements and look super polished as well. Boots in black, brown, or tan colours are your best bet. Stick to simple, fuss-free designs, as they are the ones that tend to be more timeless. It might take a while to find just the right boots for you, but once you’ve found them there’ll be no looking back.

## 4. Flat Sandals

Let your feet breathe easy in the summer months with a pair of flat sandals. Sandals with simple, strappy designs in tan or black leather-look finishes will go well with most outfits. They’re more polished than your casual flip-flops but can be just as comfortable. Keep an eye out for sandals with plaited or embellished details for something a bit different, too.

## 5. Casual Sneakers

Every now and then your feet need a break. A pair of casual sneakers is a great option and can look effortlessly chic as well. Brands like Converse, Vans, and Superga all design classic, chic looking sneakers. Wear them with denim shorts for a cool and casual look or to dress down a flirty sun dress.

## 6. Statement Heels

A pair of statement heels will instantly jazz up your outfit. These can vary depending on your own personal style. However, they’re usually dressy heels that look great when teamed with anything from party dresses to jeans. Choose ones in colourful or metallic finishes, peep-toe or strappy designs, or with stiletto or wedge heels – the choice is entirely yours! Just remember that they’re the kind of shoes that everyone should instantly recognise as ‘you.’

## 7. Wellies

Not all shoes are designed to brave the elements. All-weather boots like wellington boots (wellies) are your best bet for hitting the outdoors and braving the elements. A shiny black pair will usually do the trick, but if you’re after something a bit more fun you can’t go wrong with bright colours or patterns.

These are just some essential shoes for your wardrobe. From a polished black pump to a casual sneaker, you’ll always have something to pair your outfits with as long as you have these shoes in your wardrobe. What shoes do you think are essential for any wardrobe?
