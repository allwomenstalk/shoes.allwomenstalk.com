# 7 Party Perfect Shoes under 100 ...

Party perfect shoes can make or break an outfit. Depending on the type of party, you may want something ultra glam or more low key. Choosing the right party perfect shoe can be just as complicated as completing a mathematical equation! However, there are a few styles of shoes out there at the moment that would make the perfect addition to a party outfit. Take a look at the following shoes for party perfect feet.

## 1. Nude Glitter Open Sandal

![Nude Glitter Open Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/1_nude-glitter-open-sandal_300x289.jpg)

**Price:** $69.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Ever since Miu Miu released their collection of glitter shoes, glitter has been high on the radar. This is no different when it comes to party perfect shoes. These shoes have a light gold glitter finish and crossover straps. They’d look great worn with anything from a simple black cocktail dress to a colourful evening kaftan.

## 2. Skinny Strap Sandals

![Skinny Strap Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/2_skinny-strap-sandals_300x303.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ZOp7OJ)
A good metallic heel is just one way to vamp up your party look. This season we’re seeing a lot of emphasis placed on skinny strap sandals, like these Topshop ones. They feature rose gold and silver metallic straps as well as a glitter ankle strap and heel. Wear them with cropped pants to show off the detailing.

## 3. Velveteen Platform Pumps

_ **Price:** $32.80 at forever21.com_
Make a statement with these party perfect shoes. They come in a boisterous fuchsia colour with a lush velveteen finish. The chunky heel is great if you prefer a bit more stability in your shoes. These shoes would look great worn with a simple shift dress and a metallic clutch.

## 4. Black Currant Heel

![Black Currant Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/4_black-currant-heel_300x264.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you prefer your party look to have a more vintage twist, then these shoes just might be what you’re after. They have an enclosed to with cutout details, a slim ankle strap, and mid heel. These would look especially lovely teamed with a pleated skirt and slinky top.

## 5. Peep Toe Ankle Strap Wedges

_ **Price:** $29.80 at forever21.com_
Add some extra height with these party perfect shoes. They come in a neutral taupe colour so would be well suited to parties held during the daytime. A wedge heel like this would be ideal for outdoor parties where you’re on grass or a surface where stilettos would normally sink. Wear these shoes with a cute white dress and metallic accessories for a fresh look.

## 6. Cross Front Low Wedges

![Cross Front Low Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/6_cross-front-low-wedges_300x207.jpg)

**Price:** $57.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These low wedges are a great alternative to a skinny stiletto or platform heel. This ‘flatform’ style of shoe is also having a bit of a trending moment. Perfect for a more casual affair when it comes to parties, wear these with jeans, a statement top, and eye catching jewellery.

## 7. Black Wedge Court Shoes

![Black Wedge Court Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/05/7_black-wedge-court-shoes_300x278.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These are the kind of shoes that can take you from the office to the bar. They have a classic court shoe design and come with a wedge heel. Basic black is very versatile, and these party perfect shoes could be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

From strappy stilettos to mid heel shoes, these are just a few party perfect shoes to take you through a variety of occasions. For versatility, choose a shoe in a neutral or metallic colour. If you want to stand out from the crowd then a brightly coloured shoe is perfectly on trend. What’s your idea of the party perfect shoe?
