# 9 Studded Flat Shoes ...

Studded details have become standard features on the fashion scene. They adorn a range of items from jackets to jewellery, and are guaranteed to add a tough edge to your outfit. Studded shoes are an easy and unexpected way to wear the trend. Take a look at a few studded flat shoes below.

These studded flat shoes come in the brand’s cult ‘mouse’ design. The gold stud embellishments, however, add a tough edge to the quirky design. These shoes are made from black leather and feature stud detailing around the foot and to the front. These shoes would be fairly versatile so could be worn with a range of outfits.

## 2. Topshop Miracle Grey Stud Bow Pumps

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/A6ma0p)
These grey pumps feature large studded suede bows at the toe. They are made from a neutral grey leather and have a contrasting sole. The harshness of the studs provides a nice contrast to the softness of the suede bow. These would look great paired with full midi skirts and dresses.

## 4. Lanvin Stud and Jewel Sandals

## 5. Topshop Kosy Black Studded Slippers

**Price:** $110.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/NzycIQ)
These black suede slippers have a certain masculine style to them. They have suede body, leather trims, and slight block heel. Stud details can be found at the toe. Team these with pegged trousers and a loose-fitting blouse for a street-smart look.

## 6. F-Troupe Studded Flat Leather Sandals

**Price:** $147.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/82h8UZ)
These flat leather sandals are a great choice of summer footwear. They feature wide leather straps to the uppers and bold studded detailing. The studs adorn the straps and the heels as well. Wear these studded flat shoes under this season’s pleated and sheer maxi skirts.

## 7. Christian Louboutin Louis Studded Patent-Leather Sneaker

**Price:** $835.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9g0TDy)
These studded sneakers are a sporty take on the trend. They are made from black patent leather and have an all-over stud embellishment. They also have black laces and the label’s signature red sole. Wear these with jeans and a blazer for an effortless daytime look.

## 8. ASOS Lily Pin Studded Pointed Ballet Pumps

**Price:** $43.10 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/DczHre)
This burnt orange ballet pumps are a stylish option. They have a microsuede finish and a pointed toe with pin-stud detailing. The combination of the gold-tone studs and burnt orange body is quite fetching. These would look great worn with a black leather circle skirt and white button-up blouse.
