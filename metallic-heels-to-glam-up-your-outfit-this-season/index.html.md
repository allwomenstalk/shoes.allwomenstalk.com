# 9 Metallic Heels to Glam up Your Outfit This Season ...

Metallic heels are a guaranteed way to glam up your outfits this season. Whether you prefer gold or silver, there are plenty of different styles of shoes to choose from. Stick to a classic look in pointed pumps or go for a more on-trend approach in chunky platform heels. Whatever the case, these metallic heels will make sure you’re putting your best foot forward this season.

## 1. Gold Pointed Court Shoes

![Gold Pointed Court Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/08/1_gold-pointed-court-shoes_600x655.jpg)

Metallic heels can be a wardrobe staple if you let them. A pair of gold leather pumps could look great worn during the day or dressed up at night. These pointed court shoes from Topshop are a stylish option. Team them with a shirt dress and a structured handbag during the day or with cropped tuxedo trousers and a slinky top for a chic evening look.

## 2. Silver Square Toe Sandals

![Silver Square Toe Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/08/2_silver-square-toe-sandals_600x801.jpg)

Make a statement in these silver sandals. They have a minimalist design with a thick strap around the ankle and at the toe. The chunky heel is also on-trend for the season. Wear these shoes to balance out a feminine dress or to team with tailored shorts. These silver heels will set you back $130 from Topshop but you can also try ASOS for a similar version that’s around half the price.

## 3. Silver Low Block Heeels

![Silver Low Block Heeels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/08/3_silver-low-block-heeels_600x308.jpg)

If you prefer your heels on the lower end of the scale then opt for a low block heeled shoe instead. These shoes from ASOS have a vintage-inspired design and a modern metallic leather-look finish. They cost around $60 and you could wear them with a full-skirted dress and cat eye sunglasses for a glamorous take on daytime dressing.

## 4. Gold Sling Back Heels

Get glammed up in these shiny gold heels from Zara. They have a sling back design with cross-over front straps and chunky block heels. Try wearing these shoes with a print blouse and tailored white shorts for a polished, put-together daytime look. You can currently grab them on sale online for $50.

## 5. Gold Glitter Pumps

![Gold Glitter Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/08/5_gold-glitter-pumps_349x311.jpg)

Would you like some sparkle to go with that high shine metallic finish? Then what about a pair of glitter shoes? These Modcloth babies are a stylish option and will only set you back around $40. They come in a fairly classic design and would look great worn with anything from vintage dresses to jeans and a tee.

## 6. Two-Tone Metallic Pumps

These heels will light up your daytime and night time looks. They have slight vintage feel to them and would look great worn with a ladylike midi dress during the day or with a black shift dress at night. These Marni shoes are quite pricey though at around $700! For a more budget friendly alternative I suggest checking out ASOS for a similar, low-heeled style.

## 7. Gold Curved Strap Sandals

![Gold Curved Strap Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/08/7_gold-curved-strap-sandals_600x617.jpg)

Got some cash to splash? Then check out these metallic strappy sandals by Jimmy Choo. They come in a pale gold colour and feature curved straps throughout the foot and double fastenings at the ankle. They’d look great worn with a simple black cocktail dress or cropped pants and a silk blouse.

## 8. Silver Pointed Loafers

![Silver Pointed Loafers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/08/8_silver-pointed-heels_600x274.jpg)

These silver shoes have quite a statement-making design. They have a pointed toe and low block heel, and are made from a high shine leather-look material. They’re from ASOS and will set you back around $60. Wear them during the day with faded boyfriend jeans and a button-up top for a fashion-forward daytime look.

## 9. Silver Peep Toe Pumps

![Silver Peep Toe Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/08/9_silver-peep-toe-pumps_328x334.jpg)

Looking for a cute shoe to team with your favourite party dress? Then check out these silver peep toe pumps from Forever 21. They have a metallic textured outsole, hidden platform, and bow detail at the toe. They won’t break the bank either, coming in at a cool $21.

Metallic heels can instantly give your outfits the wow factor. This season, there’s no shortage of metallic heels for you to choose from. Which of these metallic heels would you like lighting up your shoe collection?
