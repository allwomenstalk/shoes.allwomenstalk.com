# 7 Block Heel Shoes ...

Chunky, square heels are becoming more prominent on the shoe scene. Called block heels, they can be found on a range of shoes from platform heels to ballet pumps. They could be described as being the polar opposite to the stiletto heel. The block heeled look is modern, fresh, and directional. Below are a few block heel shoes to take inspiration from.

## 1. Sigerson Morrison Asymmetric Cut out Suede Sandals

![Sigerson Morrison Asymmetric Cut out Suede Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/uz/e2nm4rjz54c92e2c8b7e4170926758_300x230.jpg)

**Price:** $450.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/s1EhKx)
These all black shoes are elegant and sophisticated. Whereas other block heel shoes are designed to be bold attention-grabbing, these are quite understated. They feature asymmetric cut outs, an open round toe, and buckle fastening ankle strap. These shoes could be worn casually during the day, or dressed up at night.

## 2. Carvela Annie Platform Block Heeled Sandal

![Carvela Annie Platform Block Heeled Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o2/hi/g1c6pk0l54c92e2d8c675918357168_300x318.jpg)

**Price:** $251.02 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/MHwv2F)
These suede slingback sandals are perfect for spring. The rust colour would work well with a colour palette of creams and spring brights. These sandals feature cross-over straps, platform sole, and buckle fastening at the ankle. Wear them with cuffed trousers for a casual, chic look.

## 3. Topshop Seventy Black Suede Cut out Front Block Heel Shoes

![Topshop Seventy Black Suede Cut out Front Block Heel Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/d7/mi7bvltv54c92e2e62cad718230269_300x272.jpg)

**Price:** $145.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/oj5nVk)
These Topshop shoes manage to incorporate both the cut out and block heel trends. These block heel shoes feature front cut out details, a closed toe, and slingback design. They are made from leather and have a wooden heel. These shoes would look great paired with midi skirts and slouchy knits.

## 4. Modcloth Catwalk Queen Heel

![Modcloth Catwalk Queen Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/z8/h84zrdh854c92e2f33567549870534_300x280.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I adore the rich plum colour of these shoes. These shoes feature a 1 inch platform and a heel that measures 4.5 inches. At this height, the chunky block heel provides added support. Pair these shoes with a full skirted dress, tailored blazer, and matching plum lips.

## 5. Bally Alicya Block-Heel Leather Peep-Toe Pumps

![Bally Alicya Block-Heel Leather Peep-Toe Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/et/nylcqevf54c92e300a170537242579_300x280.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/2fhAKo)
These shoes are sleek and sophisticated. The peep-toe pump is a classic, and the addition of the block heel gives the design a directional twist. These shoes are made from black leather and feature a gold-tipped wooden heel. These shoes would make a stylish addition to any wardrobe and can be worn in both corporate and casual settings.

## 6. Asos Shamble Block Heel Town Shoe

![Asos Shamble Block Heel Town Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/le/jlmj1ytw54c92e30d4d1e383599122_300x165.jpg)

Features include contrasting canvas and leather look panels, perforated detailing, and an almond toe. The soft pink and brown colours would work delightfully well with an on-trend pastel colour palette.
