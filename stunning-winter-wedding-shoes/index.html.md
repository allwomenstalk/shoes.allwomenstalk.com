# 7 Stunning Winter Wedding Shoes ...

If you’re planning to get hitched in the coming weeks, these winter wedding shoes will keep your feet both warm and stylish. From traditional white pumps to breakout blue stilettos, there are options for every bride-to-be out there. With these 7 picks, preparing to walk down the aisle will get a little less stressful.

## 1. Touch Ups Candice

![Touch Ups Candice](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_touch-ups-candice_304x304.jpg)

You’ll shimmer and shine in these glitter-covered pumps. For a mere $59, you’ll wow the crowds on the dance floor (even if you can barely bust a move). Find these and similar styles on Zappos for a wedding purchase you won’t regret when you look through the photographs.

## 2. Everything’s Aglow Heel in Silver

![Everything’s Aglow Heel in Silver](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/2_everythings-aglow-heel-in-silver_300x300.jpg)

These casual winter wedding shoes are perfect for the gal who doesn’t believe in going overboard. Designed out of vegan faux leather and priced at $49.99 on Modcloth, this purchase is kind to the animals and your wallet.

## 3. Rhinestoned Bow Pumps

![Rhinestoned Bow Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/3_rhinestoned-bow-pumps_328x324.jpg)

Girly girls, rejoice: These fabulous pumps feature gorgeous rhinestone bows on the heels . Paired with a cream cupcake dress, you’ll walk down the aisle with flair. They’re available now on Forever 21 for $29.80.

## 4. Touch Ups Cyndi

![Touch Ups Cyndi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/4_touch-ups-cyndi_280x333.jpg)

This pair of winter wedding shoes are fit for brides living in a warm locale. The peep toe will allow you to show off your pretty nail polish and no one will ever guess that you paid $79.95 for these bejeweled stunners! These and similar designs are available now on ShoeBuy.

## 5. Cat-Face Satin Pump

![Cat-Face Satin Pump](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/5_cat-face-satin-pump_270x255.jpg)

Meow! Subtly break tradition with a pair of Charlotte Olympia kitty pumps. They’ll be sure to make any fashionable feline at your wedding party red with envy, making the $910 price tag worth it. So, if you’ve got the cash to spare, head on over to Neiman Marcus for these purr-worthy pumps.

## 6. Dance Instead of Walking Heel

![Dance Instead of Walking Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/6_dance-instead-of-walking-heel_322x252.jpg)

These quirky vegan leather lace-ups will get you ready for a winter wonderland wedding. Priced at $37.99 on ModCloth, this purchase is a no-brainer, leaving you time to choose the perfect dress.

## 7. Truth or Dare by Madonna Sebren Platform T-bar Heeled Shoes

![Truth or Dare by Madonna Sebren Platform T-bar Heeled Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/7_truth-or-dare-by-madonna-sebren-platform-t-bar-heeled-shoes_225x248.jpg)

Leave it to Madonna to create shoes that’ll satisfy your inner glamazon. These “something blue” shoes will take any flirty wedding frock from run-of-the-mill to out-of-this-world haute. Find them on ASOS for $175.90. Just don’t be surprised when you dazzle him into speechlessness.

These are 7 of my favorite stunning winter wedding shoes. Which ones do you like best? Do you have any of your own picks to add to the list?
