# 7 Versatile Knee\-High Boots to Wear in Winter ...

Winter is the ideal time to crack out the knee-high boots. Pull them up over your favourite pair of jeans or wear them with tights and dresses. They’re great for giving you that extra coverage and can complement almost any winter outfit. Boots in simple, fuss-free designs are usually the most versatile so keep this in mind when you’re shoe shopping this season. Take a look at these knee-high boots to stomp around in come winter.

## 1. Cleated High Leg Boots

![Cleated High Leg Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24210613_2570_599x759.jpg)

Biker style knee-high boots seem to stick around season after season. And with good reason – they’re a versatile casual option. They’re great for working a model-off-duty look, worn with skinny jeans and an oversized sweater. Stick to boots with minimal details, like these Topshp boots that have buckle trims. These particular boots are pretty pricey at $196 but you can find similar styles on a budget at stores like ASOS and Forever 21.

## 2. Buckled Riding Boots

![Buckled Riding Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24210611_2683_349x465.jpg)

Tan boots are a great everyday option. The colour is versatile and works with a range of colour palettes. These boots are made from faux leather and have a stacked heel with simple buckle details. They’re pretty easy on the wallet, too, at around $60 from Modcloth. They’re a versatile boot that would look just at home in the big smoke as they would on the ranch.

## 3. High Heeled Knee-High Boots

![High Heeled Knee-High Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24210613_6376_599x564.jpg)

For something a bit dressier, try some heeled knee-high boots on for size. You can’t beat classic black leather, and these ASOS boots feature a chunky stacked heel to give you that extra bit of height. They’re pretty pricey at around $170 but the amount of wear you would get from them would prove cost effective. Wear them with shift dresses and long coats for a polished look or dress them down with faded jeans and chunky knit cardigans.

## 4. Knee-High Wedge Boots

![Knee-High Wedge Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24210612_5261_599x593.jpg)

These black leather-look boots feature a sleek wedge heel. The overall design of the boot is quite classic and streamlined but the wedge heel is just something a bit different. These particular boots are from Dorothy Perkins and would make a smart addition to any winter outfit.

## 5. Faux Suede Tall Boots

![Faux Suede Tall Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24210611_0741_349x459.jpg)

Boots with a slightly ruched design are great for working a slouchy look. Faux suede finishes are also perfect for creating a warm, textured feel. These knee-high boots are from Modcloth and are designed for wider calves. They’ll cost you around $60 and you could team them with short dresses, tights, and winter coats.

## 6. Lace up Boots

![Lace up Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24210611_5023_349x447.jpg)

A pair of lace up boots can give your outfit a bit of a vintage feel. Wear them under maxi dresses and skirts or show them off by tucking your jeans into them. These particular boots are made from faux leather material and have capped toes, faux fur lining, and side zips. They’ll set you back around $60 from Modcloth.

## 7. Black Rain Boots

![Black Rain Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24210612_4133_349x446.jpg)

These stylish boots are the perfect all-weather option. They have a sleek design and feature stylish buckled trims. You’d hardly be able to tell that they’re rain boots at all. Whip them out next time the elements are against you. They’ll set you back around $60 from Modcloth and you can also check out River Island for a cool quilted pair of rain boots.

These are just some knee-high boots to slip on this winter. Black and brown are typically the most versatile colours but you could also try grey as well. Which of these knee-high boots do you like best and how would you style them?
