# 7 Cute Socks to Show off in High Heel Shoes ...

Socks for high heel shoes come in all sorts of designs. While the socks and heels look can be divisive, if you’re a fan of this look you’ll know that half of the success lies in choosing the right combination of socks and high heel shoes. Since the look is fairly girly, opt for cute styles of socks that work well with Mary Jane and court shoes. Take a look at a few cute socks for high heel shoes, below.

## 1. Lilac Lace Trim Socks

**Price:** $6.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/MWxBgS)
When it comes to cute socks for high heel shoes, you can’t go past a pretty frill. It’s perfect for working a look full of girlish charm. These Topshop socks come in a lovely lilac colour and feature a contrasting lace trim. They’d look great worn with neutral pumps.

## 2. Navy Spotty Ankle Socks

![Navy Spotty Ankle Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/2_navy-spotty-ankle-socks_237x600.jpg)

These socks feature a fun spotted design. They come in the classic combination of navy and white and would look great worn with tan Mary Jane heels or even peeking out the top of black heeled ankle boots.

## 3. Nylon Pinspot Ankle Socks

![Nylon Pinspot Ankle Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/3_nylon-pinspot-ankle-socks_368x600.jpg)

**Price:** $6.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/F5eXj8)
Polka dot patterns are popular right now, and these socks feature a small pinspot pattern. They come in a pale pink colour and even feature scallop edging. When styling these socks for high heel shoes, choose a pair in a nude colour with a slim ankle strap detail.

## 4. Cream Lace Trim Ankle Socks

![Cream Lace Trim Ankle Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/4_cream-lace-trim-ankle-socks_415x600.jpg)

**Price:** $6.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/syzfFF)
Much like the lilac lace trim socks previously mentioned on this list, these frilly socks are great for working a schoolgirl inspired look. They come in a classic black and white colourway and would look stylish worn with black patent pumps and cropped tuxedo pants for a more grown-up look.

## 5. Heart Dots Socks

_ **Price:** $2.80 at forever21.com_
These socks feature a super cute heart pattern. They are made from a sheer fabric, so are ideal for wearing in the summer months. Try teaming them with open heeled sandals for a fresh and fun look. These socks for high heel shoes are also available in a range of other colours – perfect for getting the right colour combinations!

## 6. Pointelle Geo Socks

_ **Price:** $2.80 at forever21.com_
These ankle length socks are made from a pointelle knit and feature a geometric pattern. They also have ribbed, elasticised trims. Try teaming these socks with court style shoes or ones with a slim ankle strap.

## 7. Cream Umbrella Print Socks

![Cream Umbrella Print Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/06/7_cream-umbrella-print-socks_247x600.jpg)

**Price:** $4.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you want your socks for high heels with a bit more quirk, then look out for fun patterns like this umbrella one. Let them peek out the top of a pair of heeled ankle boots to brighten up a rainy day.

When it comes to socks for high heel shoes, it’s important to make sure the style of sock suits the shoe. Socks with details such as frills look cute paired with more feminine styles of shoes, and socks in bolder designs can look great with sturdier styles like heeled ankle boots.
