# 7 Funky Neon Flats ...

Whether you favor neutrals or bright hues, neon flats will add a dose of spice to your wardrobe. These picks prove that you don’t need stiletto heels to make an impact! From hot pink to bright blue and a rainbow of colors in between, slip into a pair of neon flats to make the season brighter. I’m planning to wear mine with skinny jeans and a sweater for a casual look or a pencil skirt and blazer for my internship. With one of these 7 pairs of funky neon flats on your feet, you’ll make a statement that can’t be ignored.

![Betsey Johnson Snippii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/1_betsey-johnson-snippii_480x248.jpg)

Let your inner girly girl run wild with Betsey Johnson’s fun and feminine neon flats. Featuring a trendy pink hue and adorable bows, they’ll spruce up last year’s little black dress. I’d wear them through winter with opaque tights and into spring with skirts and frilly socks. Get them now on 6pm for $44.99.

## 2. Steve Madden Strolll

![Steve Madden Strolll](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/2_steve-madden-strolll_480x232.jpg)

If neon’s not usually your thing, try these fierce flats on for size. The pink rim and bow will help you stand out without looking waaay out-there. Pair them with everything from leather skinnies to skirts and sweaters –they’re perfect for a college student who needs shoes that go from class to the club with ease! Buy ‘em now on Zappos for $66.99.

## 3. Neon Sole Oxford Flat

![Neon Sole Oxford Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/3_neon-sole-oxford-flat_404x315.jpg)

These oxfords have got sole (and it’s neon!). Neon soles are a good way to introduce your feet to the neon trend without the commitment. Don’t you wish dating were that easy? Plus, these tan oxfords are super versatile and can be worn through any season. Get them now on Charlotte Russe for $14.49.

## 4. Neon Toe Ballet Flats

![Neon Toe Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/4_neon-toe-ballet-flats_296x88.jpg)

Much like your nude pumps, these flats are work-appropriate and easy to wear. And at $16.80 on Forever 21, the price is right! Choose from neon yellow, pink, or aqua cap toes. I’m planning to wear mine to my new internship with dark wash skinny jeans, a white button down, and a blazer.

## 5. City Classified Sadler Women’s Shoe

![City Classified Sadler Women’s Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/5_city-classified-sadler-womens-shoe_300x160.jpg)

These may be shaped like your standard black flats, but they’re anything but boring! The pointed toe is sophisticated and the bright neon hue is just right. I’d wear them to a club or house party with a mini dress and tights. You can find this pair now on Tilly’s for $6.97.

## 6. Nine West Blustery

![Nine West Blustery](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/6_nine-west-blustery_464x184.jpg)
