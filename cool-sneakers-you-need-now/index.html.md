# 7 Cool Sneakers You Need Now ...

If you’re the kind of gal who craves comfort, these 7 cool sneakers will keep you super stylish. With a selection ranging from flower power prints to notice-me wedges, your toes will soon be begging for some pumped up kicks. No matter what the season, fashionable sneakers are always on trend. They're attractive, they're colorful, and they're comfortable. After all, who said your feet had to suffer to look oh so haute?

## 1. Flower Power Canvas

![Flower Power Canvas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/1_flower-power-canvas_480x216.jpg)

Girly prints go bold with these cool Converse x Marimekko sneakers. I’d pair ‘em with a black mini skirt, a v-neck tee, and purple tights for a whimsical (yet casual) fall look. You can find them in lots of places, from high end shoe stores to local spots both online and off, so feel free to do some searching and some comparison shopping.

## 2. Leopard Print

![Leopard Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/2_leopard-print_480x360.jpg)

Rawr! These Betsey Johnson Nixxxie sneakers are undeniably fierce. They’ll soon become BFFs with your black skinny jeans. If you’re looking for leopard on the cheap, you can also try Forever 21 –they’re usually my go-to place for all things animal print. Remember, cool sneakers come in all shapes, sizes, and prices, so if you find a pair you love and they cost a lot, you'll always be able to find a similar, but more affordable pair.

## 3. Hello Kitty Cute

![Hello Kitty Cute](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/3_hello-kitty-cute_480x360.jpg)

Leave it to Vans to create some seriously adorable Hello Kitty kicks. Your footwear will be the center of attention, courtesy of this fabulous feline. How cute would these look with a hoodie and jeans during a movie night with the girls?

## 4. Studded Hi-Tops

![Studded Hi-Tops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/4_studded-hi-tops_369x336.jpg)

Hey, stud! Charlotte Russe’s studded hi-tops are both fashion forward and easy on the wallet. But don’t limit yourself to just one pair –you can find studded sneakers almost anywhere (Nasty Gal, Urban Outfitters, and Free People are three options). I wear mine with a black lace skirt when I want to add some edge to my look. They're fabulously fashionable sneakers when you're feeling a little punk rock.

## 5. Black Hi-Top Wedge

![Black Hi-Top Wedge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/5_black-hi-top-wedge_342x342.jpg)

Some of the coolest sneakers around today are ones with wedges. And why wouldn’t they be? You’ll score both added height and cushioned comfort by picking up a pair of these Dorothy Perkins wedge sneakers. I’d pair them with opaque tights and a long tunic for a look that’s sporty yet party-ready.

## 6. Converse All Star Lo Batgirl Athletic Shoe

![Converse All Star Lo Batgirl Athletic Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/6_converse-all-star-lo-batgirl-athletic-shoe_384x178.jpg)

You don’t need superpowers to have an unbeatable sense of sneaker style. With these Converse Batgirl sneakers, no villain would dare mess with you! Dress them up with a black blazer for an ultra-sleek ensemble. The pop of vibrant color is perfect for fall.

## 7. Brown Wedge Sneakers

![Brown Wedge Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/7_brown-wedge-sneakers_480x360.jpg)

In search of cool sneakers for the new season? These Steve Madden Olympiaa shoes will deliver a dose of fall flair…and then some! The brown hue is reminiscent of crisp leaves –and it’ll go with almost anything. So get ready to step out in style.

With these seven cool sneakers, you’ll be the star of the season. Make sure you pamper your feet while still staying fashionable. Which ones are your favorites? Do you have any of your own suggestions to add?
