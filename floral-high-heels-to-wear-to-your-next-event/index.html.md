# Step out in Fashionable Floral Heels for Your Next Event ...

I adore unique shoes and there’s something about floral high heels that give an outfit that ‘statement’ look! Although floral prints have been around for some time (a lot of high street designers have been producing them), I still feel they look great with the perfect outfit. If you find the right pair of floral high heels to suit your feet, body shape and outfit, then you’re in for a treat, as I think they really elevate your look in my opinion. The majority of these floral high heels are made of a printed fabric, so do require a little extra care than regular leather shoes. Here are some floral high heels that made my top seven!

## 1. Steve Madden ‘Groovi’ Floral Print Half D’Orsay Pumps

![Steve Madden ‘Groovi’ Floral Print Half D’Orsay Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/k6/kly8zne4_245x208.jpg)

If you’re searching for something fun and flirty like floral high heels, Steve Madden is the designer to consult. These 4-inch heels will give you that added height and will look great with either a little black dress or your favorite pair of skinny jeans and a colorful top. Priced at $99.95, these are available from Steve Madden stores and [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 2. Kate Spade New York Licorice Pumps

![Kate Spade New York Licorice Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/q5/d4vw4gi4_480x312.jpg)

These are some really bright and colorful pink roses. In true Kate Spade fashion, these shoes are well constructed and will go well with one of her shift dresses and a clutch, or denim jeans and a t-shirt, if you want more of a casual look. Either way, I’m convinced these stylish heels will pair well with anything you wear. Priced at $328 they are available from Kate Spade boutiques and [couture.zappos.com](https://www.zappos.com/a/the-style-room/women/trends?rl=true).

## 3. Ted Baker ‘Saeber’ Pointy Toe Satin Heels

![Ted Baker ‘Saeber’ Pointy Toe Satin Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f3/ae/nq5yz2ck_290x151.jpg)

If you want a floral shoe that doesn’t focus too much on the flowers, then the soft multi-color ethereal print on these otherwise striking black heels would be great. They do have a little gold piece at the toe and on the 4-inch heel, but the overall whimsical effect on these is what makes them work well in my opinion. Priced at $200, these are available in a white version as well. Buy them from Ted Baker boutiques and department stores like [bloomingdales.com](http://www1.bloomingdales.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 4. ASOS Predict Pointed High Heels

![ASOS Predict Pointed High Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cj/w2/fcgz4as4_290x174.jpg)

If you are looking for a pretty coral-red rose printed heel, these are for you! They have a chunkier 4-inch heel if you don’t enjoy wearing super high heels. They at least look like they’d be durable and last a couple of occasions, particularly if you need to wear these outdoors. Priced reasonably at $34.82 you can find them online from [us.ASOS.com](https://goo.gl/zwmAAa).

## 5. Oscar De La Renta Grace Printed Woven Silk Pumps

![Oscar De La Renta Grace Printed Woven Silk Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qz/oa/b13fs6fl_600x470.jpg)

These Italian shoes will go perfectly with something red or black. The floral elements on these shoes are so subtle, that you don’t feel like you’re wearing flowers if that makes sense (in case floral high heels aren’t for you). The woven fabric is silk giving them that extra touch of elegance. Priced at $313 from [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and other boutiques that sell Oscar’s shoes.

## 6. Steve Madden Vincible Heels

![Steve Madden Vincible Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bg/kc/n4kbmnmd_378x270.jpg)

The flowers on these shoes come in a variety of colors on a black leather background, so would match any type of outfit. It is quite a heel at 4.5-inches so they might be a little high for some. Priced at $99.95, these are available from Steve Madden boutiques as well as department stores and [shopstyle.com](https://www.shopstyle.com/browse?fts=steve+madden+floral).

## 7. Dolce & Gabbana Jeweled Brocade Slingback Pumps

![Dolce & Gabbana Jeweled Brocade Slingback Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/lt/iq8f6ki5_300x292.jpg)

These are a little flashy with the jeweled brooch on the front, not to mention pricey too, but you generally get what you pay for in life! These Italian slingbacks fit great and there’s a lot of attention to detail and craftsmanship; they are sure to add that finishing touch to your outfit. While I’ve never spent $800 on a pair of shoes, if you want to splurge and wear something that no one else owns, opt for a pair of these! Priced at $845 you can find these at department stores like [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).
