# The 11 Best Oxford Shoes ...

Some of the best oxford shoes range in price from very cheap to very expensive. However depending on what you are looking for, you are bound to find something that fits your taste in a reasonable price range. Personally I think that oxford shoes are one of the staple items every man or woman should have. They are fun, unique and just something that is thought to be a classic! So if you want to join this long lasting trend, here are some of the best oxford shoes as of now.

## 1. Loeffler Randall Leopard Haircalf Oxfords

![Loeffler Randall Leopard Haircalf Oxfords](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/12025914_8101_299x181.jpg)

It is always so liberating to see an individual sporting a daring and risky pair of shoes. It just shows how much of self confidence that person entails to the point that outside opinion doesnt matter. So if you are one of those fearless people, this pair of oxfords should definitely fit the bill. Loeffler Randall Leopard Haircalf Oxfords have a leopard print made from dyed hair calf, pointed toe and lace up detailing. They are fierce, bold and definitely not subtle.

**Price:** $295 at [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. J.Crew Metallic Mirror

Don’t have a metallic pair of shoes in your closet? I think it is time we change that! Just take a look at these bold metallic pair of oxfords. These shoes come with a metallic shiny exterior, ⅞” heel, upper, leather lining and sole. They will definitely bring a bit of glamour and style into your life.

**Price:** $258 at [jcrew.com](https://goo.gl/2eyow9)

## 3. Sam Edelman Jayden Oxford

![Sam Edelman Jayden Oxford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/12025915_7712_465x231.jpg)

Sam Edelman Jayden Oxfords are the epitome of the perfect fun and edgy statement shoes. Not only do they have patterned slits all over but they also include a shoe-lace detailing and a studded out toe. Moreover they come in variety of colors such as nude, metal and black. Personally, this one is one of my favorites!

**Price:** $150 at [hsn.com](http://www.hsn.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Frye Jillian Flat

If you are looking for a shoe to fit your casual style, Frye Jillian Flat is the perfect choice. It has the perfect slim and casually fashionable style without the laces. In addition it has leather lining and cushioned footbed, so you are sure to stay comfy and cozy wherever you are!

**Price:** $168 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Steve Madden Tudor Flat

_ Steve Madden Tudor Flat is one of the most feminine oxfords on this list. Not only is it available in a beautiful silver color but it also has shiny detailing all over the shoe. It’s girly, bold and fun! You will definitely feel younger while strolling in this pair of oxfords!

**Price:** $49.99 at newegg.com_

## 6. BP Sidney Oxford

![BP Sidney Oxford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/12030204_1558_600x355.jpg)

You are bound to drown in compliments when you have something like BP Sidney Oxford on. It has a rich leather exterior, checkered inside lining and a flirty ribbon to top the whole thing off. While you will be paying for the price of one shoe, in a way you actually get 3 pairs in return. While the shoes themselves come with a ribbon, you can always take it out and go laceless or just add plain brown laces and go for more of a casual look!

**Price:** $70 at [svpply.com](https://goo.gl/hACikK)

## 7. Free People Capone Oxford

Capone Oxford shoes are definitely a luxury to own. They were actually designed by Jeffrey Campbell exclusively for Free People.The overall product consists of studded detailing over the front and back of the shoe in addition to distressed leather and the lace-up front. Although there is a little bit of a heel, the comfort level in these shoes is still at its highest!

**Price:** $128 at [freepeople.com](http://www.freepeople.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Kate Spade Poppin Flat

Available in black and nude, Kate Spade Poppin Flat is definitely one of a kind shoe. It has beautiful gold detailing on the cap toe in the nude version that is absolutely breath-taking. The material is mainly velvet and leather and the closure is completed by a grosgrain ribbon.

**Price:** $278 at [couture.zappos.com](https://www.zappos.com/a/the-style-room/women/trends?rl=true)

## 9. Zara Patent Blucher

If you are one of the regular customers at Zara, you probably know how tempting their shoe collections can often be. Well this pair of shoes is no different! While the design is overly simple, the shoe still manages to stand out with its beautiful silver coating and beige detailing. So try to grab this praise-worthy pair of shoes before it’s too late.

**Price:** $59.99 at [zara.com](https://www.zara.com/us/en/woman-shoes-l1251.html)

## 10. Pour La Victoire Zandra Monk Strap Flats

![Pour La Victoire Zandra Monk Strap Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/12025921_0533_299x225.jpg)

Pour La Victoire Zandra Monk Strap Flats are somewhat of a hybrid between ankle boots and oxfords. They have the overall shape of oxfords with a low heel and pointed toe but they also come with a stylish belt over the front and polished hardware. It’s perfect for those slightly chilly days during spring or fall season.

**Price:** $123.20 at [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Dolce Vita Ollie Flat

![Dolce Vita Ollie Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/12025921_6813_599x292.jpg)

If you are looking for an oxford inspired shoe with a little more of a feminine touch, you must take a look at Dolce Vita Ollie Flat. It has a sleek black and white design in addition to a beautiful shimmery toe. A men-inspired shoe has never looked this girly!

**Price:** $113.20 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

With so many choices laid out in front of you, it can get a little difficult to chose a pair to invest in, but hopefully this post has given you some insight into some of the best oxford shoes on the market. So which pair of shoes will you be purchasing next?
