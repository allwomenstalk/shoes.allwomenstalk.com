# 9 Trendy and Colorful Summer Sandals ...

**Colorful summer sandals** are your go-to footwear for the season. A colorful summer sandal is a great way to make a statement and to also add colour to an outfit. There’s a rainbow of colorful summer sandals currently available – you’ll be hard pressed to pick just one color! Take a look at a few colorful sandals for summer below.

## 1. Topshop Florida Simple Buckle Sandals

![Topshop Florida Simple Buckle Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/1_topshop-florida-simple-buckle-sandals_400x199.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/w7YxZO)
These **colorful summer sandals feature a** stylish crossover design. They come in a cool coral colour and feature neon accents at the edges. These would look great worn with a short denim dress and canvas tote bag.

## 2. Topshop Hatty2 Bow Sandals

![Topshop Hatty2 Bow Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/2_topshop-hatty2-bow-sandals_400x213.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/NYS6LN)
You’ve got to love a cute bow detail. These leather sandals feature wides straps at the toes and around the ankles They also feature a bow detail at the front. Wear these with a full-skirted dress to play up the feminine factor.

## 3. Modcloth Rays Eyebrows Sandal

![Modcloth Rays Eyebrows Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/3_modcloth-rays-eyebrows-sandal_400x250.jpg)

**Price:** $58.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Can’t decide on just one color? Then go for multiple colors! This **colorful summer sandal features orange, blue** and purple straps set against a tan leather main. These would look great worn with dark denim shorts and a plain white t-shirt.

## 4. ASOS Fizz Leather Multicolored Sandals

![ASOS Fizz Leather Multicolored Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/4_asos-fizz-leather-multicolored-sandals_400x244.jpg)

**Price:** $57.30 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/ftwE6p)
These mint colored sandals also feature multicoloured elastic ankle straps and silver trims. They have an open design with straps at the toes and a zip closure at the back of the ankle. Wear these sandals with a pastel shift dress and compact side bag.

## 5. Topshop Hunt Crossover Fluro Sandals

![Topshop Hunt Crossover Fluro Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/5_topshop-hunt-crossover-fluro-sandals_400x200.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/a7VmYF)
All out fluro may be a bit harsh on the eye, so a simple splash of colour will do when it **comes to colorful summer sandals**. These crossover sandals come in a light cream colour and feature a fluro pink strap at the toe. Work a cool summer look by teaming them with a colorful printed dress.

## 6. ASOS Fantasy Neoprene Twist Sandals

![ASOS Fantasy Neoprene Twist Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/6_asos-fantasy-neoprene-twist-sandals_400x154.jpg)

**Price:** $26.86 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/eWgkCN).
It’s not often you come across shoes made from neoprene. These shoes have a twisted design and feature bright green neoprene straps. These sandals would look great worn with a crisp white dress or trousers.

## 7. Marais USA Summer Sandal

![Marais USA Summer Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/7_marais-usa-summer-sandal_400x259.jpg)

**Price:** $87.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A closed toe sandal is a great alternative to those open stole styles. This colorful summer sandal has a patent finish and features strap details at the front as well as a buckle-fastening ankle. Wear these with a neutral shirt dress and slouchy shoulder bag for a fun, casual look.

## 8. K Jacques St Tropez Leather Sandals

![K Jacques St Tropez Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/8_k-jacques-st-tropez-leather-sandals_400x224.jpg)

Shades of orange are set to be big this summer. Work this colour trend with these beautiful strappy sandals. These sandals would look great worn with a floaty white dress.

## 9. Emilio Pucci Two-Tone Gladiator Sandals

![Emilio Pucci Two-Tone Gladiator Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/04/9_emilio-pucci-two-tone-gladiator-sandals_400x320.jpg)

**Price:** $980.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ovgEhP)
These colorful summer sandals make quite the statement. They feature azure and emerald suede straps and have a lace up design around the ankle. They also have a gold zip detail at the back. Since these shoes are quite busy, sticking to a fairly minimal outfit would be your best bet.

No matter what your favourite color is, you’re bound to find a summer sandal to suit. **A colorful summer sandal** will be a breath of fresh air for your wardrobe. So tell me, which of these colorful summer sandals do you like the most?
