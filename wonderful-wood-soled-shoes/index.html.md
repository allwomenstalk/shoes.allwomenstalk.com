# 7 Wonderful Wood\-Soled Shoes ...

Wedges and platforms, heels and clogs, all of these hot summer shoes have one thing in common: a wood sole! Wood-soled shoes are incredibly hot this season, and will be for a few more, so if you’re looking to add something new to your wardrobe, keep reading! Here are 7 wonderful wood-soled shoes.

## 1. Look-through Wedges

![Look-through Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nd/p1/m47wl4lk54c92b2ca8803908890829_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $178.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Pair these funky, chunky wood-soled shoes by Schuler & Sons with a pair of wide-leg high-waisted trousers and a sheet peasant blouse for a vintage-inspired look that’s sure to get you noticed! They’re available in whole and half sizes from 5 to 11, and feature a four-inch heel.

## 2. Crossover Clogs

![Crossover Clogs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/cy/n6les72254c92b2d88378134500791_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $168.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Here’s another pair of wood-soled shoes by Schuler & Sons, this one with a lush lemon-yellow leather upper, criss-crossed at the ankle for a dainty, feminine look. The heel is a walk-able 3¼ inch, with a 1 inch platform. Wouldn’t these look pretty with a floral-print tunic and leggings?

## 3. Tiny Spaces Clogs

![Tiny Spaces Clogs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/d5/xjb29ebw54c92b2ea2e0c460644671_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $148.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These deep red (raspberry) clogs by Maguba feature clever cut-outs on the upper, and prove that sometimes style can come in the smallest of spaces. My point: these run a bit small, so order a full size up to ensure the best fit. Then pair them with almost anything in your summer wardrobe for a pop of color!

## 4. To the Sky Platforms

![To the Sky Platforms](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/ss/xgb226jc54c92b2f93751466057510_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $218.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Carefully embroidered in the most brilliant blue palette, these wood-soled shoes would look divine with anything denim, perhaps a romper or a pair of cuffed paper-bag shorts? All you need is a pair of lace-trimmed anklets, and you’re set!

## 5. Fyn Heels

![Fyn Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/oz/w5pio51b54c92b30955aa171918509_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $168.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Lucky Penny’s done it again, with these oxford-clog hybrids. They feature the darling lace-up of an oxford with the ease, style, and comfort of a wood-soled shoe. They come in sweet lemon-drop yellow or tawny moss. Both are so pretty, how will you choose?

## 6. Open Agenda Wedges

![Open Agenda Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u4/h3/zdd2ipcc54c92b3185668696247480_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $88.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s like Shelly’s London read my mind - coral is my favorite color this season, and look at these shoes! Their gorgeous coral uppers are perforated prettily and there are two tin buckles at the toe and heel. They’re also available in black, but why? Who wouldn’t love to add these coral cuties to their collection?

## 7. Sveaborg Picnic Clogs

![Sveaborg Picnic Clogs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x5/ip/qycgcj1r54c92b32846ad813059048_453x340.jpg)

**Price:** $168.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These classic clogs by Lucky Penny seem like they were made for dresses with wide belts, don’t they? Or perhaps capris or long shorts? I love the lush red uppers and the duo of straps at the ankles. The blue is pretty, too! It reminds me of the summer sky.

With so many wonderful wood-soled shoes to choose from, your summer wardrobe will be anything but dull… and leather-only! I especially love the yellow heels… but which of these wood-soled shoes do you like best, and what would you wear them with? Do tell!
