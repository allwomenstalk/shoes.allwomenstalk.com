# 8 New Season Studded Shoes ...

Studded shoes have been doing the fashion rounds for a while now. This season, there’s a studded shoe to suit almost all tastes. Whether you’re after a sky high pair of heels or a casual sneaker, there seems to be studded versions everywhere you go! Take a look at just some stylish new season studded shoes, below.

## 1. Stud Pointed Shoes

![Stud Pointed Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/1_stud-pointed-shoes_600x336.jpg)

Pointed shoes are oh-so-stylish right now and made even better with some studded embellishments. Everyone from high end designers like Valentino to high street stores like Topshop are jumping on board the studded shoes bandwagon. For a little over $50, you can get your hands on these studded shoes from Topshop.

## 2. Studded Loafers

![Studded Loafers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/2_studded-loafers_600x250.jpg)

The love affair with loafers continues, so this season why don’t you go for an all-over studded finish? Studded embellishments help give the humble loafer a more daring edge. While you’ll have to fork out some serious dough for these Valentino shoes, or even a stylish Jimmy Choo pair, you can find much more affordable versions at Forever 21, Modcloth, and Tophsop.

## 3. Studded Ankle Boots

![Studded Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/3_studded-ankle-boots_600x378.jpg)

Studded ankle boots come and go each season. Right now, opt for a Western-style boot with some subtle stud detailing. Don’t worry if you don’t have the cash to splurge on a designer pair by Chloé either, with plenty of chic and cheap options available from stores like Topshop (pictured here) and ASOS.

## 4. Studded Sneakers

Stay comfy this season in a pair of studded sneakers. They’re casual and cool, but the stud detailing says you mean business. Team your studded sneakers with skinny jeans and a simple sweater for a fuss-free weekend look. There’s a studded sneaker to suit any budget, ranging from expensive Miu Miu versions to more affordable versions by ASOS and Forever 21. For example, these stylish sneakers will set you back around $30 at Forever 21.

## 5. Studded Oxfords

![Studded Oxfords](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/5_studded-oxfords_320x208.jpg)

Make your preppy oxfords a little bit more badass with the inclusion of some studded dealing. Check out classic brands like Bass and Church’s for a smart pair of these studded lace-up shoes. They’re perfect for wearing around campus or on a stroll downtown.

## 6. Studded Heels

![Studded Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/6_studded-heels_600x535.jpg)

If you’re after a statement shoe, check out shoes with ornately embellished heels. From the front they may look like a simple sandal or pump but from the back they’re a right riot! Check out Sam Edelman, Steve Madden and Aldo for different takes on this kind of studded shoe. Teamed with a simple dress and matching accessories, they’d be perfect for a fresh party look.

## 7. Gem Studded Shoes

![Gem Studded Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/12/7_gem-studded-shoes_600x632.jpg)

If metal spikes and studs are too heavy for your liking, try some gem studs instead. Thanks to designers like Dolce & Gabbana, gem embellishments will be everywhere this season. Topshop and ASOS have a few different styles of gem studded shoes in the $50-$100 price range. For something more affordable you could always go DIY and try gluing some gem stones onto a pair of plain heels.

## 8. Studded Ballet Pumps

When it comes to everyday footwear, ballet pumps are timeless. However, if you want something a bit more modern and on-trend, then a pair of studded ballet pumps may just be for you. Simply switch your old plain pair for stylish studded embellishments. They’re easy enough to add to your wardrobe too, with plenty of budget buys available at Forever21, ASOS, and Topshop.

As you can see, there are all sorts of studded shoes available this season. From everyday flats to party-ready heels, there’s a type of studded shoe for almost every occasion. Which of these studded shoes will you want to be wearing this season?
