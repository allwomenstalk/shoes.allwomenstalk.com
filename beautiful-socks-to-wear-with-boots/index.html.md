# 7 Beautiful Socks to Wear with Boots ...

As it turns out, you can make a style statement with socks to wear with boots. You’ve already treated your toes to the latest in cold weather footwear, so why not show your legs some love? Let the tops of these knee-high and over-the-knee socks peek out from under your brand new riding boots or booties. Expect your sweet socks to be showered with attention!

## 1. Sweet Stripes

![Sweet Stripes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/1_sweet-stripes_480x560.jpg)

With these Free People Merino Stripe over-the-knee socks, you’ll unleash your whimsical side. The good news: Whether you have an endless budget (you lucky lady) or live on ramen noodles, striped socks are within reach. You can pick up a pair almost anywhere! Go with monochromatic stripes like these or pick out a whimsical palette. Bright beautiful socks make gloomy days sparkle!

## 2. Let the Sunshine in

![Let the Sunshine in](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/2_let-the-sunshine-in_410x410.jpg)

Target’s Xhilaration Juniors over-the-knee socks are perfect to wear with brown riding boots. The sunny yellow tops will add a touch of summer to any outfit –even when the weather is frigid. Whatever you're favorite retailer, you'll find sunny sweet socks that snag your fancy. Go for all-over yellow or bright spots of color like you see here!

## 3. Spot the Dots

![Spot the Dots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/3_spot-the-dots_328x400.jpg)

Forever 21’s polka dot socks are stylishly spotted. Available in an array of dotted hues, you’ll find a pair for any ensemble. Go for colorful contrasts in unexpected shades or keep it simple, depending on your mood and your outfit. Thigh-high socks are so popular this season that you'll find them elsewhere as well, just keep an eye out!

## 4. Garden Variety

![Garden Variety](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/4_garden-variety_480x560.jpg)

These socks to wear with boots are fit for any Friday night. Though it might be too chilly for an outdoors party, Betsey Johnson Garden Lace Cuff over-the-knee socks are reminiscent of flower beds in spring. A pair of these will make you forget all about any winter doldrums in no time, plus they're kind of Lolita-sexy, don't you think?

## 5. Beautiful Bows

Modcloth’s Frilled to Be Here socks will make you feel like a ballerina princess. Pair them with a shocking pink mini, black booties, and a lacy blazer for a fairytale-with-edge look. Variations on this style are popping up everywhere; you can even find socks decorated will real ribbons for a saucy, [feminine look](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/super-simple-tips-to-look-more-feminine/).

## 6. Floral Frenzy

For the girlie girls, this pair of socks to wear with boots will be a mainstay in your drawer. Modcloth’s Petal-Powered socks are sweet enough for everyday wear, yet bold enough to pack on the flair! You'll definitely want to wear your favorite pair of booties with these beauties!

## 7. Cool in Color Blocked

![Cool in Color Blocked](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/10/7_cool-in-color-blocked_328x400.jpg)

Color blocking is still all the rage, especially if you love a lot of color. Forever 21’s color blocked over-the-knee socks are fun, fashionable, and deliciously affordable. They’ll make any outfit pop, so feel free to rock ‘em with your neutrals.

These are 7 of the cutest socks to wear with boots. It's easy to make a statement with your shoes, but think about highlighting your personal style with sweet socks too. They're affordable, they'll keep you warm, and they're so cute! Which ones do you love? Do you have any of your own personal favorites to add?
