# 8 Ankle Strap Shoes ...

Ankle straps are quite practical in that they anchor your feet into a shoe. You don’t have to worry about feet slipping in and out. Ankle straps can be present in a variety of shoe designs, from sandals to stiletto heels. Take a look at some stylish ankle strap shoes below.
