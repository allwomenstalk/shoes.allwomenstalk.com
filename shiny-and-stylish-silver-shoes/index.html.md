# 8 Shiny and Stylish Silver Shoes ...

Stylish silver shoes can be a great way to glam up your outfit. Metallic clothing, shoes and accessories are really popular right now and silver is the colour of the moment. This season, there’s a silver shoe to suit almost all occasions. Silver sandals and sneakers are cool daytime options while heels and wedges are perfect for glamming up a night time look. Get all futuristic in these stylish silver shoes.

## 1. Silver Mary Jane Shoes

![Silver Mary Jane Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/1_silver-mary-jane-shoes_600x252.jpg)

Stylish silver shoes can be great for working a polished look. Classic shoes like these Mary Janes are perfect for working a ladylike look. They can also be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. Wear them with a pretty dress during the day or with cropped trousers and a blazer for something more sophisticated. These Miu Miu shoes are a splurge but you could try ASOS and Zara for similar, more affordable styles of shoes.

## 2. Silver Heeled Shoes

![Silver Heeled Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/2_silver-heeled-shoes_600x484.jpg)

Silver heels will instantly glam up a night time look. Instead of strappy silver stilettos, try a statement making pump to finish off your party-ready look. Designers like Miu Miu and Stella McCartney have jumped on board the bandwagon but retailers like ASOS, Nasty Gal, and Topshop all offer up affordable takes on this trend.

## 3. Silver Flat Sandals

![Silver Flat Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/3_silver-flat-sandals_600x248.jpg)

Incorporate silver shoes into your everyday look with a pair of cool looking sandals. Choose a sandal with a sleek, fuss-free design. Team them with anything from denim shorts to sheer pleated skirts. These silver strappy sandals are a bargain from Forever 21 and you can also find similar styles at ASOS and Modcloth.

## 4. Silver Sneakers

![Silver Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/4_silver-sneakers_600x244.jpg)

The sport luxe look is all the rage and sneakers are an on-trend footwear choice right now. Choose one in a silver colour to really up the ante. These Topshop sneakers are still pretty pricey at $110 but you can find similar, more affordable styles by Superga, H&M, and Forever 21.

## 5. Silver Ankle Boots

![Silver Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/5_silver-ankle-boots_240x259.jpg)

Really make a statement in a pair of silver ankle boots. Wear them with wax-coated jeans for a fashion-forward look or with floaty maxi skirts for a futuristic take on boho dressing. Brands like Acne, Shakuhachi, and Jeffrey Campbell all offer pricey versions but if you’re after a more affordable boot then check out stores like Urban Outfitters.

## 6. Silver Ballet Flats

![Silver Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/6_silver-ballet-flats_256x128.jpg)

If you want to wear silver shoes on an everyday basis, try a pair of silver ballet flats. They’re a versatile style of shoe and you can wear your silver ballet flats with anything from ladylike dresses to simple jeans and a t-shirt. These J.Crew sandals are pretty expensive at over $100 but you can find more affordable styles by Forever 21 and Steve Madden.

## 7. Silver Glitter Shoes

![Silver Glitter Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/7_silver-glitter-shoes_464x288.jpg)

Get all girly and glamorous in a pair of glittery silver shoes. Glitter heels are a popular option, but you can also find glitter shoes in the form of brogues, sneakers, and boots. These Kate Spade heels cost a whopping $300 but you could try Target and Delia’s for a range of more affordable glitter shoes.

## 8. Silver Lace-up Shoes

![Silver Lace-up Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/04/8_silver-lace-up-shoes_600x349.jpg)

Work a menswear inspired look in a pair of silver lace-up shoes. Wear them to add an androgynous touch to your favourite dress or with a shorts suit for a fun summertime look. These lace-up shoes are from ASOS but you can find others at Nordstrom and Free People.

Silver shoes come in all sorts of shapes and styles this season. They can also be worn to suit a range of occasions. What do you think of the silver shoe trend? How would you style these silver shoes?
