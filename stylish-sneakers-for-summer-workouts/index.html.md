# 7 Super Stylish Sneakers for Your Summer Workouts ...

Give your workout wardrobe a facelift with a new pair of stylish sneakers. Right now, more than ever, sneakers are as much of a fashion statement as they are a functional piece of gym gear. You’re basically spoilt for choice when it comes to colours and designs. And buying new gym gear is a great motivator to get moving this summer. Get excited about your summer workouts with these stylish sneakers.

## 1. Nike Free Sneakers

![Nike Free Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/06/1_nike-free-sneakers_600x313.jpg)

Nike has really stepped up its game when it comes to stylish sneakers. They seem to have every colour combination sorted. These Nike Free 5.0 V4 sneakers come in a hot pink hue and feature a cool textured finish. As much as they’re suited to working out, you could just as easily wear them with jeans and a tee for a cool urban look.

## 2. New Balance Sneakers

![New Balance Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/06/2_new-balance-sneakers_400x199.jpg)

If your workouts consist more of a leisurely walk around the block, then check out these suede sneakers by New Balance. They have a retro-inspired design and come in a pretty light blue and pink colourway. When you’re not wearing them while working out, team them with a cute shift dress and bomber jacket for a casual streetwear-inspired look.

## 3. Reebok High Top Sneakers

![Reebok High Top Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/06/3_reebok-high-top-sneakers_600x381.jpg)

These Reebok sneakers are great if you’re going to be spending a lot of time walking or on your feet. The high top design also means they provide plenty of ankle support should you need it. Not only are these shoes functional, but they also look pretty cool and can be worn with jeans and shorts for more of a streetwear-inspired look.

## 4. Nike Flex Sneakers

![Nike Flex Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/06/4_nike-flex-sneakers_600x504.jpg)

You can’t go wrong with basic black and these Nike sneakers are proof of that. If you prefer your wardrobe to be timeless and classic, gym gear included, then these sneakers might be more your style. They have a mainly black design with a white sole and signature Nike tick at the sides. These sneakers are a great choice for neutral running and light workouts.

## 5. Adidas Mesh Sneakers

![Adidas Mesh Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/06/5_adidas-mesh-sneakers_480x314.jpg)

Liven up your workout outfit with a bright splash of colour. These Adidas mesh sneakers come in a cool coral colour and feature matte silver strips and white soles. They’re a sleek and stylish sneaker and will definitely add some spark to your workout wardrobe.

## 6. ASOS Polka Dot Sneakers

![ASOS Polka Dot Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/06/6_asos-polka-dot-sneakers_600x348.jpg)

If you love polka dots then you can’t pass up these cute ASOS sneakers. They have a quite sporty design and come in a light blue colour with white polka dots. I’m not sure how they’d go with high intensity workouts but they sure would look cute worn with shorts and a t-shirt for a sporty summertime outfit.

## 7. Michael Michael Kors Sneakers

![Michael Michael Kors Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/06/7_michael-michael-kors-sneakers_480x232.jpg)

It’s not just the sportswear brands that are designing stylish sneakers. Plenty of high end brands are dipping their toes into sneaker design. These MICHAEL Michael Kors sneakers have a retro-inspired design. They feature neutral panels and sleek navy trims. However, I’m going to guess that you’re probably better off wearing these sneakers out shopping than sprinting.

Are you a bold and bright gal or do you prefer basic black? No matter what you prefer, there are plenty of stylish sneakers on the market for you to choose from. What are your favourite sneaker brands?
