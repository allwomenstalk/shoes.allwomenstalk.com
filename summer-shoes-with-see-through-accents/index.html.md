# 8 Summer Shoes with See\-through Accents ...

From the runways of Michael Kors and Marni to the city streets, see-through shoes are everywhere this season. And no, they don’t look anything like those cheap plastic shoes you insisted on hobbling around in as a child! Here are 8 pairs of see-through shoes that’ll keep you stepping out in style all summer long.

## 1. Deena & Ozzy Clear-Heeled Sandal

![Deena & Ozzy Clear-Heeled Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/1_deena-ozzy-clear-heeled-sandal_137x131.jpg)

In terms of see-through shoes, you really can’t go wrong with Deena & Ozzy’s sleek strappy sandals, which feature of-the-moment clear acrylic heels. The ankle straps are classy and will keep your feet firmly in place. I’d wear them with a pair of leather shorts, a vintage tee, and chunky jewelry to really make a statement. Buy this pair now on UrbanOutfitters.com for $49.99.

## 2. Clear Wingtip Lace-up Oxford

![Clear Wingtip Lace-up Oxford](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/2_clear-wingtip-lace-up-oxford_164x118.jpg)

We all have those pairs of quirky printed socks that never see the light of day under our Converse and booties. But with these clear-paneled oxfords on your feet, you’ll be able to reveal those darling dots or feminine florals! Best of all, this pair is totally vegan-friendly, so it’d be a guilt-free addition to your closet. Pick up this pair now on CharlotteRusse.com for $35.50.

## 3. Izzy by Kate Spade New York

Not only are these platforms totally on-trend, but they’ll go with almost any sundress in your wardrobe, too! I’d wear them with a flouncy floral sundress, oversized shades, and a touch of pink lip gloss for a look that’s brunch date perfect. Buy this pair now on Piperlime.com for $249.99.

## 4. Truth or Dare by Madonna Quilliams

Hello sunshine! Add a pop of color to your wardrobe with these electric yellow flats. I’d wear them with everything from a flirty black cocktail dress to cutoff denim shorts and a button front top. Pick ‘em up now on Zappos.com for $107.99.

## 5. Bass Maebird

![Bass Maebird](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/5_bass-maebird_440x256.jpg)

These lovely oxford loafers will put pep in your step. I’d wear these summery blue and yellow hues again and again, and the clear plastic upper adds an extra dose of quirky-cool charm. I’d wear this pair with a swingy white frock and a brown satchel for a morning spent writing at a local coffee shop. Scoop these up now on 6pm.com for $76.99.

## 6. Kelsi Dagger Mercy Flats

Put-together loafers are absolute must-haves for your warm weather wardrobe. Whether you’re planning to hit the town in a little black dress or meet your pals at the mall in cutoff denim shorts and a vintage tee, they’ll add a classy finishing touch to your ensemble. Buy these red patent/clear vinyl loafers on Dillards.com now for $59.99.

## 7. Storybook of My Life Flat

Girly girls, rejoice! These adorable bow flats will complete almost any ultra-feminine summer look. I’d wear them with a flared black miniskirt, yellow silky top, and black envelope clutch on a dinner date. Pick these up now on ModCloth.com for $29.99.

## 8. Band of Bold Sandal

![Band of Bold Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2013/05/8_band-of-bold-sandal_164x196.jpg)

Who could resist a playful pair of yellow and clear sandals? Not this girl! They’ll brighten up any simple shorts-and-tee combo and can be worn everywhere from the boardwalk to the mall. Find them now on ModCloth.com for $27.99.
