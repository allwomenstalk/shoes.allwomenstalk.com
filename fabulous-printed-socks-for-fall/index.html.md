# 7 Fabulous Printed Socks for Fall ...

Printed socks are a cozy fall treat. Not only will they keep your toes toasty, but they’re quirky and cute to boot! Here are 7 pairs to wear with everything from sneakers to boots. Are you ready to sock it this season?

## 1. Let’s Hear It for the Girls

![Let’s Hear It for the Girls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/1_lets-hear-it-for-the-girls_600x857.jpg)

Unleash your inner feminist with these fearless Rosie the Riveter “Simply Riveting” socks from ModCloth. With this heroine on your heels, you’ll stay toasty (and empowered!).

## 2. Seeing Stars

These Starry Night knee-highs from Fred Flare are the Van Gogh of printed socks. Wear your masterpiece pair with heeled booties and a flirty mini for a quirky outfit any artsy guy would love.

## 3. Can You Feel the Love Tonight?

![Can You Feel the Love Tonight?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/3_can-you-feel-the-love-tonight_575x900.jpg)

These printed socks feature the loveliest (and luckiest!) couples in the animal kingdom. How cute would these “I Link I Love You” socks from ModCloth look peeking out of your fall booties?

## 4. Feline Frenzy

Meow! A pair of leopard print socks can make your feet feel like the cat’s meow. Pick up a pair at your local wallet-friendly Forever 21 store.

## 5. Love is in the Air

![Love is in the Air](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/5_love-is-in-the-air_304x176.jpg)

I can’t help but heart these printed ankle socks from Forever 21. They’d be a match with anything from girly flats to sneakers.

## 6. A Pair of Page-Turners

Calling all bookworms! Guys and gals will be lining up to read these “I’ve Tread Them All” printed socks from ModCloth. They’d look especially smart with oxford loafers and a Peter Pan collared blouse.

## 7. Strawberry Fields Forever

![Strawberry Fields Forever](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/shoes/2012/11/7_strawberry-fields-forever_250x357.jpg)

ModCloth’s “Berry Tasty” printed socks are berry beautiful. Let the tops peek out from under a pair of riding boots for a look that’s too sweet to resist.

These are 7 pretty printed socks to rock this fall. Which ones do you love? Do you have any of your own faves to add?
