Printed pumps are perfect for adding a bit of extra pizazz to your outfit. This season, ditch the basics and go crazy with prints and patterns - the printed pump is all about making a statement, after all! I’m personally a fan of a good leopard print number, but you can’t beat pretty florals or edgy abstract prints either. Keen to check out some top printed pumps? Read on!

1. Paris Pointed High Heels

Price: $43.12 at us.asos.com.
If you’re a lover of all things pretty and pastel then these printed pumps should be right up your alley. They come in a lovely lilac hue with a subtle floral print. These pumps would look wonderfully ladylike worn with a cream, full-skirted dress.

2. Loeffler Randall Tamsin Pump

Price: $350.00 at zappos.com
Polka dot prints are a favourite of mine right now. They’re also proving to be pretty popular this season as well. These low heeled pumps come in a neutral blush colour with a black polka dot print. They’d be great for wearing during the day and you could even get away with wearing them to work!

3. Emma Cook Orange Floral Printed Pumps

Price: $276.00 at us.asos.com
The floral print on these pumps is much better suited to the fall/winter season. The orange background pops against the dark floral print. Work a casual look by wearing these printed pumps with dark denim jeans and a plain blouse. You could also add an orange bag or jewellery to tie the whole look together.

4. Lucy Ballet Flats

Price: $37.94 at us.asos.com.
Pumps don’t always have to be high heeled. Give your feet a break and slip on these printed ballet pumps instead. They have a cute leopard print design and would look great worn with pastel jeans and a plain tee.

5. Zebra Print Pumps

Price: $26.80 at forever21.com
Animal print is always popular and these zebra print pumps are an affordable option. Not only do these platform pumps come in an eye-catching print, but they’ve also got a textured outsole. These would look great worn with leather-look pants, an oversized tank and blazer for a rock inspired look.

6. Betsey Johnson Ditan Floral Printed Pumps

Price: $70.99 at zappos.com
The floral print on these pumps is quite dreamy! They’d be quite fitting shoes for a garden party, don’t you think? You’d look absolutely ethereal wearing these shoes with a flowing blush dress and delicate jewellery.

7. Tribal Printed Pumps

Price: $26.80 at forever21.com
It’s all about bold geometric prints lately and boy, do these abstract printed pumps deliver on that trend! Team them with some black cigarette pants and a slinky blouse for a cool evening look.

8. Ditsy Abstract Print Pumps

Price: $26.80 at forever21.com
Not ready to branch into colour when it comes to your printed pumps? That’s OK because black and white is a timeless colour combination. These pumps also just happen to feature a quirky abstract print. Wear them with anything from your favourite jeans to party dresses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are pretty printed pumps?
They are stylish women's shoes featuring colorful or patterned designs that add a fun touch to any outfit.
How can I style printed pumps for work?
Pair them with neutral-colored outfits like black trousers or a pencil skirt to let the pumps stand out.
Are printed pumps comfortable for all-day wear?
Comfort depends on the shoe's fit and heel height, so look for cushioned insoles and manageable heels for longer wear.
Can printed pumps be worn in casual settings?
Absolutely! They can dress up jeans or casual dresses for a chic, everyday look.
What materials are printed pumps usually made from?
They come in various materials like leather, fabric, or synthetic blends, often with printed patterns on the surface.
How do I clean my printed pumps without damaging the design?
Use a soft cloth and mild cleaner suitable for the material, avoiding harsh scrubbing to protect the print.
Are printed pumps suitable for formal events?
Yes, especially if the print is subtle or paired with elegant outfits to maintain a polished appearance.
What heel heights are common in printed pumps?
Heel heights vary from flats to high stilettos, so you can choose based on your comfort and occasion.
Can printed pumps match patterned clothing?
It's best to pair printed pumps with solid-colored clothes to avoid clashing patterns.
Where can I buy trendy printed pumps?
Many fashion retailers and online stores offer a wide range of printed pumps—look for reviews to find quality options.
